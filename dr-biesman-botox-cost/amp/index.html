How can I get rid of the redness on my rhinoplasty scar?
Treatment of Red Scars
Apr 8, 2019
Thank you for your question.  Red scars are often highly responsive to laser treatment.  A combination of lasers is sometimes best depending on the thickness of the scar.  Injections with steroids, 5FU and Botox may be helpful as well. I strongly suggest a physician who has extensive expertise in treating scarring.  Best of luck.
