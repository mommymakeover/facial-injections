Bimataprost

Is Sandoz Alcon Bimatoprost Ophthalmic Solution as good as Latisse?
Bimataprost and lash breakage
Apr 14, 2017
Both Alcon Bimataprost and Latisse use same active ingredient bimataprost. If you notice black contamination in the bottle, you should discard the product. As for lash breakage it is unlikely due to the active ingredient so more likely due to other ingredients.
How will I know when Botox for Masseters / Jawline is wearing out?
Testing Masseter Muscle
Apr 14, 2017
Place your fingers over the lower face. Pretend to chew gum with your mouth closed.
If you can feel movement in the masseter muscles when you make the chewing movement, your dosage has worn out.
