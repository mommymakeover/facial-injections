I'm 53. Is my skin too old to benefit from CoolSculpting?
In short...the answer is "no"
May 1, 2020
Hi! No, your skin is not "too old" to benefit from Coolsculpting. However, it's all about what is the most bothersome to you. If it's "contour" or shape, then Coolsculpting can help. If it's texture or the lumpiness, then Coolsculpting might not be the best answer -- in that case, then a texturing device like Exilis might be more beneficial.

But in short, no you don't have skin that's too old. Have a formal consult done by someone you trust and they'll guide you along...
