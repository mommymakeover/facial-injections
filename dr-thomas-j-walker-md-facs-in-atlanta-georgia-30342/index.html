Why canât I smile 6 months post op?
Smile after rhinoplasty
May 30, 2023
By your description, you likely had a caudal septal extension graft placed that can certainly impact smiling. It is indeed unlikely at this point due to the depressor septi muscle. If this is something that bothers you, discuss your options with your surgeon. Nothing went wrong with your surgery and placement of a septal extension graft is critical for nasal tip support and long-term results. A minor revision MIGHT be an option but your surgeon will know best.
