Is it possible to get Botox around the mouth for fine lines? Also to help with down-turned corners? (ph
Combination treatment is better than Botox or filler alone
Apr 7, 2019
Botox can definitely be used to lift the corners of your mouth and also help with fine lines around your lips. However strategically combining Botox in those areas with micro-aliquots of filler would give you a more max result. Good luck!
Would cheekbone enhancement balance my face?
Combination of Botox and Voluma works best
Mar 31, 2019
I agree that there is more volume and fullness on your lower face therefore I would start with enhancing and contouring your mid face and cheekbone area with Voluma. 1 to 2 syringes of this product would give you great balance and structure and still look very natural.  I would also use some Botox in your upper face to balance your eyebrows since your left eyebrow is higher than your right. Good luck!
How long should I wait after having Bell’s palsy to start injecting my face with Botox and fillers?
Wait at least 3 months until symptoms clear
Mar 31, 2019
I agree with the opinion that a neurologist should clear you prior to getting Botox after having Bell’s palsy. I recommend waiting at least 3 months after resolution of your symptoms.  Good luck!
