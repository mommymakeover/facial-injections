28 year old female. Will laser resurfacing remove my crows feet?
Treatment of Crow's Feet
Feb 3, 2021
Hello and thank you for your question. Crow's feet lines result from muscle overactivity so the best treatment for them is Botox. Lines that are present when the muscle is not moving are more difficult to treat and respond best to a combination of treatments including Botox, Laser Resurfacing, and Radiofrequency Microneedling. Make sure you see an experienced physician for a virtual or in-person consultation and examination to determine if these treatments are appropriate for you. Best wishes.
