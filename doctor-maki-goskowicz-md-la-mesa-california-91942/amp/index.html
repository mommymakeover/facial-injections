Is it safe to use botox on my forehead, then again on my crow’s feet a month later?
Botox Visits
Jun 5, 2018
It is perfectly safe to treat one area with Botox and return in a month to treat another area. It is also fine, and likely more convenient, to treat more than one area at the same visit.
Is it safe to have multiple FemTouch treatments and will I continue to see improvement?
Multiple FemTouch treatments
Apr 2, 2018
Yes, it is safe to have multiple FemTouch treatments. FemTouch is a fractionated CO2 laser procedure for vaginal rejuvenation. It is typically recommended to have 3-4 treatments spaced 4-6 weeks apart. You and your doctor may decide that you would benefit from additional treatments.
Can I use Latisse if I have inflammation of the kidneys?
Latisse
Mar 26, 2018
Your use of Latisse should have no effect on the inflammation of your kidneys. That said, it would be prudent to review your health history with your physician and address any questions or concerns before receiving any prescription medications.
How long after putting on Latisse can I use an eyelid cream? How long does it take for Latisse to dr
Eyelid Cream after Latisse
Mar 26, 2018
Latisse, properly applied in very small amounts, should dry quite quickly, within a few minutes. Furthermore, eyelid creams typically are not applied right along the lash line so there should not be any overlap in the application areas. I would suggest waiting a few minutes, then applying the eyelid cream without worries.
Had ResurFX done and am concerned about track marks
Post ResurFX
Mar 26, 2018
It is normal to see the pattern of the laser on your skin for a few days after the procedure. You can expect these to fade within a few days. If you have any concerns, please consult your treating physician. He or she may review your skin care regimen to make sure it is optimal for healing.
What will my skin look like 2 days after the ResurFX treatment?
ResurFX Recovery
Mar 26, 2018
While recovery time will be dependent to some degree on the practitioner and settings used, you can expect some swelling initially, and redness for at least a few days. There should not be any problem applying make up two days afterwards.
Does FemTouch improve discoloration of the external vagina/labia?
FemTouch for external vaginal area
Mar 22, 2018
While FemTouch is well known for rejuvenating the vaginal wall to improve elasticity and lubrication, the CO2 laser rejuvenates skin in general including the skin of the external genitalia. In our office, patients can choose to have both the external and internal areas treated, or only the internal area.
