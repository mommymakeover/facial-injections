How is SmartGraft different from regular FUE?
SmartGraft FUE Transplant
Apr 23, 2021
Thank you for your inquiry! SmartGraft is an FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction) Transplant. SmartGraft uses a small round punch to extract follicular hair units one at a time. The hair is taken from an area of the scalp that is resistant to balding and placed in the areas that are experiencing hair loss. The old method of performing hair transplants is the strip method, which tends to leave patients with a large linear scar on their head. With SmartGraft's FUE Transplant there is no linear scar.
What types of hair loss can SmartGraft treat?
SmartGraft for Hair Loss
Apr 23, 2021
Thank you for your question. SmartGraft can treat all types of hair loss for all hair types. SmartGraft is a minimally invasive FUE hair transplant that leaves no scar. The hair is taken from an area of the head that is resistant to baldness, usually the back and sides of the scalp, and placed in areas that are experiencing hair loss using expert precision. This method allows patients to heal quickly without the linear scar that typically is left by the strip method hair transplant.
