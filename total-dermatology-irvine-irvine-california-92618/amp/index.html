Doctors

Nissan Pilest, MD

Name: Nissan Pilest, MD
Statement: Dr. Nissan Pilest is a leading, board-certified dermatologist with over 42 years of experience in corrective, cosmetic, and surgical dermatology. The founder and medical director of Total Dermatology in Irvine, California, he has earned a reputation for his superior talent and deep understanding of the intricate science and artistry behind his craft.

“My patients trust me with their personal appearance,” says Dr. Pilest, “and it’s my privilege and duty to be sure I’m amply qualified to do the job as perfectly as possible!”

Equipped with the most advanced lasers, radiofrequency energy treatments, light systems, body shaping technologies, injectables, and a full range of chemical peels, Dr. Pilest’s practice offers patients from across Orange County a broad range of medical and cosmetic dermatology treatments, including CoolSculpting, Kybella, ThermiVa, Botox, Juvéderm, Cosmelan, platelet rich plasma (PRP), Fraxel, VBeam, and Thermage.

Dr. Pilest earned his medical degree from the University of Arkansas in Little Rock, Arkansas.

Named to the Circle of Excellence in Soft Tissue Fillers by the American Society of Dermatologic Surgeons, Dr. Pilest is particularly renowned for his skills with injectables and dermal fillers. He is one of the three percent of physicians in the U.S. who qualify for Expert Injector status, an organization endorsed by the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery.

A fellow of the American Society for Laser Medicine and Surgery, Dr. Pilest prides himself on staying on top of the latest technologies and techniques. As he explains, “A growing practice demands a growing doctor.” He holds Advanced Certification for Thermage and is a Fraxel Re:Store/Fraxel Re:Pair CO2 Laser Training Physician for Solta Medical Aesthetics.

In addition to his practice, Dr. Pilest works as an active Associate Clinical Professor of Dermatology at the University of California Irvine and as an in-demand injectable and laser trainer for fellow physicians.

Throughout his career, Dr. Pilest has been recognized for his professional excellence by a number of organizations. He received the Top 100 Doctors Doctors’ Choice Award and was selected by the Orange County Medical Association as a Physician of Excellence.

Dr. Pilest is a diplomat of the American Board of Dermatology and a member of the American Academy of Dermatology (AAD) and the American Society of Dermatologic Surgery (ASDS).
