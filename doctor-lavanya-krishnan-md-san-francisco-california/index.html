What's the best treatment for fine lines?
Fine lines around the eyes
Sep 29, 2014
Hi there,Neurotoxins like Botox and Dysport are likely your best bet for smoothing out the fine lines you are seeing when smiling. They typically take anywhere for 3-7 days to work and the effects last about 3 months. I would also accompany this with a series of laser resurfacing treatments or chemical peels to stimulate collagen production underneath the epidermis. Finally, if you can tolerate a higher percentage, I would increase the tretinoin cream to 0.05% nightly. Hope this helps!
