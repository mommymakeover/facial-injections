Which implant type diminishes the risk of rippling; textured vs smooth, round vs tear-drop?
Rippling
Sep 29, 2019
Silicone gel implants typically do not ripple as much as saline implants, and implants with a higher fill are going to be more firm than those with a lesser fill. This causes them to ripple less. That said, if you are very thin and get large implants placed, you are more likely to have rippling.
