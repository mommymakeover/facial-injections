Fractionated CO2 laser

Thank you for your question. You are an excellent candidate for fractionated CO2 laser to the periocular and periorbital areas to treat the fine lines. This would be my treatment of choice for you. I have lines around my mouth. I’m 28. Is it true that derma fillers won’t get rid of th
smile lines
Jun 4, 2018
The lines that I see in your photo appear to be the smile lines. These are dynamic lines that are a result of the muscles contracting when you smile. These are more difficult to treat, but filler such as Juvederm or Restylane can be used and layered underneath the lines to soften them. You also appear to have pink acne scars. These can be treated with Pulsed Dye Laser to help decrease the red appearance.
What would be the best treatment for my face?
IPL
Jun 4, 2018
Your are a good IPL candidate. The IPL will help with both the brown and pink discoloration. You can ask your provider about topical anesthetic cream to help with the discomfort. We always use a compounded topical anesthetic cream of benzocaine/lidocaine/tetracaine as well as a cooling device called the Zimmer that when used together, make a world of difference for patients when they are getting their treatment.
Is it ok to take a multivitamin and/or iron supplement one week after getting Botox and
Supplements
Jun 4, 2018
Yes this is absolutely fine one week after botox and fillers and will not make the bruising worse. Prior to having botox and fillers done, you should avoid anticoagulants for 1 week such as Aspirin, Ibuprofen, Naproxen, Vitamin E and Fish Oil unless they are medically necessary for pre-existing conditions.

Hiding stitches

I would not recommend putting make up on the wound with stitches two days after the surgery as this could impede the healing or cause an infection at the site. You could find a small bandage that matches your skin color, which should be adequate.
