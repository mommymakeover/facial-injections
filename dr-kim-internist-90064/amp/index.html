Depends on where it is more full
Sep 19, 2017
It would be best to have a consultation to determine what would be the appropriate treatment. If you have a photo that would be helpful as well. Botox can be used if the fullness is towards the angle of the jaw or closer to your ear. Can Botox help uneven eyebrows?
Botox and Thread Lift can help uneven eyebrows
Sep 5, 2017
Yes botox can be done to help correct uneven eyebrows, and to add more arch to one if you prefer. There are various ways to inject botox to do this to varying degress. Another alternative that offers a definite result and sometimes more significant than botox is to lift one eyebrow with a Thread Lift. It is non-surgical and can last one year.
