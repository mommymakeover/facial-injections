Can you use the same syringe on the cheek/lips?
Yes!
Dec 8, 2018
You can definitely use the same syringe for multiple areas of the face. As the above answerer mentioned, each filler is unique and some are specifically designed for lips and others for cheeks, but as long as your injector chooses a product appropriate for both areas you can use the same syringe for both. You also have the option of using half a syringe during your initial visit and saving the other half for when the filler inevitably wears off!

What can I use to get rid of a scar on my upper lip?
Several options
Nov 4, 2018
There are many options available for scar treatment. Yours looks like a simple case and my first recommendation would be laser genesis. We use this treatment in our office for our surgical patients to remove any scars they have post procedure, and I have had great success with older scars as well. Its a painless laser without any downtime but will take several sessions. You could also look into microneedling for an increase in collagen production to help smooth out the scar.
I am currently nursing my 2-month-old baby. Can I get Kybella injections?
I would wait
Nov 4, 2018
It is not known if Kybella passes into the breast milk, for this reason it is recommended that you wait until you are done breast feeding to avoid any potential transfer to your child.

What can help these holes and scars on my face?
Combination treatment
Nov 4, 2018
There are several options when it comes to addressing large pores and acne scars. I would recommend a series of microneedling with RF treatments, with laser genesis treatments between sessions to help promote maximum collagen production. Another option to look at would be a TCA cross which can also be incorporated into a larger treatment plan. I've seen great success with these procedures on your skin type.
