Timing of subcision and filler with Sciton Profractional treatment

If Restylane is being used as a "spacer", I'm not sure that there is much benefit. If Restylane is being used as a filler for atrophic scarring, there is definitely some benefits.
How can I get rid of these red marks on my upper lip and my forehead?
PIH and PIE on darker skin
Nov 9, 2020
I would treat with 1064 laser, either Laser Genesis or Aerolase. It will take a few treatments. You also need to be on retin A and hydroquinone. Also consider chemical peels prior to the laser treatment.
No improvement in worsened dilated pore even after two months, post-TCA Cross! What can I do to fo
Dilated pore didn't respond to one TCA treatment
Nov 9, 2020
Typically, 4 or more treatments are needed for TCA Cross treatments. If you don't respond to treatment, punch excision can be performed.
Could this be ongoing inflammation after Radiesse in my chin, jowls and cheeks?
Prolonged redness after Radiesse in the chin
Nov 3, 2020
If the area is non tender and is not getting progressively worse, it can be treated with a vascular laser. There are a small percentage of patients who have redness related to the Radiesse. 81% experience redness in the first 12 months after injection, with 4% noting to have severe redness per the package insert.
How many Radieese syringes (more or less) are needed for a full forehead augmentation? I just need
Forehead augmentation with Radiesse
Nov 3, 2020
I should take 1-2 syringes of Radiesse for the forehead. It should be done deeply, along the periosteum, with cannulas. This is a high risk area and much be performed by a very experienced injector. Agree that it is better than fat for this area.
How long after subcision and Radiesse can I resume infrared sauna workouts?
When can I start saunas after subcision and Radiesse?
Nov 3, 2020
You can begin saunas about 24 hours after the procedure. The only negative impact is increased bruising and if thats not an issue or concern, you can do it sooner.
How long after Radiesse can you have fat grafting to temples?
How long after Radiesse can fat grafting be performed? Steven F. Weiner, MD
Nov 3, 2020
There should be no interaction of the previously placed Radiesse with fat grafting. Fat grafting is a good choice for temples but has to be done by an experienced surgeon. One other option is Renuva, which is an off the shelf product which is a scaffolding for the body to produce fat. They have done trials in the temple with success.
What filler is best for treating acne scarring?
Filler for acne scars.
Nov 2, 2020
Hyaluronic acid fillers give the best results. I use Refyne in the dermis and add subcision for best results. There is a collagen response to this technique which should give a result between 1-2 years, but everyone is different. I have started to use RHA 2 as well, another HA filler for this too.

Does the ExcelV laser impact existing filler (Juvederm)?
Does the Excel V laser affect existing Juvederm filler?
Nov 1, 2020
There is absolutely no effect on fillers with the Excel V laser. There are several peer reviewed papers that support this. Fillers are sterilized during manufacturing to temperatures much hotter than the Excel V laser for 10-20 minutes.
Had Excel V laser done on my face today to remove brown spots. Now I have raised bumps all over my
Swelling after Excel V laser, is this a burn?
Nov 1, 2020
Welting is possible after Excel V laser. Topical, oral, or IM steroids can be used to reduce the swelling and improve the healing time. It doesn't appear to be a burn which would look like severe redness and possible blistering. Sleeping upright on 2-3 pillows for 2-3 days will reduce the swelling faster as well.
Can you do Excel V laser treatment to face while breastfeeding?
Can Excel V laser be performed while breast feeding?
Nov 1, 2020
There is no problem with undergoing laser procedures when breast feeding. There is no affect on your breast milk. The Excel V laser is an excellent choice for diffuse redness and telangiectasias.
Does Excel V Beam laser effect filler?
Will fillers or Botox be affected by Excel V laser treatments?
Nov 1, 2020
There is absolutely no risk to your fillers or Botox with the Excel V laser. There are several peer reviewed papers supporting this. The HA fillers from Restylane are heated to 120 C for 15 minutes to sterilize them. The heat from the laser is less than 100 C and only milliseconds. Also, Botox is absorbed by the nerve endings within 24 hours so a week is plenty of time to wait after that treatment.
Are Excel Cutera and IPL the same lasers but with different brand?
Is the Excel V laser the same as IPL?
Nov 1, 2020
The Excel V is a laser which has 2 wavelengths, 1064nm and 532nm. A laser is collimated of a single wavelength. IPL is Intense Pulsed Light and consists of broadband of light (multiple wavelengths) which is narrowed to small spectrum using filters. In general, lasers have more energy and are better to target reds and telangiectasias. The only advantage of the IPL might be in diffuse redness such as rosacea but that's debatable. This is not a branding issue, its 2 different technologies....
Is Excel V safe close to the eye for treatment of a broken blood vessel?
Excel V Laser for lower eyelid telangiectasia?
Nov 1, 2020
The Excel V is perfect for this area. Sometime, the lower lid can be pulled below the orbital rim and the laser is safe to use with metal eye shields. If the lid cannot be pulled far enough down, cornea shields must be insert prior to treating this area. Don't worry about the meibomian glands because this only requires one or 2 pulses of the laser.
