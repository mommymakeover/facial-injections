How Long Does Botox Last?
How long does Botox last?
Mar 10, 2021
Botox is a neurotoxin that varies in duration from patient to patient, but average duration is 3-4 months. Because every patient’s muscle activity and receptor production is different, there are individual variations in how long it may last. Dose can affect this as well, with lower doses in larger muscles not lasting as long.
