Can Botox Be Injected Under the Eye?

Nov 23, 2016
Botox cosmetic is an amazing product that is the gold standard in the treatment of wrinkles. FDA approved for the treatment of brow lines and crows feet any off label use of Botox is administered on a highly individualized basis given the patients needs and anatomy. To answer your question, I can say that yes it is certainly possible but I would procede with caution.
