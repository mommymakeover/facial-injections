How long does Vollure last in lips until it’s completely gone?
Juvederm Vollure
Jun 2, 2019
Vollure can last up to 18 months. Give it 2 weeks for the swelling to go down and the filler to settle. If you still don’t like the results, ask your physician if he/she can dissolve the product with hyaluronidase. Always seek treatment from a qualified physician.
28 months since volbella lip injections, 60% lip volume remains?
Juvederm Volbella
Jun 2, 2019
You are lucky it lasted that long. For most patients, it lasts about a year. Always seek treatment from a qualified physician.
Is Voluma or Sculptra better?
Voluma
Jun 2, 2019
It is hard to determine without dong a consultation on you. Voluma is great for mid face volumization. You can see the effects of Voluma immediately. Sculptra is great for cheek volumization as well. Is it normal to still have visible wrinkles after Botox injections between the eyebrows?
Botox
Jun 2, 2019
Yes. Botox works on dynamic lines or the lines that appear when you use your muscles. It doesn’t not act like an eraser and get rid of static lines or the lines you have at rest. Over time, with continued use of Botox, the static lines will improve. Always seek treatment from a qualified physician.

I’m 19 but I have deep forehead lines. Will Botox help me?
Botox
Jun 2, 2019
Yes, Botox can help your forehead lines. Now is the time to treat, before they become permanent lines at rest/ etched into your skin. Always seek treatment from a qualified physician.
Is it normal to still not feel tightening on your forehead 7 days after Botox injections?
Botox
Jun 2, 2019
First of all, it takes 2 weeks for maximal effect of Botox to kick in. Botox is a muscle relaxer. It stops muscle movement so you don’t makes lines on your face. Also, Botox does not tighten your skin. Always seek treatment from a qualified physician.
Juvederm Lips
Juvederm
Mar 2, 2019
Juvederm Ultra, Ultra Plus, Volbella, and Vollure work great in the lips. Juvederm Ultra and Ultra Plus can last 6-12 months. Volbella can last up to a year. Vollure can last up to 18 months. Always seek treatment from a qualified physician.
Juvederm Ultra vs. Ultra Plus
Juvederm
Mar 2, 2019
Juvederm Ultra is a little thinner, less viscous product than the Ultra Plus. The Ultra Plus is a workhorse in the practice because it works well in many areas of the face.
How Long Before I See Juvederm Results?
Juvederm
Mar 2, 2019
You can see the results of Juvederm immediately. Although there will be some swelling, so I always tell patients to wait 2 weeks for the final results.
How Long Does Juvederm Last?
Juvederm
Mar 2, 2019
Juvederm Ultra and Ultra Plus can last 6-12 months. Juvederm Vollure can last up to 18 months. Juvederm Volbella can last up to 1 year. Juvederm Voluma can last up to 2 years.
Is it usual for Juvederm to last a year and half?
Juvederm
Mar 2, 2019
Juvederm can last 6-12 months. I have seen it lat longer and shorter. The length of time it lasts also depends on where on the face it is injected.
