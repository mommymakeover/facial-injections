Can a Juvederm lump on the lip be fixed?
Dissolving lip filler
Mar 4, 2019
Hi Andrea,
It definitely is possible to get a delayed onset nodule in the lip. It can be caused by a multitude of factors such as your body’s immune system, cleanliness and injection technique. It is dis-solvable usually with Hylenex or Vitrace however there are other measures that can be taken to remove it if those first line dissolvers dont work!
Do the juvederm vycross fillers ACTUALLY respond to hyaluronidase?
Dissolving Vycross Juvederm Fillers
Mar 4, 2019
I’ve had experience dissolving both Vollure and Volbella which are part of the Vycross family. They are easily dissolvable if injected correctly however it may take 2-3 sessions to achieve desirable results. Also, in order for the hyaluronidase to take effect, it must be injected at a one-to-one ratio with the Hylauronic Acid Filler. Hope this helps!
Is it safe to thread my eyebrows 5 days after having Botox?
Threading after a Botox treatment
Feb 21, 2019
Yes, it is completely safe to thread your eyebrows 5 days after. Typically it takes up to 4 hours for Botox to activate within the muscle, so threading within that time frame would not be recommended.
