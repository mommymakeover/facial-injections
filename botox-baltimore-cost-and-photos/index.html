For a detailed acquaintance with the procedure, let’s consider what botox is for the face and in cosmetology. The use of botox in cosmetology has received the greatest popularity because of the rapid effect of smoothing the facial wrinkles on the face. Cosmetologists note the main zones on the face where botox is injected:

Muscles around the mouth. Using botox, you can raise the corners of the mouth, align small wrinkles in the corners. The injections of Botox into the hypodermic neck muscles will allow to give the neck a more youthful appearance and prevent the formation of wrinkles. The most common zone on the face where botox is injected is the circular eye muscle. Usually the results of Botox injections around the eyes are placed on the photo of various clinics, because with Botox in these areas of the face the greatest effect is achieved.

The forehead is another popular face area for Botox injections.

As a result, horizontal wrinkles and creases between the eyebrows are smoothed out. The injections of the Botox into the area of the cheekbones help to get the patients clear forms of the face oval. What is the best age for botox injections, everyone determines for himself. Doctors are allowed to do injections from the age of 18. However, it is not wise to start making botox so early, because gradually the action of Botox can reduce its effectiveness. Botox injections at the age of 25 start to do if wrinkles appear under intense sunlight and due to the characteristics of the organism.

Usually this procedure is used, starting from the age of 30 years. On a personal consultation, the cosmetologists of our Clinic will help you decide on the necessity of using Botox. Preparation for Botox injections is simple. Try to rest, the day before the injections of Botox, refrain from drinking alcohol and stop taking antibiotics.

The first visible results from the botox procedure are already visible on day 3. Usually our patients fix the interval to two weeks, when the effect of Botox reaches a maximum and wrinkles are noticeably smoothed. After the end of the action, the botox gradually dissolves and is naturally eliminated from the body.

How often it is possible to do botox injections in each case will be determined by a cosmetologist. After the first Botox injection procedure, it can be repeated no earlier than 6 months later. In the subsequent injections of Botox it is enough to perform once a year, since the mechanism of change in the muscle tissues of the face will start.

Given that the procedure is injectable, many patients wonder: is it painful? It depends on the individual pain. Before the start of the injection site, the drug can be treated with a special analgesic, so that neither the drug nor the injection causes discomfort during the procedure.

Indications for the use of Botox is the appearance of facial wrinkles on the face, neck. In addition, Botox injections are actively used in other areas of the body, such as underarms or palms, for the treatment of hyperhidrosis.

The main contraindications for injections of Botox are: young age, pregnancy and lactation, and blood diseases. It is worth doing whether injections of Botox, each determines for himself. Since the appearance of this cosmetic procedure, many supporters and many Botox opponents have appeared. In one patient, Botox is ideally suited and customers consider botulinum toxin as the best anti-aging agent. Others are frightened not by the professional use of botox. In any case, the choice is yours.
