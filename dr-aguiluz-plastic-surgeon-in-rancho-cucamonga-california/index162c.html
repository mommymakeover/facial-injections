How to safely use Radiesse on top of a medpor chin implant?
Radiesse over an implant
Jan 16, 2020
Great question. Yes you can use Radiesse to add volume to your chin. This said, I recommend seeing a plastic surgeon or facial plastic surgeon familiar with chin implants to minimize risk of infection. There is always the risk than the implant could be seeded (get infected) after filler injection
Does Belotero need to be refrigerated after use?
Refrigerating Belotero
Jan 16, 2020
Thank you for your question .
Belotero does not need to be refrigerated. This said:
1- wait at least 2 weeks prior to getting more filler
2- I would advice against storing partially used syringes. Once the product has been used, it is no longer sterile. Re-using a partially used syringe is something you should discuss with your provider/injector .
Bruising months after Belotero. Could this be due to filler?
Bruising months after Belotero
Jan 16, 2020
Thank you for your question. Based on your description, it sounds like tyndall effect from the filler . If this is the case, the way to fix it is by dissolving the filler with hyaluronidase
Broken blood vessels or necrosis from Belotero?
Broken blood vessel
Jan 16, 2020

What has caused these huge ‘bags’ under my eyes after Belotero injections and dissolving?
Belotero under eye injection
Jan 16, 2020
Great question and thank you for sharing your fotos .
2 things
1- In general the undereye area always swells up after treatment and its best to wait 2 weeks to let things settle to see the result if it still remains after 2 weeks – it usually means you had a little to much filler placed
2- hyaluronidase causes swelling .
Its best to give it time after the hyaluronidase for the swelling to subside … it may all go away or you may need more…
Will Emsculpt be safe if I have dental implants?
Emsculpt and dental implants
Jan 12, 2020
Good question. Yes. It is generally safe. The areas were you put the Emsculpt paddles are far away from the dental implants. They wont heat up during treatment. Hope this helps.
Could emsculpt fix fibrosis and/or uneven lipo?
Can Emsculpt fix fibrosis or uneven lipo?
Jan 12, 2020
Good question. Unfortunately, no. Emsculpt only helps tone your abdominal muscles. Uneven lipo can only be fixed with revisional liposuction and this is a tough problem to surgically fix. VASER liposuction helps in breaking fibrosis and I find it helpful in revisional cases. Hope this helps
