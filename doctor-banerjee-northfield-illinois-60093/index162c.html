Why aren't I healing better after Day 6 of Halo?
Post Halo skin care
Apr 10, 2019
It takes skin anywhere from day 2-10 to recover or in some cases longer depending on protocol used/skins condition. Milia and bumps are normal as the laser sometimes dry out the skin, consider using a water in oil based product such as HA serum to hydrate and maybe an oil free moisterizer. Ask your provider to review your post skin care needs as they would know best.

Will I be able to wear glasses after getting the Halo Laser?
Post laser care.
Apr 10, 2019
Wearing your eye glasses shouldnt be a problem, but try to avoid having them on for long periods of time to avoid indentations since skin is fresh. As far as antihistamines every office has their own protocal to pre and post care. Be sure to ask your provider.

Halo and Accutane

Is this permanent hyperpigmentation from Halo and BBL? Can I get rid of it, and how?
permanent hyperpigmentation from Halo and BBL
Apr 10, 2019
Do not judge results until 6 weeks post your service as your skin is still healing. As far as the discoloration goes try using a topical lightening agent as well as increasing your spf (50 is recommended). As far as the acne concern, acne is very common post laser as laser sucks out sun damage and dirty water in our cells. You may need to contavct your provider to review your post skin care. Try using HA serum to hydrate and protect and an oil free moisterizer. Good luck. 
What is the best plant-based skincare product to use after microneedling?
Products post micro needling
Apr 10, 2019
Skinsueticals has a wonderful Phyto masque that hydrates the skin and adds cooling post micro needling.. also helps with inflammation and redness. Or you can use a growth factor serum. its anti aging properties and stem cell stimulate healthy cells, rejuvination, and healing. 
Can i use glycolic acid cream 10% after 0.25mm microneedling session?
What to use after micro needling
Apr 10, 2019
I would not. As Glycolic acid is pretty strong it will go into the micro channels and potentially burn and thin out the skin moire. At this moment you should be using HA serum before during and after. I would omit using acids after micro needling for at least 2 weeks as it could really dry the skin out.

Day 10 and still not looking good after the halo?
Post Halo.
Apr 10, 2019
If you have already spoken with your provider take their advice. From the picture looks as though you are still processing. Remember laser draws out years if damage, the more you have, the more will come to surface, prolonging recovery.
