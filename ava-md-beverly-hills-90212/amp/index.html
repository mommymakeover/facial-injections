Antibiotics after Dermal Filler and Botox
Apr 3, 2019
There are no contradictions between botox, fillers and antibiotics like augmentin and there should be no interaction that would hinder your results. I suggest speaking with your physician if you are still concerned. Warmest regards, Dr. Shamban.
Does Botox Work on Horizontal Forehead Creases?
Botox is a common treatment for horizontal forehead creases.
Aug 12, 2016
Yes, the horizontal forehead creases are a common site to treat with Botox. For deeper forehead lines, consider Botox in combination with dermal fillers, laser resurfacing, and skin care with retinol
Can Too Much Botox Cause Eyebrows to Drop?
Yes, too much Botox can make eyelids appear heavy.
Aug 12, 2016
Yes, injecting too much Botox in the forehead can make the eyebrows and upper eyelids appear heavy. The idea is not to be frozen, the goal is to soften your expression lines. Less is more!
When Will I Notice the Results of the Botox?
Full results show within 2 weeks.
Aug 12, 2016
You may notice the results of Botox within 3-4 days; however, the full results can be seen in 2 weeks. Try to resist the urge to add more Botox until you wait out the first 2 weeks.
What Are the Post Treatment Instructions After Botox?
I suggest not lying flat for 4 hours after injections.
Aug 12, 2016
I always educate patients to not lie flat for 4 hours and to avoid heavy exercise, facials, or massage for 24 hours. For all you Yogi's: remember, heavy exercise includes all inversions-head stands and shoulder stands!
