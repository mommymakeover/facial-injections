What procedure can lift the ends of the eyebrows without committing to a full brow lift?
Brow lift
Sep 16, 2019
Thank you for your question. You do have options besides a full brow lift. The least invasive option is with botox or another neurotoxin injected around the eye. This works to lift the tail of the eyebrow and some of the upper eyelid. This change is subtle but can be sufficient in some people. The other option is to perform a temporal brow lift which is done surgically. This doesn’t lift the rest of the eyebrow or the forehead.

Should I cut my face to remove end of pixie ear, please?
Pixie Ear
Sep 8, 2019
Thank you for your question. As you mentioned, the cause of the pixie ear is from tension on the ear lobe, pulling it down. The best way to repair a pixie ear deformity is to re-suspend the tissue underneath the skin (SMAS) and excise the scar from the previous surgery. This will ensure that there is no tension on the new scar. Depending on how much skin laxity there is, this may be done conservatively or you may require a revision of the facelift. Best of luck to you!
