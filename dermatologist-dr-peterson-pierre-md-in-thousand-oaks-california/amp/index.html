Reducing Juvederm Swelling with Cold Compress?
Lip swelling
Feb 1, 2018
The lips are very sensitive and will typically swell with lip injections. I would recommend 3-5 minutes at a time after every meal. Ibuprofen with every meal will also help. Your lips should be back to normal within 1-2 days.

How long does it take to see the results from a Vanquish treatment?
Vanquish Results
Jan 4, 2018
Vanquish destroys the fat cells and release the fat into the bloodstream. It takes 1-2 weeks for your body to flush it out (good hydration is very important) and another few weeks to see final results. After your series of 4-6 treatments, results should be visible 1-2 months later. To maximize results, you’ll want to combine your Vanquish treatments with Exilis Ultra which can be performed at the same time.
