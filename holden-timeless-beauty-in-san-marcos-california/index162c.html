Can EmSculpt Neo cause burns?
Emsculpt Neo
Nov 2, 2021
Hi, Emsculpt Neo can cause burns if the frequency is too high. In our office, If rating 1-10 you reach a 7 in discomfort you should let us know so we can turn the heat down to avoid any potential burn. If you do get a burn you may want to put Aquaphor on it to keep it moist and allow time for healing.
