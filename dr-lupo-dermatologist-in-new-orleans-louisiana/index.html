Is tretinoin proven to be more effective to reduce acne scarring than adapalene?
What a great question!
Jan 1, 2018
The most accurate answer is there have been no comparitive studies. Both tretinoin (Retin A) and tazarotene (Avage) have been FDA approved for photoaging (and collagen improvement). Adapalene has never been studied by Galderma for this indication, but might help. In my clinical experience, adapalene is not as good for aging/scars/collagen building.
Should I keep taking Doxycycline if I don’t have acne anymore?
Do NOT take antibiotics more than necessary
Jan 1, 2018
Even in more severe acne, it is not a good idea to stay on antibiotics for more than a few months. After that, topical medications should be able to maintain the clearance. If not, Accutane, hormonal therapy, or treatments like laser to suppress the over-active sebaceous glands should be considered. Discuss all your options with a board-certified dermatologist.
Is this normal after Botox?
Bruising is the most common complication of Botox.
Jan 1, 2018
Even with a tiny needle, a blood vessel can be pierced.  Bruising is much more common if you are on aspirin, Vitamin E, and many herbal remedies. The bruise is immediately blue, and then turns brownish-yellow as the hemosiderin is metabolized. There is no reason for concern.

Is this a burn from my recent chemical peel?
This is a complication. See your dermatologist ASAP
Dec 26, 2017
You have darker skin, so anything other than a very light peel can be problematic. If an erosion like this, so early on (it is best for the skin to flake, not slough off) occurs, it could put you at risk for post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation. When identified and addressed early, it will be a non-issue. But if treatment is delayed, the dark areas at the site of the erosion can last months-years. Your dermatologist can easily fix this, so get ahead of the problem.
