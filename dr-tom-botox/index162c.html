Lip plumping treatments that aren’t filler?
Lip augmentation techniques
Dec 8, 2020
Lip augmentation:
Botox or other neuromodulator for lip flip
Lip filler
Lip implant (silicone)
Fat grafting (injection vs. strip of fat with dermis attached)
Lip lift (surgery)

Any implant (silicone; filler) has a higher risk of infection than utilizing your own tissue but overall the risk of infections is exceedingly small if proper sterile technique is employed.
