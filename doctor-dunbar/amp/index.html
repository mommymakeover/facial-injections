Treatment for Acne Scars
Mar 4, 2019

If SculpSure doesn’t work, what are my other options?
Skin tightening and smoothing
Dec 7, 2018
There are several devices that can tighten skin. I like Exilis Ultra 360 because it combines radiofrequency (RF) and ultrasound to heat the tissues and simulate production of collagen and elastin.
