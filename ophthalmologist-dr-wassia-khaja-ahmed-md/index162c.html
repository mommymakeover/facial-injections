What are the risks and benefits of medial epicanthoplasty?
Risks
Jan 4, 2021
Epicanthoplasty definitely can open up your eyes more. It is most commonly done in the Asian population so make sure you seek a provider that has lots of experience with Asian patients – I would recommend an oculoplastic surgeon. It is a relatively simply procedure that can be completed in 15-20 minutes. There will be bruising and swelling for up to a week and sutures are removed at the one week mark. The main risk with epicanthoplasty is scarring and webbing of the inner eye fold.

Is lip filler less painful the longer you leave the numbing cream on?
Filler pain
Dec 23, 2020
Numbing cream can decrease the pain with filler injection as it numbs the skin and you do not feel the puncture of the needle. Depending on the type of numbing cream, it needs to sit on the skin for 15-30 minutes. Leaving it on longer than that does not help because the numbing cream only absorbs into the top most layers of the skin.
Most fillers have lidocaine mixed into them, the area will feel numb as the filler injections are going in. Good luck!
