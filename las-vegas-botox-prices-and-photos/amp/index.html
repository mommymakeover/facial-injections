Las Vegas Botox Prices And Photos

Botox (the name of the analogue of Dysport) is a specially treated neurotoxin of botulinum A. It blocks the transmission of the nerve signal to the muscle and thus prevents its contraction. It is about smoothing the facial wrinkles by removing the muscles and facial skin from the constantly repetitive load. As a result, the skin is actively restored and wrinkles go away.

Unfortunately, it is impossible to get rid of any wrinkles with the help of Botox injections in Las Vegas. It is only about the mimic wrinkles around the eyes, in the forehead and above the upper lip. Botox is not correct nasolabial folds or wrinkles on the cheeks. Because, first, they have a different mechanism of formation, and in – the second, we can not stop there. So, we have to open our mouth and block the work of muscles in this area is impossible.

What are the possible troubles?

Let’s start with the fact that if a good and experienced professionals makes the injections, the likelihood of unpleasant consequences is reduced to zero. And do not worry, Botox is not toxic.

The main danger is overdose. As a result, you can get the omission of the eyelid due to too strong a weakening of the muscles. The second danger is small hematomas. If you have weakened vessels, tell the doctor about it.

Of course, there is a theoretical possibility that the injection will do “wrong”. But this is unlikely in a clinic with good specialists. In any case, the dosage and place of administration of the drug is determined by a specialist after personal consultation.

BOTOX Is A Prescription Medicine Of Purified Protein

Attention! Even if you are a long-time nurse, do not try to inject yourself! There is a big risk of making mistakes with the choice of the place of injection and the result can be the most deplorable!

Botox is contraindicated for:

  • Pregnant and lactating women,
  • people suffering from myasthenia gravis (pathological, fast fatigue of muscles),
  • if there is inflammation on the skin in the treated area,
  • when treated with antibiotics and strong tranquilizers.

Do not recommend injecting Botox to 30 years of age.

How long does Botox work?

The result of the injection is observed after one or two weeks. The duration of the action is from 4 to 9 months. Optimal to make repeated injections in half a year.

Once or twice the effect will be more and more prolonged. The skin gets less and less facial loads, both by blocking the nerve signal, and by the fact that you are weaning from the familiar facial expressions.

What not to do after the Botox injections?

  • For 48 hours you can not go in for sports and homework, which requires the inclination of the head.
  • In the first two weeks, refrain from the sauna, hot bath and face washing with hot water.
  • After injections, for the entire duration of its action, gymnastics for the face and face massage in the injection zone are prohibited.
  • Completely eliminated the use of scrubs. Cosmetic funds on the eyes and forehead should be applied with light, stroking movements, rather than patting.

What is the difference between Botox and Disport?

These are products from different companies and Botox is a more concentrated product, which means that one of its ampoules costs more than a dispenser of Disport.
