Allergy to Botox
Oct 20, 2020
Thanks for your question.
Allergies to Botox / Dysport and the like are very rare, and while these are both used to treat migraines, they can also trigger temporary headaches / migraines due to muscle spasm from the needle (not the product) and / or the preservatives in the product. Migraines can and frequently manifest as dizziness. I do not know of any test to tell you whether you are “allergic” to the toxin because the product in and of itself, a toxin.
Best of luck!

Retin-A and AHA
Oct 18, 2020
Retinoic Acid is like an exfoliant in that it turns your skin cells over so it is a fantastic medication for antiaging. However, it will make your skin more sensitive to acids and the sun.
Wait until you are acclimated to the medication – no peeling, major redness, or “anticipated reaction” before using acid washes or peels.
