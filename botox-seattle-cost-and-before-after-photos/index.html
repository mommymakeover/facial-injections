Youth, unfortunately, is a temporary phenomenon. No wonder the kings of cosmetology invent new methods for its prolongation. Botox is one of them.

Botox: mechanism of action

Botox is called purified protein. It is injected by injection, with special thin needles, at certain points in the musculature of the face.

In general, the procedure is painless, but in some cases involves local anesthesia.

For many people who want to get Botox injections, the price does not matter much – so important is the result.

The duration of the procedure varies depending on the number of points in which the drug is administered.

The result becomes noticeable after a couple of days, the maximum expression reaches a week later. The duration of the effect depends on the area of exposure and the characteristics of the organism.

Affordable Botox Seattle

Botox injections are recommended for those with:

  • deep wrinkles on the forehead;
  • wrinkles around the lips;
  • age atrophy of the skin of the face / neck;
  • wrinkles between the eyebrows;
  • wrinkles near the outer corners of the eyes;
  • asymmetrical outline of the face or the shape of the lips;
  • thin lips.

Botox is effective for those who want to get rid of increased sweating: in the areas of feet, armpits, palms.

Contraindications for the administration of the drug are:

  • pregnancy / lactation;
  • inflammatory processes;
  • relapses of somatic diseases;
  • neuromuscular disorders;
  • tendency to scarring;
  • hemophilia;
  • damage to the skin on the face;
  • taking certain medications;
  • violations of the endocrine system.

In rare cases, the effect of Botox is unpredictable: in some patients (5% of known cases), small hematomas can form at the sites of administration, and 2% have headaches during the first day.

Botox Is Ideal For Treatment Of Dynamic Wrinkles Of The Face

On a gentle skin, injections can briefly leave swelling and swelling. Very rarely after the procedure, eyebrow / eyelid can be observed, but this effect disappears within a few days.

Botox is known to harm? Experts’ opinions about this are very different, but the overwhelming majority testifies to positive clinical results.

Doses, recommendations for injections

In accordance with age, patients are conditionally divided into 3 groups:

  • up to 30 years old;
  • 30-50 years old;
  • over 50 years old.

For patients from the first age category, it is recommended to use botox to fill the appeared mimic wrinkles. In patients under 50 years of age, cosmetologists increase the elasticity of the skin through the use of drugs-fillers.

People over 50 are given injections with a combined composition: botox + drug-filers. In addition, a number of cosmetic procedures (peeling, laser resurfacing, etc.) are recommended.

Botox Is Used To Relax Facial Muscles

In what quantities is botox introduced? It depends on the sex and skin type of the patient. So, the depth and expressiveness of mimic wrinkles in men is much larger than that of the female half, so for them this substance will be required more.

Patients with thin, dry skin, due to insufficient moisture, are given a minimal dose. Patients with a fatty skin type, on the contrary, are recommended a larger amount of the drug.

How to behave after the procedure?

After the introduction of the drug is recommended in the full sense of the word to grind for its uniform distribution under the skin. During the first 24 hours it is recommended not to lower your head and avoid household chores related to this: cleaning, ironing, cooking.

After the procedure, it is not recommended to take a horizontal position for a long time (about 4 hours), you should refrain from drinking alcohol, visiting the solarium, sauna, sun exposure, physiotherapy procedures, facial massage.

Summing up

Injections using botox are not in vain called beauty injections. The introduction of this drug ensures smoothness and evenness of the skin, increasing its elasticity. Botox is an effective way to combat increased sweating. Improving appearance, these injections raise self-esteem and inspire self-confidence.
