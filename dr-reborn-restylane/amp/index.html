How can I get rid of these indentations near my nose?
Fillers for nasolabial folds
Aug 24, 2019
Hello and thank you for your question.  Those indentations, called nasolabial folds, can easily be treated with fillers.  Fillers such as Juvederm, Restylane, or Radiesse can be injected into those folds, giving more volume to the indentation which creates a smoother appearance.  Be sure to seek an experienced injector who specializes in facial aesthetics for best results.  Good luck!

Possible to eliminate weird shadow formed by under-lip fat pockets?
Fillers for chin smoothing
May 31, 2019
Hello and thank you for your question.  With the help of fillers you can minimize the shadow cast by these protruding pockets just under your lower lip.  Fillers such as Juvederm, Restylane, or Radiesse can be injected just under these pockets and in the mental crease of your chin to create a smoother transition from your lower lip to your chin.  Be sure to seek an experienced injector who specializes in facial aesthetics for best results. Good luck!
