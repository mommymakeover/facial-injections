I got smile line injections 1 wk ago. I still have bruising and lumps on the inside of my mouth where
Post Injection Concerns
Aug 15, 2018
This is a normal concern after having fillers injected. The lumps will start to settle down 2 weeks after you were injected, but if it lasts longer then that I would make an appointment with the provider who performed the injections, they can massage the area and smooth out the product. As far as the bruising, give your body a little more time to heal.
Kybella for bra fat. What are the risks?
Kybella for bra fat
Jul 30, 2017
While Kybella is only FDA approved for under the chin, it is often used "off label" to treat the bra fat and is very effective. It will probably take more than one session so keep that in mind. The risks are minimal but you can expect to have some bruising and swelling following your treatment. See a really experienced injector to be assessed in person and discuss. 
Continue Kybella or seek other options for chin?
Does it make sense to continue Kybella?
Jul 30, 2017
First of all, are you treating the sleep apnea because this could be contributing to your issue. If not, start there. Next, yes, you could continue with the Kybella but it's expensive and the results, though good, are not as extreme as what you would get from liposuction. So, if you aren't adverse to surgery, you may want to consider that.

Longevity of Volbella
Jul 30, 2017
In general, Volbella results last about a year. Longer on some patients and longer in certain areas. Filler under the eyes tends to last awhile but 2-4 years seems like a stretch. However, it's such a new filler.... who knows.
