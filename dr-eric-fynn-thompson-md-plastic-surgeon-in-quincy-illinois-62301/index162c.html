Tummy tuck or not?
Dec 3, 2018
I think that you have a component of loose skin what will likely not completely shrink with weight loss and time. Loose skin that has a slight overhang can only be corrected with a tummy tuck abdominoplasty procedure. That will give you the best results, help correct any muscle separation, and can be combined with some liposuction to improve contour. Once you have reached your ideal body weight, seek a consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon to discuss options. Good luck.
