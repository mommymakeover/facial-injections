Is it safe to get lip fillers prior to pregnancy?
Filler before pregnancy is safe
Nov 26, 2019
Right before is fine just be very sure that it is in fact before and not during early pregnancy. There is not any data on receiving filler during pregnancy so with all things related to pregnancy if we do not have data on it we advise against it.

Address lip asymmetry with HA fillers

What are the options for lip augmentation when my body metabolizes HA fillers too quickly?

It is possible that the latest highly cross-linked HA fillers could last long enough to be worth your while. If you haven't you might try Vollure in the Juvaderm family.

What would the best non-surgical procedure to decrease my gummy smile and add fullness to my upper
How to improve gummy smile
Nov 12, 2019
I would recommend upper lip augmentation to address both the gummy smile and thin upper lip. One syringe should be sufficient to be effective. I would avoid neurotoxins such as botox, in this case. What is the best way to fix my thin & misshapen scarred lips? (Phtotos)
I would suggest trying fillers
Nov 7, 2019
Lets give it a try! Fillers could be a good solution. Scar tissue has a way of making lip augmentation a bit less predictable. Sometimes the scar acts as an internal baffle however you don't know until you try. Should it be an unacceptable result you can melt the filler using Hyaluronidase. If you get a good result it will last six months to a year and could address the asymmetry and the smile.
Is it possible to get chin Botox without an odd smile as a result?
Inject low to avoid asymmetry and affect on the smile
Oct 30, 2019
Yes it is possible. My trick is to stay very low and not to inject more than a finger breadth above the lower border of the chin. If you inject above this you can get weakening of the muscles that position the lips which results in asymmetry.

Sequence of fillers and non invasive therapies
Oct 18, 2019
