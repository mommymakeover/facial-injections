What is the best injectable filler for undereye hollows?
Under eye filler
Feb 15, 2020
My preferred filler for the area around the eye is restylane L. This can be used to treat the under and sometimes the upper eye hollow. In expert hands, it is safe and effective. I almost always use blunt cannulas to minimize the chance of intravascular injection. This should only be done by a board certified, experienced injector.
I have bruised from Restylane before. Will Juvederm cause the same bruising?
Bruising with filler injection
Feb 15, 2020
Thanks for your question! Bruising can happen with any filler injection, regardless of brand. There are several things the patient can do to prepare for injection. Stopping all “blood thinning” medications and supplements 1-2 weeks prior to your appointment and avoiding alcohol 1-2 days prior may help. Icing the area immediately prior to injection and using blunt cannulas along with good technique can also decrease the chance of bruising.
