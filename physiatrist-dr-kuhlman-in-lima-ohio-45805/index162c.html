At these very high doses I have seen only 2 cases of Botox resistance in the past 28 years. You can absolutely have your crows done now and the odds of Botox resistance is about one in a million. if your injector believes that you can not, then they probably do not know much about Botox.

Help! Entire lower face has changed shape (wide/sagging jawline), what can I do?
jowls
Apr 16, 2023
Your cheeks are starting to atrophy and you are developing jowls. This is a common sign of aging where you go from a heart shaped to a rectangular shaped face. Consider dermal filler injections to your lateral cheeks to help pull up the lower face and widen your mid face. Then consider Kybella injections to your marionettes and jowls to narrow your lower face. Botox to your masseter muscles can also narrow the lower face. You need a very skilled injector to do this.
