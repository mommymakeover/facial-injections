How soon for PRP should I stop Tylenol and ibuprofen? Had a small procedure on September 2
Pain meds and PRP
Sep 23, 2018
There is no reason to stop the Tylenol but you should hold anything that can interfere with platelet function. These include ibuprofen, aleve, toradol etc for 48 hours before and 48 to 72 hours after. I also tell my clients to hold the omega/fish oils.

Can you have noticeable results from Microneedling with PRP after only one treatment?
Yes
Sep 12, 2018
This depends on what you are treating and the depth of the needle used. Shallow needles penetrate less and may be less effective. I use a 2 mm to 3 mm needle depending on skin texture and quality..
