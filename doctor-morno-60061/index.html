Can Botox fix sagging skin on buttocks, legs and arms?
Botox for softening facial lines
May 6, 2022
Botox is FDA approved to relax facial muscles and in turn, soften the lines created from moving the face. Unfortunately it will not tighten skin on the body, but I recommend looking into nonsurgical skin tightening treatments for the body! You may be interested in CoolSculpting or JPlasma.
How much Juvederm would be needed to correct these nasolabial folds?
Filler for Nasolabial Folds
Dec 2, 2021
I recommend seeing an experienced injector in person. Every face is different and needs to be fully assessed before deciding on a specific amount of filler. It may be a better option to use a sturdier filler in your cheeks for lift, rather than fill the nasolabial folds. Find an injector you trust and listen to their expertise after assessment!
Should I go for another Hyaluronidase session and should I get third opinion?
Hyaluronidase to dissolve lip filler
Nov 15, 2021
To have all of the filler dissolved, it will most likely take a few sessions. It is possible to dissolve it all. I would recommend researching experienced injectors in your area and scheduling a consultation to discuss. If the original medspa is not meeting your expectations and led to lumpy filler, I would recommend finding a new place with a well-trained injector.
Botox for asymmetrical smile?
Botox for Smile
Oct 26, 2021
Great question! Botox would be a great option for you to even out the muscles pulling higher on the left when you smile. You'd also be a great candidate for a "lip flip" which is when we inject a few units of Botox above the top lip to relax the smile.
Can you use Botox to change the shape of my smile?
Botox for Smile
Sep 8, 2021
Botox can absolutely be a great option for relaxing your top lip. Botox cannot necessarily change your smile, but it can relax the muscles that pull, in turn lower the top lip when you smile. I've seen great success with about 4 units of botox in the top lip.
Botox, gum contouring or both for gummy smile?
Botox for gummy smile
Sep 8, 2021
Great question! I would recommend a few units of Botox to the upper lip to relax the muscles that pull up and show the gum. I've seen great success with just a few units to relax gummy smiles.
Lumpy filler ruined my face. Advice?
Lip Filler
May 14, 2021
I would recommend finding a new injector that you trust, and having them remove any remaining filler. Once it is confirmed any remaining filler has been removed, they can properly add new filler to even it out. I would recommend Juvederm Volbella.

Acne Scars
Apr 6, 2021
Great question! Acne scarring is a common concern for patients. There are many options such as microneedling, BBL facials, and topical products that can help resurface the skin and even out your tone. I would recommend scheduling a consultation to discuss your options.

Liquid Rhinoplasty
Apr 5, 2021
Great question, many patients feel that filler to their nose could make it appear enlarged, but when injected properly, it will only even out the tip. I would not expect a small amount of filler to make your nose look larger.
How can I make my top lip symmetrical and also scar less noticeable?
Filler for lips
Apr 5, 2021
Dermal fillers would be a great option for your lip. Placing a small amount of Juvederm Ultra or Ultra Plus in specific areas could help even out the lip and create a symmetrical appearance for you.
I am interested in removing belly and stomach fat. Will CoolSculpting work for me?
Coolsculpting for fat reduction
Apr 5, 2021
I believe Coolsculpting would be a great option for you, since you are already slim. With Coolsculpting, you can see up to 25% fat reduction in the areas treated. We always recommend multiple sessions. You will not see results immediately, as it takes up to 6 months for the fat cells to remove themselves from your body.
What kind of filler can I use in this area of my cheek?
Juvederm Voluma
Apr 5, 2021
Great question. Many patients search for a sculpted jawline to enhance the overall shape of their face. Juvederm Voluma does a great job at this, because it will not fill in the "sunken in" part, but instead. sculpt the jawline. You could also look into Sculptra, which is an injectable that stimulates collagen production.

Lip Filler
Mar 22, 2021
An experienced injector will make sure you achieve a natural look with lip filler. I would recommend starting off with Juvederm Ultra to even out and add volume to your lips.

It appears you would be a great candidate for lip filler. Lips naturally thin over time, so a hyaluronic acid dermal filler does a great job to restore volume and hydration.
