Lump 1 year post filler. Is this normal or should I be concerned?
What to do about a chronic lump from a filler injection?
Nov 5, 2023
A lump that develops a few weeks after a filler injection could be several things - a migration of the filler, a reaction to the filler, a delayed infection, or a seroma (fluid collection), or a gynecologic issue that is simply coincidental. I would recommend that you visit your injector and/or gynecologist to make an assessment.

Why is there risk of nerve damage to the clitoris with a labiaplasty?
All surgeries have risks
Oct 3, 2023
All surgeries have risks. The only way to damage the dorsal nerves (there are 2 of them, not one) is to cut them or burn them. In theory, every surgeon who does a labiaplasty should know where these nerves are and use that knowledge to avoid cutting them or burning them. In reality, some surgeons don't know where these nerves are, don't know that they even exist, and that's why there is still a risk.

Labiaplasty first or laser vaginal rejuvenation?
Labiaplasty before ultra femme is best
Sep 8, 2023
Tissue that has been cooked recently with laser doesn't heal as well from labiaplasty as tissue that hasn't been recently cooked. For that reason, it would make sense to defer any laser therapy in that area until healing is complete. At least 6 weeks. Preferably 12 weeks. However, UltraFemme is not a laser treatment. It is a radiofrequency treatment and doesn't generate as much heat as a laser, but the same recommendation would still apply.
