Can't book next microneedle appt for 8 weeks?
Microneedling
Nov 16, 2023
Hi, thank you for your question! I recommend for microneedling sessions to be repeated every 6-8 weeks. If you are seen at the 8 week mark it will be just as effective. Yes, I always recommend for patients to between 2-4 sessions for a patient that is most concerned with textural issues. If you have scaring that is deeper I would recommend 6-8 sessions. How long should I wait to use tretinoin after microneedling?
Microneedling
Nov 16, 2023
Hi, thank you for your question! I would recommend stopping your tretinoin a week to two weeks before having microneedling done. I would also recommend waiting the same amount of time post microneedling.

Is this much swelling common for Mira Dry?
Miradry
Nov 9, 2023
Hi, thank you for your question! Yes, unfortunately there can be great deal of swelling after a Miradry treatment. The swelling is due to the injectable numbing but should subside within a few days.If the swelling does not subside within the week I would recommend following up with your provider.
Can you perform Miradry without anesthesia?
Miradry
Nov 9, 2023
Hi, thank you for your question! I would not recommend having Miradry performed without an anesthetic because it is a painful procedure. I would recommend visiting an allergist to see which anesthetic can be used.
Does some Botox remain in the body?
Botox
Nov 3, 2023
Hi, thank you for your question! Botox is not a forever chemical. Botox is not systemic, it stays in the localized area that it is injected into. Overtime it is metabolized and excreted from the body.
What is the brand you use for Botox?
Botox
Nov 3, 2023
Hi, thank you for your question! I use Botox manufactured by Allergan in the office. I would recommend having an in person consultation with a certified injector!
I have a muscle & fat pad bulging next to my nose. Will Botox help?
Botox
Nov 3, 2023
Hi, thank you for your question! I would not recommend botox to help with this area. Due to weight gain this could be a combination of multiple things. I would recommend following up with a certified injector for an in person consultation.
Would using a vibrating massage tool affect the longevity of Botox?
Botox
Nov 3, 2023
Hi, that is a great question! A vibrating massage tool can affect the longevity of botox because it is making the muscle contract. When you are having the muscle contract over and over again it is causing the botox to wear off faster because it is being metabolized at a faster rate.
Can Botox and Dysport be done at the same session but in different areas?
Botox and Dysport
Nov 3, 2023
Hi, thank you for your question! Yes, botox and dysport can be performed on the same day in different areas.
Is it ok to do RF treatment a day after microneedling?
RF after microneedling
Oct 27, 2023
Hi, thank you for your question! I would recommend waiting 4 weeks after microneedling to receive RF treatment. Morpheus combines the two treatments in the same day.
How long after thread lift can I get microneedling?
Microneedling after a threadlift
Oct 27, 2023
Hi, thank you for your question! I would recommend waiting 4 weeks post threadlift to have microneedling preformed. I would only recommend waiting this amount of time so that the threads have time to settle and also the filler.
Planning to do PRP microneedling on the face 1 month after having a tattoo in another area of the bo
Microneedling after a tattoo
Oct 27, 2023
Hi, thank you for your question! I do not know of any contradictions of receiving prp with microneedling after receiving a tattoo on a different area of the body. As long as the tattoo is not in the same area as the microneedling this is safe to do.
Safe to Get Lipodissolve While Pregnant?
Lipo dissolve while pregnant
Oct 27, 2023
Hi, thank you for your question! I do not recommend receiving lipo dissolve while pregnant. There is not enough research done to show that it is safe. Any injections during pregnancy may have a risk of affecting the child. I would recommend waiting until you are not pregnant or breastfeeding.
Do you have to fast before your blood draw for PRP injections?
Blood draw for PRP injections
Oct 25, 2023
Hi, thank you for your question! Whether you fast or not it will not affect the outcome of the procedure. I would recommend eating before so that you do not become light headed during the blood draw. I would also recommend drinking 64oz of water the day before and the day of the procedure.
