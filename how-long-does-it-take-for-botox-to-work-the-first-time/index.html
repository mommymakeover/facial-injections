How Long Does It Take For Botox To Work The First Time?

The results of Botox take a few days to start to take effect. Most patients begin to see a difference in the first 3-4 days and will see the optimal results about 2 weeks after the injections.

I usually see patients every 3-4 months for Botox treatments, however each person’s body has a different metabolism which effects how long the product will last.

I recommend following up with your injecting physician about 3 months after your injections, so that they can assess if you are ready for more.

Sometimes, I have patients wait another couple of weeks if the Botox hasn’t completely worn off, therefore ensuring that they get the best result possible.

There is no research that has shown a limit to the amount of times a patient can receive Botox treatments.
