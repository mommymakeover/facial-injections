When can I expect swelling to go down after Cosmelan?
Cosmelan is a very superficial peel
Sep 16, 2019
Cosmelan is a very superficial peel. Some people will swell quite a bit with it. Usually comes down in a few days. Talk to your dermatologist and he/she can give you some medications to help it resolve faster. 
Can Cellfina work for minor laxity where there is no cellulite?
NO
Nov 16, 2017
Cellfina is not used for skin laxity. non-invasive tightening devices and in some cases injectables may work better for this indication. 
Cellfina: dimpling on the front of my legs.
Combo approach is best
Oct 23, 2017
CELLFINA is best used for the buttocks and posterior/lateral/inner thighs. It treats dimples only. It is not intended to treat loose or lax skin, stretch marks, etc. The anterior thigh is less forgiving with CELLFINA than the other areas BUT CELLFINA is still used for dimpling on the anterior thigh. The guide plate on the thighs must be taken directly parallel with the dimpling (unlike the buttocks where different angles may work). Full releases are NOT recommended on the thighs. 
Will Cellfina help dings & dents from aggressive lipo?
Some doctors treat lipo irregularities with cellfina but results are NOT guranteed
Oct 23, 2017
CELLFINA is indicated for cellulite. Irregularities from liposuction can be treated with CELLFINA but may or may not improve. Irregularities from Lipo are best treated with more lipo and/or fat grafting.

How can a complication from Cellfina be fixed?
you need an in person evaluation
Oct 23, 2017
Cellfina can cause some temporary lumps during the healing process. These lumps go away in a few weeks. It is unclear what the lump is you are talking about and likely needs an in person examination. Cellfina is not intended to be used for post-lipo irregularities.
