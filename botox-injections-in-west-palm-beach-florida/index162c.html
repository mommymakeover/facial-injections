What is Botox?

In fact, botox is a highly diluted and purified botulinum toxin type A. (a strong natural poison neurotoxic effect).

When introduced into the areas of formation of facial wrinkles, it blocks the transfer of motor impulses to muscle fibers from the nerve endings. Due to this, mimic muscles relax, and the skin over the muscles no longer contracts and stretches.

As a rule, the first positive results appear already on the second-third day after the procedure. After a couple of weeks, the skin relief is leveled. Small creases and wrinkles disappear without a trace, and the deep ones become less pronounced.

A Minimally Invasive Answer To Fixing Wrinkles
