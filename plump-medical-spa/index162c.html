What are these weird chin lines when I smile called?
These are caused by a hyperactive mentalis muscle.
Feb 28, 2023
Botox/Dysport injections in the chin muscles, such as the mentalis muscle, can assist in providing the chin dimple a smoother, less wrinkled appearance. The goal of this procedure is to inject small, strategically positioned dosages of botulinum toxin into the chin muscles to create a controlled weakness.
