How Much Are Botox Injections For Wrinkles?

Botox cosmetic is used to treat dynamic wrinkles of the face. Botox has become one of the most commonly used medicines for non invasive facial rejuvenation.

The cost of botox depends primarily on the number of units injected and the technique of injection.

Typically, in a saturated market such as Los Angeles, Botox is administered at 12 to 15 dollars per unit, but the amount varies depending on the depth of treatment and the degree of dynamic wrinkling.

Botox cost depends on several factors

The price of botox to the physician is the same everywhere. This assumes your doctor purchased botox legally in the United States. Ask your doctor to show you the bottle of botox before it is reconstituted.

It should say Botox Cosmetic. If it only says Botox, even from Allergan corporation, it may have be purchased through a foreign company. If there is fluid already in the bottle it may have been reconstituted last week or last month.

Remember Allergan recommends to use it within 4 hours of reconstitution or it begins to lose potency. Lastly the more it is diluted, the less concentrated it is, and it will wear off sooner.

Botox: Does it cost an arm or a leg?

The price ranges from your geographic area, if a physician or nurse injects you, and the amount of botox or dysport needed to improve your animation wrinkles. Typically $10-14 a unit. The typical duration of effect is about 3 months with the usual sterile saline dilution.

By adding lidocaine with epinephrine and bicarbonate extends the duration for ten days and dysport 13 days. This technique would extend the timing before reinfection and thus save some money over the year with less repeat injections necessary.

A zinc supplement with an enzyme (Zytase), has been shown to increase the effectiveness and extend the botox effect in patients who’s duration of effect has been shortened over time.
