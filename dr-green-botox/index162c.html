Which dermal fillers would I benefit from as my face looks very flat?
Fillers and Botox for a fuller face
Oct 28, 2020
The main issue here seems to be volume loss and I would want to restore this lost volume. Two syringes of Voluma (one in each cheek) would add some beautiful structure and replace lost collagen. I would add a small amount of Botox in the glabella and Botox the masseter area to give you a less square jaw. Finally, some subtle lip augmentation with Restylane Kysse would be best for you. Please consult an expert in facial rejuvenation for the best cosmetic results. Best, Dr. Green in NYC
Should I get lip fillers or Botox around the lips? I’m 47 years old and want a very natural look.
Botox or fillers
Oct 28, 2020
A Botox lip flip will give you a subtle lift to the top lift and you may be happy with that alone. However, the lips are thin and would probably benefit most from one syringe of a finer hyaluronic acid filler and you could absolutely have natural looking results. Best, Dr. Green
What can I do after forehead wrinkles still prominent after Botox?
Botox and lines on forehead
Oct 22, 2020
Since these lines have been etched into the forehead, it will take repeated treatments over the course of a year, and consistent Botox, to help improve their appearance. In addition, you need to have additional topical treatments to work on the skin texture and improve its appearance, like micro needing with PRP, chemical peels, or eMatrix. Please consult an expert in Botox for the best cosmetic results.
What can be done to fix sagging skin on my face? I am in my early 30s.
Fix sagging skin with Thermage, dermal fillers, and Botox
Oct 22, 2020
The best way to help improve this sagging is with a combination of dermal fillers like Juvederm Voluma, Sculptra, or Restylane Lyft. A small amount of Botox injected into the corners of the mouth will help as well. New collagen stimulation with Thermage will help improve the jowls as well with amazing skin tightening production. Please consult an expert in skin tightening and cosmetic injection for the best results. Best, Dr. Green
