Botox and Dysport

At the heart of these drugs is a poisonous substance – botulinum toxin. Both Botox and Disport contain already purified substance, which is completely safe.

And already in this form they can be used for medical and cosmetic purposes.

The effect of these drugs is that they relax the muscles, eliminating wrinkles and unwanted facial creases. This caused an incredible popularity of Botox and from the recent time of Disport.

Botox and Aging

It would be incorrect to believe that Botox or Disport are a panacea in wrestling by skin aging. The possibilities of these drugs in this sense are limited.

Botox and Dysport are used to eliminate small creases in the corners of the mouth, wrinkles on the forehead, in the corners of the eyes. Recently, cosmetologists also use these drugs for the decollete zone and for the neck.

The mechanism of action of Botox and Dysport on tissues and muscles is much the same. Through the injection, they paralyze the muscle, relax it, so that wrinkles are smoothed out.

Cosmetologists are able even to change a sad expression on a joyful face, lifting the corners of the lips with the help of Botox or Disport. The effect of drugs lasts for six months, then the procedure must be repeated again.

At present, cosmetology has further expanded the scope of Botox. Now it is increasingly used to combat hyperhidrosis (increased sweating) and even to eliminate headaches.

Using and contraindications

Like any other medications, Botox and Disport have contraindications. First of all, this pregnancy and lactation. The drug is contraindicated in chronic diseases, when taking antibiotics, with inflammatory skin diseases.

Turning to the clinic for cosmetology service, ask to show the license of the medical institution, as well as documents confirming the legal origin and quality of Botox. The procedure must be performed in the sterile conditions of the clinic by specialists who have a license for this. The procedure is not painful, the thinnest needles are used. The desired effect you will see after 2-3 days.
