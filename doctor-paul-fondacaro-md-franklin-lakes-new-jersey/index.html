Is it safe to do Smart Lipo if I have Type 1 diabetes?
Smartlipo and Type 1 Diabetes
Apr 6, 2020
Smartlipo is an elective cosmetic procedure. Diabetes is a condition that requires medical clearance before any procedure is performed. In addition, one should have reasonable blood glucose and A1C levels. Anyone with diabetes may result in slower healing and increase risk of infection
