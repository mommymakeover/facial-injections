Dr. Serrão is especially well-known for his excellent results from vaginal rejuvenation procedures, such as clitoral hood reduction, labiaplasty, vaginoplasty, and perineoplasty. He is also a skilled injector who can expertly treat patients with neuromodulators such as Botox, to smooth wrinkles, and dermal fillers to rejuvenate, sculpt, improve symmetry, and add fullness.
