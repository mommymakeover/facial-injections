Are hip implants placed inside/behind muscle like properly placed butt implants?
Hip implants are typically placed above the muscle.
Sep 15, 2023
Unlike buttock and breast implants, hip implants are typically placed above the muscle. The implants are placed between the tensor fascia lata and the subcutanous fat. If you are very thin, the implant may be more visible, but usually there is enough soft tissue that hides the implant well.
Will skin in the hips be stretched after removing hip implants?

Sep 15, 2023
This will depend on how large of hip implants are placed. If they are very large implants and the skin becomes very stretched, it may not completely contract. However, if the implants are more modest sized, then there is a good chance that the skin will contract. The implants get placed deep on top of the TFL and thus unless you are very thin, there should be a thick flap of tissue which is likely to contract.

Considering a breast augmentation - which incision placement would work best for me?
Incision Location for Breast Augmentation
Jan 12, 2023
The three main incisions for breast augmentation are IMF fold, peri-areolar, as well as trans-axillary.

It appears that your left breast fold is higher compared to your right and will need to be lowered for better symmetry. I typically advise patients away from peri-areolar incisions as they have the highest rate of capsular contracture. You may consider a trans-axillary approach as these scars heal very well and are difficult to see.
