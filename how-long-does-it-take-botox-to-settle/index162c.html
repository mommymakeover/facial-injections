How long does it take for Botox to begin to work?

In my opinion, Botox usually starts to work in 2 to 3 days.

The maximal effect is usually seen by 10 to 14 days. It normally lasts 3 to 4 months.

When does Botox start working?

Neuromodulators like Botox work by making muscles relax. Botox starts to show an effect in 3-5 days and has its maximum effect in two weeks. Xeomin is quite similar in its action. Dysport begins to show an effect within a day and its effects tend to diffuse over a larger area than Botox.

Generally I offer my patients a “touch-up” at two weeks to ensure a good effect. Typically the results last about three months. I advise that if patients like the effects, they should come in for a new treatment when the muscles between the eyes (the ones that make the frown lines) start to work again, but before they are at their full strength.

After a couple of treatments on this schedule, you can start to see longer treatment durations as the muscles atrophy slightly. (Robert S. Schmidt, MD, Richmond Facial Plastic Surgeon)

Dr. Kimberly Short, MD, FACS, Indianapolis Plastic Surgeon – Botox

Botox starts to settle in 1-2 days but reaches full effect in 2 weeks

You may start to see a difference from Botox and other botulinum toxins including Dysport and Xeomin in several days, but it takes 2 weeks to achieve peak effect. It is usually helpful to follow up in 2 weeks for a touch up, especially if you are a first time patient.

There is no limit to how many times you can get Botox and patients usually need treatment every 4-6 months since it does wear off. (Giuseppe Militello, MD, New York Dermatologist)

Time for Botox to settle

For Botox to have its full effect, you normally have to wait around 2 weeks. As you rightly say, the effects last about 3 to 4 months.

There is no recommended limit on how long one can keep getting Botox, so you can keep having injections indefinitely. (Adam Sierakowski, MBBS, FRCS(Plast), Chelmsford Plastic Surgeon)

Botox effects take 1 – 2 weeks

Botulinum toxin (Botox Cosmetic, Xeomin, Dysport) is a medication injected in or near a muscle to stop signal transmission from the nearby nerves.

So it makes sense that the effect is not immediate. It takes about 90 minutes for the medication to be taken up by the target cells.

Although effects can start to be seen as early as 3 days, it takes 1 – 2 weeks to see the full efffect. That’s why I ask new toxin patients to return for follow up at 2 – 3 weeks after treatment for evaluation.

How Long Does Botox Take To Work Properly

When does botox start to work

Botox usually takes about 4 days to work. At 10 to 14 days you should see the results and if not, that is the time to call your practitioner. It will gradually wear off, and is usually gone by 3 to 4 months.

People who are very active, (in the gym twice a day) and those with lots of facial expression tend to wear off faster. (Jeffrey J. Roth, MD, FACS, Las Vegas Plastic Surgeon)

Botox results typically visible in 1 week

Botox results can be visible within several days depending on the area treated. Definitive results are usually seen in 1 week which is when I tell my patients it will look just right.

We often see our 1st time patients within 10 days post treatment to ensure that the results are perfect and determine if any minor adjustments are needed. (Andrei Metelitsa, MD, FRCPC, FAAD, Calgary Dermatologic Surgeon)

Botox treatment timeline

The effects of Botox usually begin to be noticeable between 4-7 days, with peak effect seen at 14 days.

It is for this reason that many practitioners (including myself) like to follow up 2 weeks after a treatment to ensure that an optimal result has been obtained.

Of note ‘static’ lines (those that are in the skin at rest) will continue to improve long after this time, as the body is able to remodel and replace proteins in the skin – meaning that in some situations skin continues to improve for some time following the initial 2 week period. (Scott Ellis, MBBS, Adelaide Physician)

Botox time to onset

Generally, I see the effects beginning to occur in 5-7 days for Botox and 3-5 days for Dysport. The neurotoxin injections should last 3-6 months. If it lasts less than 3 months, it may be appropriate to increase the dose the next time one is injected.
