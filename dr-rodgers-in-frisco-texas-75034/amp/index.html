Mole removal
Sep 5, 2018
Moles that are removed and made flush with the skin using a “shave” technique can grow back and re-pigment since part of the mole is still left underneath the skin.  It can be shaved again without harm.  The pigment can sometimes be treated with liquid nitrogen as well. 
 
Is it OK to take a long flight immediately after a fully ablative laser for acne scars?
Traveling after ablative laser treatment
Sep 5, 2018
Please discuss this with your treating provider, but I would recommend against any sort of travel for at least a week after a fully ablative laser treatment.  The skin will need to be protected and proper wound care will need to be performed in order to avoid any complications.

Alcohol is a well-known trigger factor for rosacea and many patients are advised to limit its use. However, one night of drinking should have only temporary effects on the skin.

Tattoo post accutane

Can I massage my chin to drain the fluid from my Kybella injections? 4 days post op.
Post Kybella swelling
Feb 27, 2018
Swelling after treatment with Kybella is normal, expected, and actually desired.  The swelling simply indicates that the fat cells were destroyed and the body is getting rid of them.  You should just let it run it’s course, but ice packs and ibuprofen can help to lessen any discomfort.  I would not recommend using a Clarisonic or any manual massage in an attempt to speed up the process.  
If I have bruising and swelling in my nasolabial folds after Juvederm, how long should I w
Work outs post filler treatments
Feb 13, 2018
You should wait at least 48 hours prior to any intense physical activity that raises your heart rate or blood pressure.  Bleeding after Juvederm injections is typically short-lived, but you should always consult your injecting provider after the procedure if you have a significant bruise, continued bleeding, pain or other symptoms. 
 
Juvederm for vertical lip lines: how long should I expect to take off work?
Down time with Juvederm
Feb 7, 2018
There is usually little to no down-time with Juvederm.  It is always possible to get a bruise after treatment, but bruises can be minimized by using ice packs after the procedure and can be covered up with make-up. 

One of the bests treatments for vertical lip lines is Juvederm Volbella, a long-lasting, natural-looking lip filler that helps to minimize the appearance of fine lines around the mouth.  

Can Botox be used to lessen severe marionette or nose to mouth lines?I look so miserable b
Botox and marionette lines
Jan 31, 2018
Botox is used to treat lines that show up when muscles contract, such as the forehead, crows’ feet, and frown lines.  Fillers, such as Juvederm, are used to treat lines that are present at rest.  This usually includes areas such as the marionette lines, nasolabial folds (nose to mouth lines), and the lower face.  It sounds as if you would be a candidate for fillers like Juvederm in the lower face.  
When can I get Botox after receiving a CO2 laser treatment ?
Botox and CO2 laser treatment
Jan 29, 2018
Wait until the skin is fully healed, usually 1 to 2 weeks after a CO2 laser treatment, depending on if it was fractional or fully ablative.  After that, there should be no reason to wait unless there was a complication from the laser, such as an infection.
