Would Ellacor be a viable option to help lift the philtrum?
Ellacor help lift philtrum
Feb 16, 2023
Micro-coring allows for removal of skin in the upper lip. This would allow for lifting to occur. As this is the only non-surgical way of skin removal, this is the best option for lifting the philtrum while avoiding an incision. Ellacor can be used over several sessions to obtain the results you are looking for. Good luck with your procedure.

Dr. Eric J. Wright

Is Ellacor an absolute no-no for people with facial implants?
Ellacor facial implants
Feb 16, 2023
I would recommend discussing this with your Plastic Surgeon. The concern relates to wanting to avoid any complications including injury of the implant or infection. After an examination, your surgeon can answer the probability of this occurring and if other options would be more beneficial.

Dr. Eric J. Wright
