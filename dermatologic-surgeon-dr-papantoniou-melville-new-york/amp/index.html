Is uncomfortable pressure in cheeks normal after Radiesse?
Healing after Fillers
Jan 26, 2019
It can take 1-2 weeks for swelling to settle after fillers. It is possible that you may have some deep bruising and that is why it feels so swollen and stiff. I would give it some more time, and sleep elevated on 2-3 pillows (sleep on your back). If in another week you do not see that you are improving I would return to your doctor for evaluation.Best, Dr P.
Is radiesse used on lips?
Radiesse not recommended for lips
Jan 26, 2019
The product is too thick for this area. For lips we use hyaluronic acid based fillers such as juvederm or restylane, for a softer more natural finish. Best, Dr P.
Could the raised area between my eyes be related to Dysport or is it something else? (Photo
Dyport
Jan 26, 2019
This can happen when more Dysport is needed in the glabellar area. I would see your injector and show them what is bothering you. A few more units in the correct placement will settle this unopposed muscle.Best, Dr P
What's the best procedure for pigmentation on African American skin?
Chemical Peels Only
Jan 26, 2019
I have extensive experience with skin of darker color, and I own many lasers. I do not use lasers on darker skin types to correct discoloration.... I have seen lasers worsen skin discoloration. It is safer and better to use a series of mild/moderate chemical peels to achieve your goal. Better to get there safely and not look worse than you did when you started.Best, Dr. P
Wrinkles and under eye sagging - what can be done?
Exilis Elite
Jan 26, 2019
Noninvasive radiofrequency devices such as Exilis and Thermismooth work well to tighten and improve skin laxity around the eye. When you start earlier you can help to reduce the appearance of upper eyelid skin laxity as you age. It takes 3-4 sessions, and is painless. Maintenance is 1-2 times per year.Best, Dr P
The lines in my neck seem more pronounced after a Fraxel treatment. Are they permanent? I a
Fraxel - Delayed results
Jan 26, 2019
2 days post Fraxel is way too soon to see any improvement. It will take 2-3 months for new collagen to be produced, so be patient. Your results will also be cumulative with your sessions. After a single session improvement for deeper lines will be subtle, which is why most will have 3-5 session.Best, Dr P.
What is the best laser or treatment for my acne scars?
Fraxel + Belafill
Jan 26, 2019
I would still recommend Fraxel 1550nm, 5+ sessions. It will help soften the appearance of your scarring. Combining the laser treatment with a long lasting filler such as Belafill may be a good option for your deeper pitted scarring to help level and smooth the areas. Best, Dr P.
How often can I get Fraxel Dual laser treatments?
Fraxel Dual
Jan 26, 2019
I recommend once every 4 weeks, no sooner. The fraxel sessions can be spread over larger stretches, such as 2-3 months without any negative impact on results.
Can I get Fraxel laser for acne scars if I'm under hormonal cure (contraceptive pill)? Or t
Fraxel
Jan 26, 2019
If you do not have a history of melasma, you have a high chance of tolerating the fraxel. I have treated many women with fraxel that have been on birth control without any incident of melasma. I find that melasma is unpredictable, and taking a birth control pill may theoretically increase your risk overall for melasma, but it may still be work you getting a fraxel treatment if your scarring warrants improvement. The risk of melasma is there but is very small.Best, Dr P.
EXTREMELY broken out and red 7 days post treatment. Is this normal?
Fraxel Post Care
Jan 26, 2019
This can sometimes happen. I find that people with more acne prone skin will have this response to fraxel treatments. I recommend non-occlusive aftercare to help prevent clogged pores, better to use a hyaluoronic acid based moisturizer to hydrate (avoid aquaphor or ointments/heavy creams), and I will often prescribe topical clindamycin for patients that I know are high risk of getting break outs (sometimes even an oral antibiotic).Best, Dr P
Is 3 weeks from my first fraxel to soon for another?
Fraxel
Jan 26, 2019
I usually recommend at least 4 weeks between sessions. I always tell my patients that they are still going to see the benefits and changes from 1 session over 3 months post treatment. If it was a very mild fraxel treatment then it may be possible to repeat it in 3 weeks, I would confer with your doctor.Best, Dr P
Do I need scar revision after riff?
Fraxel Dual

I use the Fraxel Dual which is a resurfacing laser, that only requires topical numbing, most patients with this type of scarring benefit from at least 5 sessions. Cost will vary depending on size and location. It is very safe and effective, it will smooth out the scar and also fade away the discoloration.Best of luck, Dr P.
