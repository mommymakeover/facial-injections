Can infection happen after pdo facelift ?
Not likely to be an infection.
Feb 5, 2019
Typically a painful lump after a PDO Thread Lift is caused by the thread buckling or curling under the skin. There are two ways to get rid of the lump. One way is to do a subcision treatment which releases the thread from the skin and allows it to unwind. Another option is to make a small stab incision to remove the thread completely and replace it at a later time. There is always a small possibility of infection particularly if the lump is warm, red or fluctuant.

Is it OK to take Advil after IPL treatment?
It is OK to take Advil.
Jan 28, 2019
After IPL, it is OK to take Advil, generally because it is not an injectable procedure and there is no risk of blood thinning. It is mainly important to avoid non-steroidal anti-inflammatory (like Advil) before any treatments that make cause bruising such as Botox and Filler treatment.
What you should avoid doing after an IPL treatment:
- No sun exposure for several weeks
- Skin exfoliants
- Medicated creams such as Glycolic or Retinoic acids

Jan 17, 2019

However, patients who see the best results typically combine the PRP treatments with topical agents such as Rogaine and oral hair growth treatments such as finasteride (finasteride is for male patients only).
Am I a candidate for PRP hair loss treatment?
Yes, you are a great candidate!
Jan 17, 2019
You are definitely a good candidate for PRP, especially since women are not a candidate to take oral finasteride. PRP treatments are one of the safest hair growth options for women. Typically a series of 6 treatments are recommended followed by several maintenance treatments per year. PRP treatments usually work better when combined with topical hair growth agents, such as Rogaine for women.
