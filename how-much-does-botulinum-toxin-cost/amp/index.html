How Much Does Botulinum Toxin Cost?

The cost of botulinum toxin will vary greatly – but in general, I advise going somewhere that charges you per unit. That way you know exactly how much product you are getting. If you get injected by a plastic surgeon, it may cost you more – in my office we charge $12-14 a unit.

I’m sure you see ad’s that are charging Botox at a cheaper price – but most patients prefer to pay a little more to have a plastic surgeon inject.

Botulinum toxin is a technical art, and to get a good result, experience and a good understanding of a patient’s anatomy is important.

In the end, find someone with experience and ask your friends for referrals. (Catherine Huang-Begovic, MD, FACS, Beverly Hills Plastic Surgeon)
