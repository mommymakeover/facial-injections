After laser hair removal, what happens to the hair that would have grown from those follicles?
Good Question!
Jul 5, 2017
After the laser hair removal treatment, the damaged hair is slowly expelled from the hair follicle which is why it sometimes takes a few weeks to see any immediate reduction in hair after the treatment. For overall reduction it does take a series of treatments done at scheduled intervals to achieve good results.
