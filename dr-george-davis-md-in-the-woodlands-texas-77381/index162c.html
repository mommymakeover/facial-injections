Hypopigmentation
Apr 18, 2019
Thanks for your question. The darker skin types run the risk of hypo pigmentation with any lasers. The pico laser has less chance of hypopigmentation but it can happen. PRP and shedding hair
Apr 18, 2019
Thanks for you question. PRP will see effects on hair growth usually by the 3 or 4th treatment. The hair cycle is about 3 months so until new hair is growing, you may not see any change.
