How difficult is a chin pad reduction? What are options for reducing size of chin pad?
Chin Options
Sep 25, 2019
Hi,
There are several treatment options for the chin. A lot of the decision making depends on your anatomy and what your goals are. Fillers can be used in the chin area to increase projection and make a chin “longer.” Botox can be used to smooth out dimpling and also slightly increase projection. Surgical options include chin implants, mentopexy, and genioplasty. I would recommend an in person consult to address your concerns!
