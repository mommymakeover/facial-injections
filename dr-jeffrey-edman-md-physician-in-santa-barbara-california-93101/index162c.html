I took two Aleve a couple of hours after I had CoolSculpting. Will this ruin my results?
Aleve shouldn’t affect your CoolSculpting results
Feb 21, 2018
Taking a couple of Aleve (or any other non-steroidal pain killer – Motrin, Advil, etc) shouldn’t affect your ultimate CoolSculpting results. Taking them immediately after the procedure may increase the possibility/extent of bruising. But, in reality, bruising is uncommon with CoolSculpting anyway.
