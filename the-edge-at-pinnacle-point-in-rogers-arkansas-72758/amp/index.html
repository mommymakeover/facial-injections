Doctors

Kevin Haney, MD

Name: Kevin Haney, MD
Statement: Dr. Kevin Haney is a board-certified physician in Rogers, Arkansas. As the medical director of Ozark Regional Vein Center and a leader in the field of phlebology, his medical expertise spans all areas of venous disease. Dr. Haney specializes in vein treatments for concerns such as varicose and spider veins.

At his practice, Ozark Regional Vein Center, Dr. Haney is dedicated to helping patients feel happier about their appearance by treating their various vein conditions with the most up-to-date methods and technology. Dr. Haney was the first doctor in Arkansas to use Venaseal and Varithena to treat varicose veins. He has helped over 18,000 patients with their vein problems.

Other treatments offered at Ozark Regional Vein Center include Venacure Endovenous Laser Ablation (EVLT), VeinGogh, ClosureFast, sclerotherapy, and the laser treatment Excel V.

Dr. Haney is also the supervising physician for The Edge at Pinnacle Point, a men’s clinic specializing in male optimization, as well as Renew Aesthetics, a medical spa that offers a variety of aesthetic treatments, such as Kybella, LaseMD, BodyTite, Emsculpt, and Vanquish, along with Botox, dermal fillers, Morpheus8, Emsella, and additional beauty services.

After graduating with honors from Arkansas Tech University, Dr. Haney obtained his medical degree from the University of Arkansas for Medical Sciences. He then went on to complete a radiology residency in Galveston, Texas, at the University of Texas Medical Branch Hospitals Programs.

Dr. Haney is board-certified by the American Board of Radiology and a member of the American Board of Phlebology.
