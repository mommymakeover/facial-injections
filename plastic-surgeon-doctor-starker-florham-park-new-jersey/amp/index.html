63 year old with Replicon implants which are very encapsulated. Can these be safely removed?
Can Replicon Implants be removed safely?
Aug 1, 2018
Thank you for posting this question.  Replicon implants were silicone implants  with a polyurethane surface  that gave them a 'fuzzy' texture in an effort to decrease the incidence of capsular contracture.  The appropriate treatment is the same as for any other implant that has developed capsular contracture, removal of the implant and the surrounding capsule.   Make sure to see an experienced board certified plastic surgeon for removal and to discuss how to proceed after that.  Best of luck!
