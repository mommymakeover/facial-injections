Fill cheeks before tear troughs
Nov 16, 2023
Photo is not a lot to work with but based on what I see I would fill cheeks first and then revaluate. The way the skin under your eyes will drape differently and you might not feel that you need under eye filler or might need a lot less.
Crows feet and comma line worse after forehead Botox, will it wear off?
Comma over brow after forehead Botox
Nov 12, 2023
Andrew, I think that the comma over brow certainly could have occurred after Botox. This can happen when muscles of forehead, which pull brow up, are relaxed and skin drapes differently. However, I have not seen crow’s feet get worse as result of Botox in forehead. Maybe you are just seeing more contrast now that forehead smooth. The good thing is that your Botox should be about to wear off and everything will go back to where it was before. No permanent changes expected.
Is there a reason why one cannot only put 1mls syringe of saline into 100 units of botox?
No wrong or right amount for reconstitution Botox
Nov 10, 2023
There is no correct or incorrect way to reconstitute your botox/neurotoxin. As long as the person that is injecting the neuromodulator is aware of how reconstituted and knows how many units /1 or 0.1 cc etc, they can do this however they like. Different ways of reconstituting will allow the injector to use the same tool in a bit of a different way so some people might do differently for different patients or for different areas of use.
Can masseter Botox change my face?
Master Botox to slim lower face
Nov 4, 2023
When someone has prominent master muscles Botox in the muscles can certainly slim that part of the face. This is what we see when we treat bruxism but also what we can do for those that are just interested in changing the shape of the face. I don’t usually recommend for older patients(not you) as method to change shape as they might not appreciate the volume loss.

Aug 23, 2023
Lip flip is very different than correcting gummy smile. Lip flip is meant to evert the lips a bit and make them look a little fuller. Gummy smile correction drops the lip down so you don’t see you gums when you smile.
