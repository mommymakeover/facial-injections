Botox lip flip does not add volume!
Jun 19, 2023
Botox lip lift with botulinum like Botox, Dysport, Jeuveau, Xeomin or Daxxify is great for creating a beautiful lip lift. But, be aware that it lasts only about 2-3 months, and does not add volume to your lip. For that reason, I prefer lip fillers to create a lip lift with added volume that lasts for years.
Under eye wrinkles – How can this be addressed?
Under eye wrinkles
Jun 15, 2023
If your skin is tight and healthy under eye wrinkles can be addressed with hyaluronic acid fillers injected into the tear trough area. If your skin is a bit older due to decreased elastin and collagen production your choice becomes an under eye procedure called blepharoplasty.
Had under eye filler twice, what is this line under my eye?
Under eye filler comes to surface
Jun 13, 2023
When getting under eye fillers you need to know that the skin is very thin and the filler can find its way to the surface. In such cases, injecting a little hyaluronidase will open the filler which is then massaged it into place. Once you get under eye fillers I recommend to start working on thickening the skin in the area with good product as well as modalities like Morpheus and IPL.

23 y/o male, what are these lines around my eyes and how do I flatten them? photos)
Lines around my eyes
May 26, 2023
Lines around the eyes can start forming in our late 20s. Under the eyes in the tear trough area it is caused by volume loss which can be replenished with hyaluronic fillers. This is particularly common in young men that don’t have cheeky bone structure. On the side of the eyes it’s caused by muscle movement of the lateral obicularis, commonly called craw’s feet. These lines can be undone with botulinum treatment like Botox, Dysport, Daxxify, Jeuveau or Xeomin.

Fillers for the Nasolabial folds
May 25, 2023
The Nasolabial Folds are created because of loss of volume in the upper facial parameters, typically the temples and the under eyes areas. I would consult with a doctor about injecting those areas first and then very lightly go with a nice filler in the folds. If you only inject the NLF you will end up very bulky around the mouth area and not get to the bottom of the problem.
