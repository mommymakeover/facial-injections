Dr. Aspacio’s specialities include surgical and nonsurgical cosmetic and medical dermatological treatments, including laser rejuvenation, cosmetic injectables, skin tightening, fat reduction, sclerotherapy, skin cancer screenings, platelet-rich plasma (PRP) therapy for hair loss, and managing chronic conditions like melasma, psoriasis, eczema, and acne.
