Duration of Botox effect

Most patients find Botox will last about 4 months– with a common range being 3-6 months. Many factors play a role in your results, but the 2 most important are (1) the experience of your injector and (2) the amount of Botox used for your given area.

Proper injection technique is critical. Understanding facial anatomy, the muscles involved and how they effect your wrinkles, placement of Botox correctly within these muscle bellies, and appropriately spacing the injections along the course of these muscles will all impact your Botox results.

With greater skill and experience, people find the injections are more comfortable, successful and last longer. Additionally, the amount of Botox injected will impact your results. If your making trip out of town, but you only fill the tank up 1/2 way, your not going to get to your destination.
