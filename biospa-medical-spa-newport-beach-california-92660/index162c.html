Is Kybella my best option 3 weeks prior to wedding?
Kybella injections 3 weeks before wedding
Jan 8, 2019
I would not recommend Kybella before your wedding.  The swelling after the injections is real and can last 7 to 10 days.   It would be best to do ThermiSmooth to tighten the area until after the wedding when you can fully commit to Kybella without worrying about the side effects.Best of luck.  Enjoy your special day!
Extreme swelling of lips following Restylane silk injections.
Swelling after Restalyne Silk on the lips
Jul 6, 2018
Extreme swelling after Restalyne Silk injections tends to be pretty usual especially if injected on the lips.  In our office, we get patients started on a Medral pack right  away as well as an antihistamine like Zyrtec and that seems to make the swelling a bit better .  You may want to   call your provider's office and ask for a steroid pack.  Be patient, your lips will look beautiful in a few weeks.
