Hi Anthony,
Unfortunately, scarring can be a very common result of picking at cystic acne. There are many treatments that can be done over a course of several months to help improve the scarring such as TCA cross, micro needling, and fillers. Resuming Normal Routines After Botox and Juvederm?
Answer
Jan 16, 2018
This is probably one of the most common questions following injections. After Botox injections the most important thing is not to rub the area around the eyes. You want to allow time for the product to settle. As for exercise the main concern is that raising your blood pressure can lead to bruising or swelling around the injection sites which most individuals try to avoid.
