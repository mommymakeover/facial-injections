Advantages of Botox at young age?
Advantages of Botox at young age
Nov 10, 2017
The theoretical advantage of getting Botox (other neuromodulators) at a relatively young age in the face of dynamic forehead wrinkles is to reduce the constant muscle contraction that distorts and destroys collagen in the area that leads to wrinkles and etched lines. I would add daily application of antioxidant-fortified broad spectrum sunscreen to slow UV-related skin aging changes and a peptide based formulation to help with collagen to your daily skin care regimen.

IPL and Laser Hair Reduction are not recommended for pregnant or breast feeding women.
