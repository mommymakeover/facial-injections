Botox for forehead lines
Nov 15, 2021
I often recommend a neurotoxin such as Botox or Dysport to smooth the forehead. Your lines are actually not very strong, so you would not need a very high dose to relax the movement. I recommend researching trained injectors in your area and scheduling a consultation.
Does stopping Botox do more damage than never having it?
Botox in 20's
Oct 26, 2021
Botox works by temporarily relaxing the muscles in the face. When you relax muscles, you develop less wrinkles. If you stop receiving Botox injections, the movement will simply come back, but not make anything worse.

It's important to schedule regular injections if you wish to improve static lines and overall texture. I recommend treatment every 3 months for maintenance, but you may be able to go longer at your age.

Botox in your younger years will mean you develop less wrinkles to treat...
