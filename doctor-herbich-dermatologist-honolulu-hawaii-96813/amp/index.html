Is there a difference between how deep voluma vs. Radiesse is injected in the temples?
Temple and zygoma fillers
Nov 8, 2019
I normally inject both fillers right above the bone to avoid blood vessels. Both fill above that plane because of displacement. Normally I gently place the needle over the bone. If I did a subdermal placement which I have done on occasion I use a 22 guage cannula to avoid the blood vessels. I would chose that technique to inject the temples for a patient with osteoporosis with very thin periorbital bones to avoid diffusion of lidocaine around the eye which could cause temporary double vision.
