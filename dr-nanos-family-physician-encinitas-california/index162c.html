Would I be able to receive Sculpsure, Emsculpt and Velashape on the belly on the same day?
Sculpsure, Emsculpt, and Velashape on the same day
Aug 3, 2022
Thank you for your question. We would not recommend doing all three of those treatments in the same day. You can combine Velashape and Emsculpt on the same day however, Sculpsure should be reserved for a separate day.
How long after a C-section can I do an Emsculpt session on my abdominal muscles?
Emsculpt after C-Section
Aug 3, 2022
Thank you for your question. It is best to wait 6-8 weeks after your C section. However, you need to check with your OBGYN to ensure you are cleared for the treatment.
Can you have EMsculpt with a Kyleena IUD?
Emsculpt and IUD's
Aug 3, 2022
Thank you for your question. Yes you can. The only IUD contraindication for Emsculpt is if you have a copper IUD.
Does Emsculpt help with diastasis?
Emsculpt and Diastasis Recti
Aug 3, 2022
Thank you for your question. Yes, Emsculpt helps with Diastasis Recti. Emsculpt treatments target the major muscles of the abdominal wall with powerful contractions , including the rectus abdominal muscles.
I would like to firm up, not so much as to gain volume. Would I benefit from Emsculpt?
Will Emsculpt cause significant volume?
Aug 3, 2022
Thank you for your question. Yes, Emsculpt only tones the muscle as well as decrease in body fat. It does not cause you to gain significant volume.
Should I/should I not exercise the same treated areas prior and post Emsculpt?
Exercising and Emsculpt
Aug 3, 2022
I would not work out the same area the day before the treatment. I recommend waiting at least 2 days post treatment to workout the same area.
Wondering if Emscult on the inner thigh would help my torn acl?
Emsculpt with a Torn ACL
Aug 3, 2022
For a torn ACL it is imperative that you focus on building strength in the muscles surrounding the knee which includes the hamstrings and the quadriceps. Emsculpt would be great at strengthening the muscles around the knee thus improving support of the knee. We had a patient who tore their ACL and had an 8% muscle deficiency in their quadriceps, however after 4 treatments of emsculpt on the quadricep the muscle was back to normal.
