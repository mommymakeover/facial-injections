Microneedling and Juvederm Vollure lip filler: I’m worried it somehow dissolved.
Avoid microneedling after Juvederm
Apr 22, 2018
Microneedling prior to filler is a plus! One only needs to wait 3-4 weeks prior after the needling for the dermal filler. The reverse is not true. Microneedling after the filler is not helpful and will shorten the duration of the filler. Your brief treatment today most likely did not make much difference, but I would not continue that practice. Microneedling the lips is not recommended either, the lips are more subject to inflammation and can place you at risk for granuloma…
