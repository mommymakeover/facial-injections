Is it possible to get Botox around the mouth for fine lines? Also to help with down-turned corners? (ph
Combination treatment is better than Botox or filler alone
Apr 7, 2019
Botox can definitely be used to lift the corners of your mouth and also help with fine lines around your lips. However strategically combining Botox in those areas with micro-aliquots of filler would give you a more max result. Good luck!
Hollow and darkness under eyes - How can I get rid of them?
Juvederm Voluma works best for midface and under eyes
Apr 7, 2019
Thank you for sharing the photo. You have volume loss in the middle area of your face extending from your under eyes through the upper part of your cheeks.  The best treatment for this is an injectable call Juvéderm Voluma. Usually one syringe per side is enough to brighten your under eye area as well as a lift and contour your cheeks. Good luck!
Lip injections: will this heal or is it permanent?
Use warm compresses instead of ice
Apr 1, 2019
There are ways to prevent bruises after lip injections but once they are there it can take a week to resolve. I recommend warm compresses instead of ice, to encourage flushing/circulation of the trapped blood (bruise). Good luck!
Volbella for Smokers Lines - Is this related to swelling and is this normal?
Swelling after Volbella can last 72 hours
Apr 1, 2019
At 16 hours, it’s a bit too soon to assess because swelling after Volbella can last about 72 hours. Given that your upper lip already projected more than your lower before the treatment I recommend waiting a week and possibly adding some around lower lip edges to create a more balanced, natural appearance. Good luck!
It’s been almost a year Since kybella. Will hardness go away? Is this going to be permanent?
Use warm compresses and massage
Apr 1, 2019
Hardness or lumps after Kybella is often due to lymphatic congestion.  In order to release this congestion I recommend applying warm compresses regularly and getting lymphatic drainage massages. Good luck!
Would cheekbone enhancement balance my face?
Combination of Botox and Voluma works best
Mar 31, 2019
I agree that there is more volume and fullness on your lower face therefore I would start with enhancing and contouring your mid face and cheekbone area with Voluma. 1 to 2 syringes of this product would give you great balance and structure and still look very natural.  I would also use some Botox in your upper face to balance your eyebrows since your left eyebrow is higher than your right. Good luck!
A month after steroids and dissolver there's no change in my under eye bags. How do I get rid of them a
Juvederm pockets under the eye should be dissolved
Mar 31, 2019
In general Juvederm ultra or ultra plus should not be used to treat the under-eye area because it is too hydrophilic, meaning it draws too much water into the tissues and risks creating a visible bubble or bag as you demonstrate in the photo. Prednisone does not help clear the bag, and steroids  should not be injected into this area because there is a risk of skin atrophy. Precise  injections of hyaluronidase, an enzyme that dissolves hyaluronic acid, works best. Good luck!
How long should I wait after having Bell’s palsy to start injecting my face with Botox and fillers?
Wait at least 3 months until symptoms clear
Mar 31, 2019
I agree with the opinion that a neurologist should clear you prior to getting Botox after having Bell’s palsy. I recommend waiting at least 3 months after resolution of your symptoms.  Good luck!
How can I get rid of these lines along my cheeks?
Juvederm Voluma is the best option
Mar 29, 2019
You have classic volume loss in your mid face causing shadowing under eyes and in cheeks.  This may be visible because of your genetics or perhaps you have lost weight in general during the past couple years. Either way you would respond really well to two or three syringes of Juvéderm Voluma. Good luck!
Should I have an excision for this acne scar?
Don’t replace a scar with another scar
Mar 29, 2019
I advice against surgery as your first option.  While I don’t think laser resurfacing is safe for your skin type I do think your scar would respond well to subcision and filler. Good luck!
Do I have an infection? What are my options?
Delayed reactions to filler are rare but can occur
Mar 29, 2019
Thank you for sharing the photos. Delayed reactions to hyaluronic acid fillers can occur. It means that your immune system has been triggered by something that resembles the hyaluronic acid filler that you previously had. I recommend a short course of prednisone to reduce your immune system response. Good luck!
Wrinkles and veins and creepy skin around eyes. Can a CO2 laser correct this?
Combination of laser and filler works best
Dec 31, 2018
For your facial architecture and skin type, I recommend CO2 laser resurfacing followed by filler, 2-3 months after laser. This will yield max results. I’d also recommend a skin care regimen that includes a growth factor serum and hyaluronic acid eye cream. Good luck!
Should I do more CoolSculpt now or try Emsculpt?
High def Lipo > Coolsculpting/Emsculpt
Dec 31, 2018
I agree with Dr Emer. Coolsculpting or emsculpt by itself won’t get you the long-term results you’re probably going for. Try Vaser, or high def microcannula Lipo. One treatment and done. Good luck!

Halo is good, Fractionated CO2 is even better
