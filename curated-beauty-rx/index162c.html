What is the downtime for Opus Plasma?
Downtime for Opus Plasma
Oct 20, 2020
There’s really little downtime. You can expect redness for up to 48-72 hours depending on the settings used for your treatment. You can also anticipate texture changes to be visible for about 1 week. Makeup can be applied the next day. That’s the beauty of Opus.

Permanent or temporary nerve damage?
Nerve injury after FaceTite
May 24, 2018
Temporary nerve palsy can happen after FaceTite and/or neck liposuction procedures. This will resolve with time and is usually temporary. If it persists for a few months you may consider botox to help improve symmetry while its healing.
