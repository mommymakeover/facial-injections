I got Botox and it didn’t work, what may be the reason that I didn’t get any results?
Botox not working…
Jul 29, 2018
There are few possible reasons for Botox to not to work as expected
1- Men usually have stronger facial muscles compare to women and will require more units of botox.
2- Botox in some of the patients may take up to 7 days to show full effects.
3- Rarely, some patients have antibodies against botox which makes them more resistant and may need more units compare to others.
4- Unfortunately some of the providers may dilute the botox too much which makes the injection less effective.
