Is it normal for filler to cause broken capillaries around my nose?
Filler and capillaries
Nov 30, 2019
Capillaries around the nose are very common even in patients who have not received filler. These can be easily treated with a laser that treats small capillaries.
Is there a difference between how deep voluma vs. Radiesse is injected in the temples?
Volluma and Radiesse in the temples
Nov 30, 2019
Both Volluma and Radiesse are injected at the same depth just above the bone in the temple area. Different providers feel comfortable with different products, I would choose a product that your provider is most comfortable working with.
Can Botox (in masseter) and dermal filler injection be done right after Thermage? Should cannula inje
Botox and filler after Thermage
Nov 30, 2019
It is perfectly safe to receive Botox or Filler injections by either needle or cannula technique one week after Thermage.
My botox has went away and I was curious if I could still get botox now that I have thyroid issues?
Botox with thyroid goiter
Nov 30, 2019
Your thyroid issues should not have any effect on the results or duration of your Botox treatment. Full results from Botox will be visible after 7-14 days.
I had a shingles shot and a flu shot yesterday. Can I have a cosmetic Botox treatment today?
Shingles and flu shot and Botox injection
Nov 30, 2019
Your shingles and flu shot should not have any effect on the results or duration from your Botox treatment. Full results from your Botox should be seen in 7-14 days.
Is It possible to smooth the entire forehead with Botox, but keep natural movement of the brows?
Botox and eyebrow movement
Nov 30, 2019
It is possible to have a smooth forehead and still maintain the movement of your eyebrows. This requires very specific placement of the Botox to achieve this result. Given that your 3 wrinkles are above your brow, I would discuss your concerns and desired outcome with your provider.
How would the at-home Radio Frequency facial machine affect fillers and Botox?
Botox/filler and at home radio frequency machine use
Nov 30, 2019
An at home radio-frequency machine should have no effect on your filler or Botox. However, I would recommend wating 48 to 72 hours after receiving Botox or filler before using your radio frequency device.
Would immune response from minor illness (cold, mild sore throat, etc.) on day of Botox injections tr
Botox injections with a cold
Nov 30, 2019
A minor infection like a cold or sore throat should not affect your response to Botox. Some providers prefer that you wait until you are no longer ill. I would recommend contacting your provider prior to your appointment to see if they are still willing to administer the Botox.
What can I do about hereditary horizontal forehead lines?
Botox and forehead lines
Nov 30, 2019
You are a great candidate to begin the use of neuromodulators (Botox, Dysport, Xeomin) to reduce your forehead lines. These lines are caused from active use and expression in your face and take root over many years. Botox is a protein that relaxes the muscles responsible for these lines. Starting to use "baby" Botox in your 20s will prevent these lines from becoming deeply engraved and more difficult to eradicate later in life.
Bells Palsy and Botox/Fillers... is it safe?
Botox and Bells Palsy
Nov 30, 2019
Most patient's begin to recover from Bells Palsy within two weeks; however, full recovery may take 3-6 months. I would wait until you are fully recovered, but if you do retain some asymmetry Botox and filler can be used to help restore facial symmetry.
How long after microneedling can I get Botox?
How long after microneedling can I get Botox?
Nov 30, 2019
In our office, we provide miconeedling and Botox with the same appointment. However, if you have already received your microneedling, I would recommend waiting 48-72 hours to allow your skin time to heal before getting Botox injections.
9 months after Botox I suddenly got festoons. Are these from Botox? Need a second opinion.
Botox and Festoons
Nov 30, 2019
Complications from a Botox injection would not begin to arise 9 months after the fact, so it is safe to say that your festoons are not caused by your Botox injections. Botox is metabolized by the body after 3-4 months. Sorry for your difficulties, and please continue working with your Dermatologist for options to treat your festoons.
Is it possible to get Botox to crows feet only?
Botox for crow's feet only
Nov 30, 2019
Yes, it is completely possible to just have Botox injected for your crow's feet. You shouldn't need to get Botox in your forehead if you do not want Botox in your forehead.
You may want to look for a different provider that can help you achieve your desired results.
