What kind of treatments can I get to help with my nasolabial folds?
Nasolabial folds
Oct 10, 2022
The quickest and least invasive treatment with immediate results that actually improve is filler. I like Radiesse hyperdiluted for full facial lifting to contour the face for a liquid facelift. It will lift up the cheeks and jowl area and sculpt the jawline to help the folds concerning you. PDO threads is more invasive but will also give nice lifting results and stimulates collagen production.
Can I have botox a month before shoulder surgery (rotator cuff)?
Botox before shoulder surgery
Jul 8, 2022
Unless your surgeon tells you otherwise in order to err on the side of extreme caution by reducing all chance of possible infection from needle punctures, there should be no problem having Botox a month before your surgery. Having said that the decision is best made in conjunction with your surgeon who knows your medical history and background.
