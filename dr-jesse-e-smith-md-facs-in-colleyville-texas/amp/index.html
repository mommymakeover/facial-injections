His private practice encompasses the entire spectrum of facial rejuvenation and reconstruction, with procedures ranging from minimally invasive treatments to highly complex surgeries.

Dr. Smith specializes in rhinoplasty, face and neck lifts, and eyelid surgery (blepharoplasty), as well as FUE hair transplant surgery and facial reconstructive procedures. He is also known for his expertise with injectables such as Botox, dermal fillers, and Kybella.

Lip filler after facelift

Allergy to fillers
Jul 29, 2020
Thank you for your great question. It is possible to have reactions that can resemble allergic reactions, but can be infectious or inflammatory in nature instead. In our practice with situations like these, we will cleanse a small area in the forearm, inject a small amount of a different dermal filler, and observe for any reaction. If no reaction, we proceed with injections. Our offices have the necessary protocols, medication, and supplies present in the event of allergic reaction.
