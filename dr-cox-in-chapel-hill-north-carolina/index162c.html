Do facial fillers stretch out skin and cause sagging? Do they ever fully dissolve?
Do fillers stretch out skin and cause sagging?
May 28, 2020
Hello and thank you for your question! Fillers do not cause stretching of the skin. I recommend using a HA (hyaluronic acid) filler that can be dissolved. Improper placement of fillers in certain areas could cause migration. This is commonly observed around the eyes. If you are interested in facial fillers, I would recommend seeking out a board certified dermatologist or plastic surgeon.
How long should I wait after my Cellfina procedure to have my Emsculpt sessions done on my butt?
Emsculpt After Cellfina
Feb 12, 2020
Hello and thank you for your question! I recommend my patients wait one month post Cellfina to begin Emsculpt treatments to the buttocks. We want to make sure that most of your swelling and soreness have resolved before beginning the treatment. The supramaximal contractions of Emsculpt could be more painful if you still have a lot of residual soreness and bruising from Cellfina. Hope this helps!
