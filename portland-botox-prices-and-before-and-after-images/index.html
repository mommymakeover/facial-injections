Portland Botox Prices And Before & After Images

Botox is a drug that contains inactivated botulinum toxin.

Injection of the Botox solution into various muscle groups on the face (in the forehead, eye area or nasolabial sulcus) leads to a permanent relaxation of the corresponding facial muscles, so that the mimic wrinkles in the injection area disappear or decrease.

Is it possible to increase the volume of lips with Botox?

Botox lips – this misconception arose because of the misuse of the names of various drugs. Botox on the face is used only to reduce facial wrinkles. If you want to increase the volume of the lips, you need injections of intradermal fillers, such as Restylane or Juvederm. Botox – application features.

The constant reduction of facial muscles leads over time to the appearance of facial wrinkles. Such wrinkles cosmetologists call – dynamic.

Botox Clinics Portland Oregon

The introduction of botulinum toxin into mimic muscles (causing wrinkles) causes a persistent long-term relaxation of these muscles, which leads to the disappearance or reduction of wrinkles in the area of injections. Indications for use of Botox for cosmetic purposes

  • wrinkles on the forehead, and between the eyebrows
  • wrinkles around the eyes – the so-called “crow’s feet”
  • pronounced nasolabial folds.

Botox injections take only 10-15 minutes. The maximum result after the procedure is 10-14 days.

After applying the Botox, the effect lasts approximately 6 months, after which you can undergo a second procedure.

It should be noted that Botox is recommended by the FDA (the most prestigious medical organization in the US), as one of the best methods of removing facial wrinkles.

It should be noted that with very deep wrinkles, Botox injections will allow them to only be reduced, but most likely they will not be completely removed. Therefore, with deep wrinkles, Botox injections are carried out in combination with injections into the base of the wrinkles of intradermal fillers based on hyaluronic acid – “Restylane” or “Juvederm” preparations.

Botox Analogues

The analogues of Botox include, first of all, a drug such as “Dysport”, as well as preparations “Xeomin” and “Myobloc”. All these drugs are slightly different from each other, and therefore are not completely interchangeable. Consultation of a specialist and the following information will help you find out what is best for you – Dysport or Botox.

What is better than Botox or Dysport?

Dysport and Botox have very similar mechanisms of action (due to neuromuscular blockage of facial muscles with botulinum toxin).

Dysport contains less protein, and therefore has a lower protein load than botox. This means that the introduction of Dysport will cause the formation by the immune system of fewer antibodies directed against it. Our body uses antibodies to detect and destroy foreign proteins (antigens).

A smaller number of antibodies means a longer duration of action. Therefore, the effect of Dysport should be somewhat longer than from Botox.

However, when injected into the soft tissues of the face, Dysport captures a larger volume of soft tissues during injection, spreading more than botox. This is both good and bad. This is good because on the forehead, then fewer injections are needed, because the area of the forehead is large enough.

And so it means for the patient less chance of discomfort, swelling or hemorrhage after injections.

The bad thing is that the distribution of the disport in the tissues is less controlled, and if the doctor’s skills are insufficient, the drug can spread to undesirable areas (especially around the eyes), causing adverse side effects, for example, omission of the eyebrows or blurred vision.
