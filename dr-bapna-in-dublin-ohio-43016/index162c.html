Will the leftover filler affect the rhinoplasty since it is a closed rhinoplasty?
Dissolving filler before rhinoplasty
Apr 13, 2021
I always recommend dissolving filler in the nose before rhinoplasty. I routinely do this for my surgical patients even if they have had their filler placed by a different practice. We also take new pre-surgery pictures once we think all the filler is gone.
What can be done for drooping and pointing nose too close to lips?
Rhinoplasty to improve tip drooping vs. a trial of Botox/Dysport
Feb 16, 2021
The most definitive way to improve this would be a rhinoplasty to improve the position of the tip cartilages at rest and when you smile.

You could try a small dose of Botox to the muscle (depressor septi) which makes the nose drop when you smile. This may help very slightly for about 3 months.

Does anyone have another suggestion versus filler for upper eye hollows?
Upper eye hollows
Aug 6, 2019
You appear to be a good candidate for filler injection into the hollows above your eye. It is a challenging injection but I have plenty of experience injecting this area with a cannula for the last several years. Filler tends to last 1-2 years or more in this area. It's important to be conservative to maintain a natural appearance. We do have a couple before and afters on my own patients on our Instagram. 
What can be done to straighten my nose? Have been told it's impossible.
Facial Asymmetry and Rhinoplasty
Apr 30, 2019
It is very challenging, almost impossible, to obtain a perfectly straight nose on a patient with facial asymmetry. That being said, you should be able to get some improvement and help with the imbalances that you have, if it bothers you. If your expectations are in line with your surgeon's, you could be a good candidate for surgery. 
Open vs closed rhinoplasty? (Concerns about scarring)
Open vs. Closed Rhinoplasty
Feb 2, 2018
It would be very rare for you to have any keloid or hypertrophic scar from an open rhinoplasty procedure. In the end, it is important that you find a rhinoplasty surgeon that you are comfortable with and either approach may be able to give you the result you want.
Lower eyelid surgery done inside eyelid with FX laser, also to lower eye area three weeks ago. What now
Swelling after lower eyelid surgery
Feb 2, 2018
At three weeks after lower eyelid surgery, you definitely still have swelling. I would be patient and follow your doctor's recommendations for now. After about six months, if you still have some issues, and there is firmness in the area, injections can be used to soften any scar tissue. If the area is soft, there may be some residual fat that could be removed or repositioned.
Is there anyway you can get rhinoplasty without taking naroctics?
Pain after Rhinoplasty
Dec 9, 2017
Most of our patients can manage the discomfort after rhinoplasty surgery with just Tylenol for a few days. 
1 week post rhinoplasty, are these the final results?
Rhinoplasty Swelling
Dec 9, 2017
At one week after surgery you still have a lot swelling. By one month half of your selling should be down, but it may take one year to see the final result. Be patient and follow the advice of your surgeon. The dorsum may look over reduced because the tip may be more swollen then the rest of the nose. 
I got my lips injected 5 days ago with Juvederm and my top lip has bumps, can this be fixed?
Bumps in lips after Juvederm
Nov 19, 2017
Start gently massaging the bumps and see your injector after another week or so. Most bumps will flatten out within 2 weeks of injection.
Can my nose be fixed without something too invasive? What kind of procedure is used in fixing the colum
Rhinoplasty for Collumellar Show
Sep 11, 2017
You may be a candidate for a minor tip rhinoplasty to just correct the hanging part of your columella if you are happy with the rest of your nose. I would recommend a consultation in person to determine if you would be a good candidate for this. We need to assess your cartilage and what changes could be made. If you're a good candidate this could be a relatively easy procedure with minimal downtime.

Swelling with Juvederm Vollure

The swelling you are seeing is definitely within the range of what to expect with any lip injections. Try to ice today and avoid heavy salt foods. How to control pain during buccal fat removal?
Pain during Buccal Fat removal
May 20, 2017
The procedure is usually down under local anesthetic. After 1-2 quick injections of numbing medicine into the cheek, you should not feel anything except pressure. If you really wanted to, the procedure could be done with IV sedation or even general anesthesia to keep you even more comfortable. Talk to your surgeon about the options.
Will lying down 2.5 hours after Botox affect the results or negatively?
Lying down after Botox
May 20, 2017
You will not affect your results at all, lying down after treatment makes no difference in results. In fact, many practitioners inject with patients laying down, while I inject with patients sitting up. In the end, the results are the same. The only thing to avoid is rubbing the muscles for several hours after injections, especially brow and forehead, as this could push the Botox into unwanted areas and affect neighboring muscles making your eyelids droop.

May 16, 2017
 You look like a good candidate for rhinoplasty. Also consider a chin augmentation to help balance your facial features if the overall size of your nose bothers you.  You may not notice a drastic difference with your nose only but I think balancing your facial features with some volume in the chin will definitely help.
