Jun 12, 2017
You may lose some hair but the
hair removal lasers use a much longer pulse width so that they can better
destroy the hair follicle. The ultrashort pulse widths used to shatter the ink
and allow the tattoo to be removed are too short to permanently remove most
hairs but they can stun hair growth for several months .
