How to Minimize Scar After Mole Removal?
Scarring will be minimal in the hands of an expert
Feb 14, 2018
The only way to establish whether a mole is benign or not is through laboratory pathology. It is impossible to say definitively that a mole "looks" benign. The best way to prevent scarring is to have the lesion completely excised (cut out). You especially want to avoid any type of treatment that will destroy the tissue such as laser or liquid nitrogen cryo therapy. These types of treatments leave no tissue for a pathologist to examine.
