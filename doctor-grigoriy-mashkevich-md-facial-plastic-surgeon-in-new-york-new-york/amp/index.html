Will my nose still grow after getting Rhinoplasty at 16?
Rhinoplasty at 16
Apr 11, 2015
Everyone follows different timing with their growth spurt. While general guidelines about one’s age are a useful starting point, it is important to know if the growth spurt finished before undergoing rhinoplasty. That can be gleaned from recent changes, if any, in height or shoe sizes. However, generally speaking, most 16 year old patients can undergo rhinoplasty safely, without worries of additional nasal growth.
