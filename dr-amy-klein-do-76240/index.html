Is Botox safe in regards to its ability to cross the blood brain barrier?
Botox safety
Feb 6, 2019
Generally, the Botox in injected into the muscle and the effects stay locally. If it is injected into an artery or vein, it could get into the blood stream. I am not aware of any post-treatment CNS problems. However, I advise you to only get injections from a trained medical provider to assure injections are being placed correctly.

I advise you to follow up with your provider for an examination.

How will the MonaLisa Touch procedure affect lichen planus?
Lichen Planus
Nov 14, 2018
Lichen planus can affect the vagina. However, menopause causes many changes to the vagina that result in dryness and painful intercourse. I would recommend you see your gynecologist for a thorough exam. If there are any suspicious lesions, a biopsy can confirm the diagnosis. MonaLisa Touch is helpful for lichen sclerosis and dryness from menopause. Lichen planus is generally treated and controlled with topical steroids.

The MonaLisa Touch can be used on patients that have a mid-urethral sling in place for incontinence. Generally, it is not performed on patients that have a vaginal mesh in place. I would not recommend any laser treatments in the vagina if the mesh is exposed.
