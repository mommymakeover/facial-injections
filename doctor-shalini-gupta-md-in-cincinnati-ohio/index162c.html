Statement: Dr. Shalini Gupta is a dermatology physician and the founder of the Dermatology, Laser, and Vein Center in Cincinnati, Ohio. Best known for her expertise with laser skin resurfacing treatments, Dr. Gupta offers a wide range of cosmetic dermatological services, including dermal fillers, neuromodulators such as Botox, body contouring procedures like Emsculpt and CoolSculpting, and hair restoration. Passionate about helping her patients feel confident, Dr. Gupta has built her practice on the philosophy of “look young, live young.”

Since opening her private practice in 2001, Dr. Gupta has expanded her range of laser skin resurfacing and rejuvenation offerings from two to 25 advanced therapeutic devices. She takes a holistic approach to practicing medicine, integrating her knowledge of skin rejuvenation methodologies with her studies in traditional Ayurvedic medicine. Her goal is to empower every patient and provide them with natural-looking, aesthetically pleasing results.

Dr. Gupta graduated with her bachelor’s degree from Ohio’s Miami University in just two years with a 4.0 GPA before earning her medical degree from Harvard Medical School with honors. She then completed a combined plastic surgery and general surgery internship at Brown University, followed by a fellowship in photomedicine at New York University Medical Center’s dermatology department. She completed her training with a residency at the University of Washington in the dermatology department before relocating to Cincinnati.

Dr. Gupta frequently shares her expertise in aesthetic medicine at professional conferences, in news articles, and on television segments. She also proudly acts as a trainer to other physicians and medical providers, instructing them on the best techniques for performing aesthetic procedures. In her free time, she enjoys the company of her two sons and family, as well as traveling, painting, and dancing. Dr. Gupta is trilingual, speaking German, English, and Hindi.

How to get rid of Radiesse?
Hyaluronidase injection
Mar 3, 2021
A Hyaluronidase injection will break down the chemicals from the filler. It acts as a "dissolver". Every doctor that performs injectables, especially filler, should have hyaluronidase at their office.
What is the best way to flatten and remove pigmentation from a keloid scar?
Diode Laser:
Mar 3, 2021
At our office, when it comes to keloid scars, our favorite to use is our Diode laser. The Diode laser is able to flatten the scar as well as reduce some of the pink pigment. You would probably need around 2-3 sessions to see the results you are looking for. If there is some pigment left after the Diode laser, you can follow up with a Nd:YAG to help with the vascular color of the scar.
