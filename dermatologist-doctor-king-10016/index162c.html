Statement: Dr. Hadley Claire King, MD, is a Harvard- and Columbia University-educated, board-certified dermatologist in New York City with over 15 years in practice.

Dr. King specializes in medical and cosmetic dermatology. She treats skin concerns like acne, rosacea, eczema, psoriasis, hair loss, seborrhea, and skin cancer. On skin rejuvenation front, she offers neuromodulators like Botox and Dysport to soften lines and wrinkles; dermal fillers like Restylane and Juvéderm to plump, lift, and smooth skin; and TruSculpt iD, a nonsurgical treatment to remove unwanted fat.

Dr. King graduated magna cum laude from Harvard University with a degree in biochemistry. She received her medical degree from Columbia University before continuing her medical training at Greenwich Hospital, which is affiliated with the Yale University School of Medicine. She then completed her dermatology residency at the Weill Medical College of Cornell University.

Post-residency, Dr. King worked as an attending physician at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, where she specialized in cutaneous oncology (skin cancer) and photodynamic therapy, a treatment to kill cancer cells. Her research in immunology has been published in a variety of medical journals, including the prestigious Journal of the American Medical Association.

A RealSelf Top Doctor, Dr. King is board certified by the American Board of Dermatology and is a fellow of the American Academy of Dermatology. She is also a clinical instructor of dermatology at the Weill Medical College of Cornell University.
