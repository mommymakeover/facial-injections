What is the best way to dissolve HA under the left eye?
Swelling After Filler Injections to Undereye Area
Oct 29, 2018
Since you just had the treatment today, you are swollen from the recent injections and will continue to have some swelling over the next couple weeks.  It is much too early to see a final result from your fillers, and it is common for one side to be more swollen than the other.  If there is still a lump or asymmetry after the swelling has resolved, then the filler can be dissolved in the office by injecting an enzyme called hyaluronidase.

Hair Regrowth After Mole Removal
Oct 29, 2018
Dear luntana~
Thanks for your question.  Although some hair follicles may have been removed with the mole, some of the remaining follicles may also be traumatized from the procedure with the potential for improvement as the area heals.  You may benefit from PRP or topical treatments to help stimulate hair growth.  Hair transplantation would also be an option later on if the follicles do not recover.
Best wishes, Dr. Amy Hsu
