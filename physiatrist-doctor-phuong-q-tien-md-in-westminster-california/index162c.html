Tattoo Removal: What is The “Pulse Width”
Laser Tattoo Removal – What is pulse width?
May 17, 2019
Pulse width is the time it takes for the laser to deliver a certain amount of laser energy to break down the tattoo ink pigments. The time duration of the laser energy must be very short, the shorter the better, so that the tattoo pigment is heated to fragmentation temperature before its heat can dissipate to the surrounding skin. Otherwise, heating of the surrounding tissue can cause burns or scars. For laser tattoo removal, this duration should be on the order of nanoseconds…
