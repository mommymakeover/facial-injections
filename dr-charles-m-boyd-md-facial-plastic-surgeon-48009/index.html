RF Microneedling damaged my face and left me with crepey skin, what treatments will correct the dam
RF Microneedling Follow Up
Mar 30, 2022
Thank you for your question. I would follow up with the provider who did the treatment to reassess how your skin is healing. Generally RF microneedling helps with fine lines/wrinkles, skin texture, and collagen production with a series of treatments. Want to tighten and lift cheek under eyes - Is Ultherapy what I need?
Skin Tightening
Mar 30, 2022
Ultherapy is a great non-surgical option for skin tightening and collagen production. We often treat the mid and lower face. In my practice we also generally do a combination approach of ultherapy, PDO threads, and injectables such as HA fillers or sculptra to help lift the skin. Best results are seen over a few months. I recommend scheduling a consult with a board certified plastic surgeon or experienced provider to discuss a treatment plan that's right for you.
Why does my skin look like this? How can I get rid of it and have clear skin?
Acne Treatment
Dec 3, 2021
Acne commonly requires a combination approach of topical medication and other treatments for redness and scarring. In severe cases, you may even need a course of oral antibiotics. We commonly do BBL laser for acne redness and Blue light to kill acne causing bacteria. VI peels are also helpful in reducing pigment and breakouts. I would recommend scheduling an appointment with a dermatologist to determine what treatment options are best for you. Best of luck!
Can masseter Botox or Ultherapy achieve a flat-cheek look?
Facial Contouring
Dec 3, 2021
Thank you for your question.Ultherapy can help lift and tighten the skin and is commonly used for the mid and lower face. Masseter botox will contour the face but will not reduce cheek size or jowling unfortunately. Dermal fillers placed in the correct areas, however, may help reduce the appearance of jowls and buccal cheek fat. I recommend scheduling a consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon or experienced injector to discuss what treatment options are best. Best of luck!

What are options for hair loss treatment/prevention?
Hair loss
Apr 26, 2021
Thank you for your question. In my practice, we often do Platelet rich plasma (PRP) injections combined with microneedling to restore hair volume as well as prevent hair loss. This is a great non-invasive alternative, and 4-6 treatments are generally recommended with yearly maintenance. I would recommend scheduling a consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon or dermatologist to see what options may be best for you. Good luck!

What's the best treatment for visible pores on nose and cheeks? Microneedling, intracel, Venus viva
Large Pore Treatment
Jan 26, 2021
Thank you for your question. Enlarged pores are best treated with CO2 fraxel laser and microneedling. Both of these treatments are usually done in a series of 3-6 sessions.
