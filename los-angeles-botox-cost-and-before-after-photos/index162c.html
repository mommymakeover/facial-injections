The word “Botox” is familiar now to almost everyone who pays sufficient attention to their appearance; and women, and men. It, probably, as well as aspirin, it is possible to write already with a lowercase letter, so it is commonly used.

Botox is a drug produced by Allergan (USA). Since 1989, it has been used in neurology for the treatment of diseases accompanied by muscle spasms, for example, infantile cerebral palsy.

The active substance Botox is purified and weakened botulinum toxin.

Application area

In cosmetology, Botox and its analogue – Dysport are used with to eliminate facial wrinkles.

It is mimic, the emergence of which is due to the motor activity of mimic muscles.

With the tension of these muscles, the skin gathers into folds. Over time, they stop completely breaking down and mimic wrinkles are formed. Most characteristic of their appearance in the upper part of the face: on the forehead, between the eyebrows, in the corners of the eyes.

For the smoothing of wrinkles of another origin, there are other methods: contour plastic surgery, threadlifting, chemical peelings, etc.

How does Botox work?

Traditionally, Botox is injected intramuscularly into mimic muscles, where it temporarily blocks their susceptibility to nerve impulses.

Due to this blockade, the muscle or part of it relaxes, ceases to contract and the wrinkles formed by it are smoothed out.

The art of the doctor is to not completely immobilize the muscles responsible for the appearance of wrinkles, but only to remove their increased tone without violating the natural facial expressions.

Under the influence of botulinum toxin, neuromuscular bonds are temporarily blocked. Neither muscle nor nerve fibers are affected.
Botox action is reversible – in 4-6 months blocked neuromuscular connections are restored. A special technique for using Botox is mesobotox, when it is injected not intramuscularly, but into the skin.

How to prepare?

Three days before injections of Botox it is recommended to stop taking antibiotics and drugs that prevent blood clotting: Aspirin, Heparin, Fenindion, and others. For the day you need to stop drinking alcohol. On the day of the procedure, do not exercise, heavy physical labor.

How is this procedure performed?

At the consultation, the doctor evaluates the severity and character of wrinkles, the role of facial muscles in their formation, the state of the tissues, determines the points of Botox administration, and the amount of it. As with normal injection, the skin is treated with a disinfectant. Then, using a syringe with a short and very thin needle (0.3 mm in diameter), Botox solution is injected into those muscles whose activity should be reduced. This takes 3-5 minutes.

At the end of the session, antiseptic treatment of the skin and its cooling are performed. Data on the lot number of the product, its expiry date are entered in the outpatient card.

The painfulness of Botox injections is low. They can be performed without anesthesia, but most cosmeticians prefer to do local anesthesia with a special anesthetic cream. In the case of Botox injections without anesthesia, most of our patients compare their sensations with mosquito bites.

How many units are needed?

The amount of Botox in a vial is measured not in grams, but in units of action (units of action). The price of the service is determined on the basis of the amount of the drug used. Since price is an important factor, you can estimate in advance how many Botox units you will need to correct wrinkles:
