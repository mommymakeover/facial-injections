Can I get lip injections after I've already had permanent lip implants?
Lip Filler
Sep 8, 2020
Yes, you are able to add lip filler to your lips after lip implants. A small amount of Botox added to the filler can allow more of the lip to be seen (a lip flip). A lip lift - a surgical procedure where the area between the lip and the bottom of the nose is shortened so that you can see more lip - is also a possible idea.Best to you.
