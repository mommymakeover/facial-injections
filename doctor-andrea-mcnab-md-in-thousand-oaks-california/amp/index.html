Juvederm ultra 4 v. Voluma XC for cheeks: what’s the difference?
Voluma vs Juvederm
Dec 11, 2019
Voluma is a thick filler that targets more of a “lifting” effect onto the cheekbones and that is its primary function. Juvederm is more of a utility filler (medium thickness) that can be used in multiple different areas of the face. Voluma has been shown to last longer which accounts for some of its higher price. Needless to say, not all fillers are equal! Be sure to visit an experienced injector for the best and most personalized advice.
