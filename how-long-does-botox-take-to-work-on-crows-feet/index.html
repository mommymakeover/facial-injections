How Long Does Botox Take To Work On Crow’s Feet?

Botox can treat a multitude of facial lines including crows feet, frown lines, forehead, wrinkles.

It works by relaxing the facial muscles in the injected areas and it takes about 2-4 days to see some softening.

The full effect is not appreciated for about 2 weeks. However, this does vary from to patient to patient. Once the Botox takes effects, your skin will appear more youthful and smooth.

Its recommended that you schedule follow-up’s every 4 months as the effect begins to wear off by then.

Longstanding surface wrinkles will take continued treatments for the muscles to relax sufficiently to repair the surface irregularities.
