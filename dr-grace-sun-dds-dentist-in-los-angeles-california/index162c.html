What teeth whitening treatment has the best results? With zoom lamp or something else?
Laser teeth whitening is the best option!
Apr 20, 2018
Laser teeth whitening is administered in a dental office exclusively, and is the most effective method for whitening. Bleach is applied to each tooth, and heat generated by a laser, typically an Er:YAG laser, enhances its effectiveness while ultimately speeding up the process. This procedure uses a strong bleaching gel that the laser activates and enhances for quick, dramatic results. Typically a hydrogen peroxide bleach gel is used ranging from 25 to 40 concentration.
