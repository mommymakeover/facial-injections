What’s the Best Laser Hair Removal Machine?
Best Laser For Hair Removal
Dec 27, 2017
There is not one answer to this question. The best laser for hair removal depends on skin type. In general, the best laser to treat individuals with light skin is a 755 nm wavelength Alexandrite laser and the best laser to treat individuals with dark skin is 1064 nm wavelength NdYag laser. It is especially important that dark skinned individuals are not treated with the wrong laser to avoid burns, hyperpigmentation and/or hypopigmentation.
