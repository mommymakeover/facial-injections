How can I tell if I will be square shaped after a tummy tuck?
square tummy tuck?
Jan 22, 2020
The final result definitely is a combination of what the surgeon does and the preoperative appearance of the patient. Typically after a woman has children, the abdomen seems more "square-shaped" due to stretching of her rectus muscles. When the plastic surgeon repairs the muscle and performs an "internal corset" it is called a diastasis repair and makes the abdomen look more hourglass. This is something you should definitely discuss with the plastic surgeon you end up choosing. 
Does it look like I need lipo, muscle repair, and tummy tuck? I am hoping I will not require an ex
tummy tuck
Jan 22, 2020
Typically, a tummy tuck includes liposuction. In my practice, a "tummy tuck" includes repairing the diastasis (muscle), liposuction and removing the skin. When a procedure is done to remove only the skin, it is typically called a panniculectomy. Liposuction alone will not address the issues of extra skin that needs to be removed. When shopping for a plastic surgeon, look at credentials as well as before and after photos that actually are similar to your current appearance and your goals....
