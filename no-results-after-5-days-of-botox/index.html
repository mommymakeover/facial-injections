No Results After 5 Days Of Botox

Movement 5 Days After Botox

Five days is still pretty early after a Botox treatment – I generally counsel my patients that they may see initial results of their treatment at 3 days, and that it takes 14 days to see full results.

20 units dose is a standard dose between the eyebrows, and should be sufficient to achieve a good result in most patients. I would recommend that you follow-up with your injector after 2 weeks if you still notice movement – if needed, they should be able to perform a touch-up treatment at that time.

You should not experience any numbness after your treatment. Botox should only impact muscle movement and not sensation in any treated areas. (Faiyaaz Kalimullah, MD, FAAD, Chicago Dermatologic Surgeon)
