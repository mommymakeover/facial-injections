I'm starting to develop Exogenous ochronosis due to hydroquinone. I've stopped using it. What treatments
Exogenous Ochronosis
Sep 12, 2017
Ochronosis is associated with prolonged use of hydroquinone (generally greater than 3 months). Unfortunately, once you get, it can be quite difficult to treat especially with darker skin types (Fitzpatrick 4-6). You can try to various lasers but the safest and most effective would be pico-wave laser. Another option would be microneedling.
Best wishes, Dr. Tam Nguyen
Is it safe to have a glass of wine after stomach Botox injection?
Botox after wine
Sep 12, 2017
Hello Lilly,
In general, wine can increase the risk of bleeding/bruising. Other than bruising, the wine should not interfere with the Botox. However, most people who enjoy daily glass of wine should not have any problem including bruising.
Regards, Dr. Tam Nguyen
