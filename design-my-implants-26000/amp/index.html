Would I be a good candidate for a chin implant?
chin implant
Feb 24, 2023
There are two options for a recessive chin. You can consider a chin implant or sliding genioplasty. When the chin is very recessed and needs 7-9 mm of added projection I prefer the sliding genioplasty. When the chin is less recessed and only requires 5-7 mm of projection I will consider an implant. I some cases, a large implant can make the chin look wide and more masculine which is fine in most males. Both procedures are performed in the office with intravenous anesthesia.
Would a 9mm anatomical chin implant be too big for my face?
chin implant
Feb 23, 2023
When a chin is very recessive and needs 9 mm of advancement I recommend a sliding genioplasty procedure. I perform both genioplasties and the placement of implants, and when a large implant is placed in a women it can sometimes look wide and masculine. The genioplasty procedure brings your own natural chin forward and looks more feminine. The procedure is performed in the office with sedation anesthesia and does not affect your bite. Most people resume a normal diet the same day.

Chin implant with cleft preservation?
facial implants
Jan 31, 2023
A custom or even a stock implant can give you a permanent improvement to your lack of chin projection. In some cases the cleft of the soft tissue is unaffected in other cases it can be diminished. Custom implants cost significantly more and will not preserve your cleft better than a stock implant. If you implant is created by a cleft in your chin bone a better option to preserve the cleft is a sliding genioplasty.
