Is it normal for filler to cause broken capillaries around my nose?
Filler and capillaries
Nov 30, 2019
Capillaries around the nose are very common even in patients who have not received filler. These can be easily treated with a laser that treats small capillaries.
Is there a difference between how deep voluma vs. Radiesse is injected in the temples?
Volluma and Radiesse in the temples
Nov 30, 2019
Both Volluma and Radiesse are injected at the same depth just above the bone in the temple area. Different providers feel comfortable with different products, I would choose a product that your provider is most comfortable working with.
My botox has went away and I was curious if I could still get botox now that I have thyroid issues?
Botox with thyroid goiter
Nov 30, 2019
Your thyroid issues should not have any effect on the results or duration of your Botox treatment. Full results from Botox will be visible after 7-14 days.
I had a shingles shot and a flu shot yesterday. Can I have a cosmetic Botox treatment today?
Shingles and flu shot and Botox injection
Nov 30, 2019
Your shingles and flu shot should not have any effect on the results or duration from your Botox treatment. Full results from your Botox should be seen in 7-14 days.
Is It possible to smooth the entire forehead with Botox, but keep natural movement of the brows?
Botox and eyebrow movement
Nov 30, 2019
It is possible to have a smooth forehead and still maintain the movement of your eyebrows. This requires very specific placement of the Botox to achieve this result. Given that your 3 wrinkles are above your brow, I would discuss your concerns and desired outcome with your provider.
