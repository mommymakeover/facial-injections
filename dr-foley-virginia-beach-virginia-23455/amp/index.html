Massage after a Botox injection

Can Botox in the forehead lift brows? Does anyone use this technique?
Botox brow lift
Feb 10, 2019
To achieve an effective lift of the brows it is necessary to address the muscles that act to depress the eyebrows, the orbicularis oculi and the corrugator-procerus complex. By treating just the frontalis muscle, which elevates the eyebrows, the lifting is not achieved. However, if all the muscles mentioned above are treated carefully, a nice mild lifting effect can be noticed.
