Nasolabial folds over filled – what can I do to do to rectify it?
Overfilled Nasolabial folds
Nov 16, 2022
If you feel your nasolabial folds are overfilled, then you can have the filler dissolved if it was a reversible filler. It is a very simple procedure, but depending on the filler injected, it might take a few sessions to get you to your desired result. -Dr. Yovino
Will Botox work for headaches caused by hyperactive trapezius muscles?
Botox for trapezius hypertrophy
Nov 16, 2022
Botox should definitely work to reduce the size of the trapezius muscles, but it will take many months of disuse and retreatment for that to happen. It is frequently injected in hyperactive muscles or trigger points to calm them down. You can also try Dysport, which works better and lasts longer.
Not seeing results from Sculptra, can I be immune to its properties?
Impossible to answer
Nov 16, 2022
Without any before and after photos to base it on, it is impossible to answer your question. Chances are there are some subtle improvements in your face, skin and underlying structure. Sculptra also continues to show improvement over time. I would return to where you had it done to take a follow up photo and compare it to your preprocedure photo.
Eyebrow droop after Botox for Migraines – will it go back to normal?
Droopy brows
Nov 16, 2022
Botox only lasts three to four months on average. After this, the muscles regain function and your eyebrows should go back to their usual position.
Help for this sun damage on chest – will IPL help?
IPL
Nov 16, 2022
Yes, IPL will help even out your tone, brown spots and redness. It will take a few sessions to get you where you want to be. Make sure the area has not been exposed to sun in the two weeks proceeding your treatment and arrive with washed skin that does not have lotion or a spray tan on it. Recent sun exposure, moisturizer and spray tans can all make you more prone to burning from an IPL treatment. Hope that helps! -Dr. Yovino
RHA filler for marionette lines in jaw and cheek. Is it going to make a difference?
RHA Fillers
Nov 16, 2022
RHA fillers have become my filler of choice for the nasolabial folds and marionette lines and the soft tissue of the face. It acts like natural tissue and makes the face look as though it was photoshopped. Make sure your injector is not heavy handed with the fillers. It is always better to be a little deflated than overfilled.
Will Botox help me with my gummy smile?
Botox for a gummy smile
Nov 16, 2022
Botox would definitely help you with your gummy smile! Once the lip relaxes downward, you may want a touch of lip filler as well.
