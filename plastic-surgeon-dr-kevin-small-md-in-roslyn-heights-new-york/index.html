To achieve optimal contour, patients may need revision body contouring after pregnancy or weight changes. Fleur De Lis abdominoplasty may not correct persistent flank lipodystrophy and ptosis (Love handles). To correct this deformity, an abdominoplasty may need to be converted to a lower body lift (a 360 degree circumferential incision) to contour the waist, the flanks and the buttock.
If I’m 270 lbs, how much weight will I have to lose to get a BBL and 360 lipo?
Body Contouring and Obesity
May 5, 2020
Patients should be close to their goal weight (Body Mass Index <30) and their weight should be stable for minimum three months before any plastic surgery procedure. Operating before patients have achieved their goal weight increases your chance of post operative complications and poor aesthetic outcomes. Morbidly obese patients should consult a weight loss specialist or bariatric surgeon before considering a plastic surgery procedure.

Do I have Tubular Breasts? Could I correct my breasts without implants? Perhaps with an areola reductio
Tuberous Breast deformity
Jan 22, 2020
Tuberous breast deformity consists of an enlarged areola, protrusion of breast tissue, and a constricted lower pole of the breast. Unfortunately, you have a mild tuberous breast deformity. A tuberous breast deformity can be corrected by reducing the areola size, releasing the constriction bands, and filling the breast envelope with a small implant.

Swelling after a Breast Reduction
Jan 22, 2020
The breast will continue to settle over the next few months. 50% of the swelling dissipates 3 weeks after a surgical procedure; 80% of the swelling dissipates 6 weeks after a surgical procedure.
