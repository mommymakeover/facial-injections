Bruising after Xeomin in forehead, 2 weeks post op, what should I do?
Xeomin and bruising
Feb 8, 2018
Dear Amal3242,
Bruising can happen after xeomin injections especially in the delicate tissues and fine blood vessels around the eyes. Your chances of bruising are also higher if you take any blood thinners, NSAIDS, vitamin E, fish oil, or certain herbal supplements. The bruising tends to settle in the area right below the eye due to gravity and at this point applying warm compresses, IPL or laser treatment can make it go away faster.
Best,
Dr. Liu
