How Long Before Botox Results Can Be Seen?
Timing of Botox effects
Nov 13, 2018
Patients typically start to notice Botox taking effect in 4-5 days after the procedure but the peak effect occurs at approximately 2 weeks, therefore, it’s recommended to wait two weeks before deciding whether or not a touch up may be necessary. There is no data to date on treatment restrictions of long term use.
