10 days post belly button issues
Belly button
Dec 26, 2017
The material stuck to your belly button is an antibacterial gauze called Xeroform.  At 10 days post-op I think you should be fine to remove it.  Assuming you have no drains, the easiest way would be to soak it off in the shower.  If you are not allowed to shower, you could soak the area with sterile water or saline.It looks like you have a nice early result.  Good luck!
Drains following tummy tuck/liposuction. How long is too long to keep drains in?
Drains after TT
Dec 26, 2017
There is a tremendous amount of variability regarding how long it takes to get drains out after TT.  Make sure you keep all f/u appts. with your Plastic Surgeon and avoid twisting and shearing movements at the waist.  That said, 3 weeks is a long time to continue drainage, and your surgeon may need to consider a sclerosing procedure, such as with Doxycycline, via your drain tube.Hope this helps.  Good luck.

Dec 26, 2017

Nicotine patch before breast augmentation?
Nicotine and Breast Augmentation
Dec 26, 2017
I agree that you should not be receiving Nicotine in any form (smoking, chewing, patch, gum) prior to surgery.  It takes several weeks to clear the Nicotine from your system.  I require my patients to be Nicotine-free for at least 3 weeks before and 3 weeks after surgery.  If necessary, we obtain a urine Nicotine level for documentation.  Good luck!
