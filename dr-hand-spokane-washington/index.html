Would Botox tighten my double chin?
Botox vs alternative treatments for double chin
Aug 4, 2019
A Nefertiti style microdroplet Botox technique can help improve minor skin laxity in the jaw and neck but does not significantly improve changes due to sub mental fat. Better treatment options for “double chin” due to fat would be kybella, liposuction or combined liposuction with laser or radio frequency tissue tightening.
