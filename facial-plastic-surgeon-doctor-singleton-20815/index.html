Post-rhinoplasty

Why is my nose different at left and right angles?
Nasal assymetries
Jul 18, 2019
Hello - everyone has asymmetries between the two sides of the face, especially the nose. Subtle differences in how the cartilage is structured under the skin between the two sides will result in different appearing shapes on the various views of the nose. Along those lines, deviation of the septum maybe pushing the cartilage to one side versus the other, which again will create the subtle difference between the two sides. Hope this helps
-Dr. Singleton
