Can a person with kidney and gall stones get Emsculpt?
I recommend to wait
Apr 14, 2023
According to the manufacturers of Emsculpt, the treatment has not been specifically tested on people who have had kidney stones or gallstones. Without knowing more about the specifics of your case, it's difficult to say whether or not Emsculpt would be a safe treatment option for you.
