Mini Tummy Tuck Straight After C-section?
Mini Tummy Tuck Straight After C-section
Apr 9, 2019
As many have said, I would recommend you are at least 6 months out from your C-section before performing a mini tummy tuck. This will give you time to get down to your pre-baby weight and also allow your body time to heal. By being down to your pre-baby weight the surgeon will be able to remove the most skin and give you the best contour. Good Luck and congrats on the baby!!

Under eye Botox issue - had Botox under eyes 4 months ago with bad result. One eye puffs out
Lower lid swelling after botox
Nov 26, 2018
I'm not a believer in injecting Botox in the medial aspect of the lower lid because of the exact complication that you're describing. Fortunately your botox should be wearing off by 3-4 months. As the Botox is metabolized you should return to your pre-injection state. If the issue hasn't resolved by 5-6 months I would recommend you get a second opinion from a board certified Plastic Surgeon or Oculoplastics Surgeon.
