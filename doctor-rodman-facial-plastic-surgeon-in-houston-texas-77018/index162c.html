Do Kenalog injections after rhinoplasty reduce the swelling?
Kenalog
Aug 18, 2019

My chin protrudes and it has a lot of fat in it. What is the surgical procedure required to fix this?
Chin surgery
Aug 7, 2019
For most patients the shape of the chin is determined by the bone.  To create a real change in the shape, the bone needs to be reduced or set back. This is done through an osseous genioplasty.  Although many patients demonstrate that they can pinch skin or fat off the chin, the majority of what makes that shape is the bone.  A soft tissue reduction will only create a small change. Consult with a craniofacial surgeon to discuss your optionsBest of luckDr Rodman
