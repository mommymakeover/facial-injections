Lopsided lips - what to do about it?
Research Your Injector
Oct 13, 2023
The injector's technique plays a big role in the shape and symmetry of the lips. It is also important to allow the lips to heal fully before judging shape and size. Sometimes one side takes a little longer to heal and settle. A more contoured look is achievable by injecting the cupid's bow and vermillion border in a certain way. The product used can also play a role in how crisp the results look. 
How much lip filler will I need to get the results I want?
Filler Results
Oct 13, 2023
The amount of filler needed depends on the final desired outcome you want as well as your starting point. This is difficult to decipher without seeing photos. However, it is important to note that slow and steady building is best. Placing filler too quickly back-to-back can render complications such as filler migration.
Lumps after lip filler for about 7 years. What should I do instead?
Superficial Lumps
Oct 13, 2023
The lumps that you are seeing are indeed superficially placed filler. Your provider can use a dissolving agent to break down the lumps. After that, I would suggest only going to an injector you know and trust moving forward. It is important to stay with a provider who is familiar with your situation and anatomical features.
I feel like my lips look crazy after getting filler dissolved. What can I do?
Filler Dissolve
Oct 13, 2023
It appears as though there is filler outside of the lip boarder and that the dissolving wasn't done thoroughly. Often times, dissolving filler can take several sessions. It is also important to wait 2-3 weeks before refilling the lips.
Will adding more filler to the bottom and dissolving the top lip help me achieve a natural look?
Natural Filler Results
Oct 13, 2023
More filler can be added to the bottom only to achieve a more natural, desirable ratio. If for some reason you aren't satisfied after the filler is added to the bottom, then options of dissolving the top can be explored. 
Space above upper lip deformed by filler? Any advice on how to fix it?
Lip Filler Fix
Oct 13, 2023
I would recommend dissolving everything again in several sessions, waiting a full 2-3 weeks before refilling, and refilling using a different filler product of your injector's choice.

What is causing bulging when I smile after fillers/Botox?
Bulging Under Eye Cause
Oct 3, 2023
It appears as though either too much filler was used or the filler used was not placed deep enough. The best option is to dissolve this area using a hyaluronidase solution to reduce the bulging.
How can I reduce the appearance of sunken eyes?
Reduce "Sunken Eyes"
Oct 3, 2023
Filler, PRP injections or lower blepharoplasty surgery are all options that can be used to treat this area. Seek a consultation with a provider to see what the best treatment plan is for your individual case.
Can filler be done in upper and lower eyelids for my hollowing?
Filler For Hollowing
Oct 3, 2023
Filler can definitely be used in the tear-trough (under eye) area to help fill the volume loss and hallowing. Filler placed in this area can also help with any under eye darkness. As far as the upper eyelids go, this is not an area that filler is used in. For upper eyelid drooping or excess skin, it is best to seek surgical options such as upper blepharoplasty or browlift procedures.
How much restalyne would I need to lift the mid face?
Mid Face Filler Amount
Oct 3, 2023
One syringe of filler equates to the size of a blueberry when injected. You will most likely need three to four syringes to achieve the desired outcome. It is important to treat the midface area (cheeks) first, as this is where the majority of volume loss occurs.
Lip filler nodules/swelling months after procedure. Will I ever be able to get filler again?
Nodule Solution
Oct 3, 2023
There can be several possible causes for nodules forming after filler. I would recommend dissolving everything completely, waiting 2-4 weeks, and re-injecting your lips with the filler injections placed more superficially rather than deep in the lip tissue.
