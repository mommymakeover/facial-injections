Does Laser hair removal after hydrafacial cause wrinkles?
Does Laser hair removal after hydrafacial cause wrinkles?
Jun 28, 2019
Hi,
I'm sorry to hear about your experience. Performing a hydrafacial 2 days after laser hair removal on the face should not cause wrinkles. The laser can cause hyperpigmentation if you are in the sun a week prior to and after your laser treatment. It is imperative to use sunscreen daily to prevent this. It would be best to follow up with your provider to discuss your concerns and be evaluated.
Is it normal not to be red the day following microneedling with PRP?
Microneedling
Jun 28, 2019
Hi,
That is an excellent question. More often than not, redness can exceed 24 hours. There are many variables that can cause one to have prolonged redness. Factors such as if your provider was more aggressive with the treatment settings, and using topicals such as retinols and acids applied near the treatment time, can play a role in redness. The best thing to do is follow up with your provider as we all appreciate feedback.

Juvederm wore off in a week, what is the best alternative that would last longer?
Metabolizing Filler
Jun 23, 2019
Its true that we can metabolize filler at different rates but metabolizing filler within one week would be unlikely and more than likely just the swelling diminished. I always recommend for patients just to continue to build on their prior filler if they are willing to do filler in a slow and steady way. There are also some great surgical options like lip lifts if filler doesn't turn out to be your thing :).
What happens if you have a soft lump from having fillers and want it removed by having
Hyaluronidase
Jun 23, 2019
Hyaluronidase can be used to dissolve fillers such as Restalyn and other HA fillers. It can be used in mini doses or full doses, however it is difficult to control how much it dissolves of the filler in any one setting. Some practices charge for this service and others (likely only if its their own work) may not.
Do I need more filler in my lips to correct the unevenness?
Uneven Filler
Jun 23, 2019
Uneven filler can sometimes occur and we always recommend following up with your provider so they can assess in person. Sometimes it just requires a baby dose touch up to make things more even. Anatomy can make some situations more challenging than others but there are some filler tricks your provider can do to make it look as even as possible.
Can I get Botox injections from 2 different doctors in the same week?
Staying with one doctor would be recommended
Jun 9, 2018
It would always be recommended and best to stick with one doctor or practitioner, as Botox can take a full 14 days for the protein to bind to the muscles. Jumping around from doctor to doctor in the same week can potentially cause over treating an area and could give you unwanted and non appealing results such as Spock brows, droopy eyes, etc.
