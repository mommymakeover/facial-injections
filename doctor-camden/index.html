How long should you wait after having a baby to get Botox?
Botox
Jul 9, 2020
As long as you are not breastfeeding, you can restart Botox (or any other neurotoxin such as Xeomin or Dysport) at any time after giving birth. If you are breastfeeding, you will need to wait until you're done breastfeeding. Best wishes!

Facial filler
Jul 5, 2020
There is no evidence that facial filler will cause tissue damage or distortion if removed or dissolved over time. Does the type of lip filler influence migration?
Lip filler migration
Jul 5, 2020
As long as the injector uses proper technique and you follow aftercare instructions, filler should not migrate. Products with higher cohesiveness tend to resist migration, as well. I tend to prefer fillers such as Belotero, Juvederm Ultra, and Juvederm Volbella in the lip area, depending on the look the patient wishes to achieve. Kysse is a newer product in the US that has also shown great results in the lip area.

Jul 5, 2020
There is no contraindication while you are not pregnant (and filler will not impact your fertility evaluation/labs).
