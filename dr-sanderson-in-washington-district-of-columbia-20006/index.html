Can you have laser resurfacing in an area that you have a tattoo (that you don't want to remove)?
Laser resurfacing and tattoo
Sep 1, 2019
Thank you for your question. The pigment of a tattoo is deposited deep in the skin. To remove a tattoo you need a high energy laser designed to target the specific color of the tattoo (there are different lasers for different colors). Laser resurfacing to improve skin quality typically has a different target and should not have any effect on a tattoo. I would recommend consultation with a board certified physician to discuss options for treatment. Good luck!
