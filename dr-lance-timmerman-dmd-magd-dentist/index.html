Can a crown be permanently dyed to match the other teeth?
Dental crowns are very color stable and not easily removed
Aug 11, 2021
The short answer: no.

The first issue is removing the crown to do anything to it. Crowns don’t just “come off”, they must be cut off. To change any color would be so problematic, that making a new one is really the only option.

So the BEST advice is go pay for an exam and records (be CERTAIN it is a 3D xray, called CBCT) and the dentist can tell you what it would take for dental implants. Any dentist that quotes a fee…

I would like another professional opinion on my bulky veneers?
Porcelain veneers cannot be placed temporarily
Jun 22, 2021
Your dentist is right, they CAN’T be placed temporarily. While some contouring could be done, any replacement might not yield a significant difference. In MY office, if someone approved them as they were tried in, even if I planned to replace them I would have you wait 6 months. Often, it just takes time to love your new smile.
How can I straighten my teeth without braces and preferably not Invisalign?
Straight teeth requires orthodontics
May 24, 2021
The only way to move teeth (make them straight) is braces or invisalign (or similar aligner method). The only other way to have teeth that APPEAR straight would be porcelain veneers, which can be done very quickly (usually 2 visits, a couple weeks apart).
Will MSE help with widening the face and making cheekbones more noticeable?
Changes to teeth rarely affect cheeks
Apr 14, 2021
The short answer is no. Expanding arches affect cheeks so minimally that it is best to consider any change as “no change”. Fillers or surgery is the only way to affect cheeks.
Is this decay or staining on my teeth?
Stain CAN look like decay
Mar 23, 2021
The only way to know is to get your teeth professionally cleaned. Often, the tartar (calculus) buildup can be stained from the diet or smoking, so CAN be removed.
How can I remove a stain from a filling?
Not all discolorations are stains
Mar 2, 2021
Some composite materials simply change color over time, some are more color stable. The photo does not appear to be a stain, but rather a filling that has changed color (assuming it matched when it was first placed). The only solution is replacing the filling.
Botox Injection sites. What anti-wrinkle benefit does injecting below the hairline have?
Botox injection sites based on muscles
Jan 13, 2021
The best way to understand where Botox is injected is to look at a text of the muscles of the face. The reason no injection in the middle of the forehead is done is because no muscle is there, just connective tissues. If you are still experiencing movement, then the dose per location may be insufficient or the Botox was diluted too much (or both). Another visit for some fine tuning might be a good idea.
Would braces be medically necessary for me? How much would it cost?

Jan 13, 2021
Don’t get me wrong, there are many benefits to having straight teeth, but I think you will be hard pressed to make a compelling argument that they are medically necessary. It is like the internet meme, “But did you die?”

It is likely that you simply need to finance your treatment

Can porcelain veneers fix an crooked open bite to make it appear straightened?
Porcelain veneers are often called “instant orthodontics”
Dec 8, 2020
The short answer is yes, you CAN fix this with porcelain veneers/crowns. I would consider orthodontics first, as the amount of tooth reduction needed might be aggressive or require root canal therapy, where simply waiting a month or two for minor alignment might be better. Without moving the teeth, the resulting veneers will look HUGE.

Start with a diagnostic waxup first, see what you should expect. You are likely looking at a minimum of 10 teeth treated, but you might need more.

Other than an implant, what can be done to fix my missing front tooth?
Front tooth gum recession won’t affect sinus
Dec 2, 2020
The most esthetic option would NOT be any form of Maryland bridge, but a more traditional bridge. There will always be a chance for recession, which can be managed better with a traditional bridge with an ovate pontic. Periodontal grafting to plump up the soft tissues might help.

Don’t worry about affecting the sinus, that space is only in the molar region.

What alternative treatments will fix my crowded teeth besides braces or invisalign?
Braces is the best option for crowded teeth
Dec 1, 2020
One year is PLENTY of time to get straight teeth. 6 Month Smiles focuses on the front teeth, so you could be done with time to spare. All other options would likely require removal of teeth or so much reduction in tooth structure that root canal therapy is likely, along with a lifetime of issues to maintain.
