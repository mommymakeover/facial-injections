Avoiding side effects of botox.

Here are some pointers for avoiding side effects of botox:

  2. Step wise / less is more approach to the procedure. You can always add a touch more but you cannot undo the results! (of course until it wears off). This will ensure natural looking results / and reduce the risk of an ‘over botoxed’ appearance
  3. Follow the aftercare given by your practitioner who should provide you with suitable information such as avoiding excess exercise for 48 hours, not rubbing the area or using make-up directly after injections…(Rupert Critchley, MD, MRCGP, London Physician)
