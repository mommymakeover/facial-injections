What is Botox and Dysport?

These are different brand names of the same drug – neurotoxin type A, which produces the microorganism Clostridium botulinum.

Botox is produced by the American company Allergan, Dysport is the French firm Beafour-Ipsen-Speywood.

Before use, the neurotoxin of botulism undergoes a special purification and binds to albumins. Such a connection makes the toxin more stable, preserves its biological activity, provides a local action of the drug.

What problems can be solved with the help of BOTOX injections?

The skin on the face is directly connected to the underlying muscles. Therefore, when facial expressions are formed wrinkles on the forehead, nose, around the eyes.

An additional mechanism for the formation of wrinkles is genetically programmed and enhanced by external factors, the aging process of the skin.

If the facial muscles are blocked for a long time, the skin over them restores elasticity, wrinkles are smoothed.

An excellent effect is observed when combining injections of Botox or Dysport with cosmetology programs.

It should be noted that the neurotoxin helps to get rid of facial wrinkles, but is not able to eliminate excess skin. Therefore, in 60-year-old patients and older persons, it is necessary to begin rejuvenation with surgical lifting, removing excess skin without affecting the facial muscles.

Mechanism of action

Neurotoxin type A blocks the transfer of motor impulse from the nerve to the muscle fiber. After the administration of the drug, there is a pronounced relaxation of the facial muscles.

Atrophy of muscles is not observed, since their blood supply remains the same. On average, the maximum therapeutic effect is shown on day 14-15. There are observations of the very rapid action of Botox or Disport – already on 2-3 days, and deferred for 3-4 weeks.

Neurotoxin blocks the transfer of momentum not only to the muscles, but also to the sweat glands and, thus, reduces sweating. The drugs are successfully used to treat increased sweating on the palms, in the armpits and on the feet.

The duration of the action is 6-9 months, sometimes up to 1 year. Musculature mobility is partially restored after 3-4 months, complete recovery is observed after 5-8 months. After repeated injections, the duration of the effect reaches 6-12 months.

To achieve a persistent and long-lasting effect, it is recommended that the drugs be administered 2-3 times a year. With local administration in therapeutic doses, botulinum toxin type A does not cause systemic (for the whole organism) actions, the duration of treatment is unlimited.

How is the procedure performed?

Any procedure begins with a consultation. You discuss with the doctor the purposes of injections, the expected results, possible contraindications and complications, after which you sign the Informational consent to the procedure.

Pre-anesthesia is performed only in the treatment of excessive sweating: an anesthetic cream “Emla” (contains lidocaine) is applied on the palms, axillae or feet.

Correction of mimic wrinkles anesthesia, as a rule, does not require. After the disinfection of the skin, subcutaneously or intramuscularly (depending on the zone of administration), the drug is injected with a thin short needle. On average, the procedure lasts 3-5 minutes. After injections, ice is put on the skin for 10-15 minutes.

Comparison of Botox and Dysport

The difference between the two drugs is only in terms of storage and the number of active units in the vial. A comparative study of tolerability, efficacy, duration of action, and the presence of adverse reactions showed that there are no differences between the two drugs in these indices.

Combination with other methods of wrinkle correction. Injections of Botox and Disport can be successfully combined with injections of “Restylane” and “Perlayna”. But between these procedures there should be a break – first make injections of Botox or Disport, and after 2 weeks – inject gels.

Chemical peelings should be carried out 2-3 weeks after the administration of botulinum toxin. If the peeling is already done, then you need to wait for the full restoration of the skin.

Photorejuvenation is combined with injections, but it must be remembered that this procedure improves skin drainage and somewhat accelerates the excretion of the neurotoxin.
