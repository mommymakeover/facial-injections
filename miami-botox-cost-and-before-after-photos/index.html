Botox Contraindications

The botulinum toxin is contraindicated:

  • during the administration of antibiotics-aminoglycosides
  • if there is a purulent-inflammatory process in the area of the proposed injection
  • in the period of any acute illness, do not inject botox with a patient with hemophilia and muscle weakness (myasthenia)
  • Do not inject Botox during pregnancy, breast-feeding and passing the menstrual cycle.
