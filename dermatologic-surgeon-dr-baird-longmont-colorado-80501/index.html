Statement: Dr. Kristin M. Baird is a board-certified dermatologist specializing in cosmetic, medical, and surgical dermatology. She is the founder and medical director of the Dermatology of the Rockies, with offices in Estes Park and Longmont, Colorado.

Dr. Baird and her team have been serving the Front Range of Colorado with comprehensive dermatology services for over 20 years. Dr Baird offers a full range of nonsurgical treatments which can address a wide range of skin concerns. She helps you reach your aesthetic goals without the need for invasive surgery. Dr. Baird’s specialties include injectables such as neuromodulators, Botox and Dysport, dermal fillers like Juvederm, Restylane, and RHA and volumizer products such as Sculptra. In addition, Dr Baird is known for her numerous lasers that can be used to treat various skin conditions, Fraxel, Excel V, Genesis and IPL.

Dermatology of the Rockies is continually upgrading their Body Contouring Center and has been the first in Colorado to offer: EmsculptNeo, Emface, Emsella, Resonic, Sofwave and more.

Dr. Baird received her medical degree from Virginia Commonwealth University School of Medicine (formerly Medical College of Virginia) in Richmond, Virginia. She completed an internship in internal medicine and a residency in dermatology also at Virginia Commonwealth University Medical Center before returning to her home state of Colorado to continue her dermatology career.

Dr. Baird is board certified by the American Board of Dermatology. She is a fellow of the American Academy of Dermatology and a member of several professional societies: the American Society of Dermatologic Surgery, the American Medical Association, the Colorado Dermatologic Society, and the Women’s Dermatologic Society. In addition to private practice, Dr. Baird has served on the faculty at the University of Colorado’s Department of Dermatology, where she trained dermatology residents.

In her spare time Dr. Baird enjoys traveling, numerous outdoor activities, and spending time with family, friends, and her dog.
