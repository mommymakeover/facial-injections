Botox For Forehead Wrinkles Before And After

It’s not unusual for patients to request treatment of horizontal forehead wrinkles. These wrinkles are caused by contraction of the frontalis muscle which is an elevator of the brow.

When botox is strategically injected in the forehead, relaxation of this muscle occurs. This results in significant improvement in forehead wrinkles. This treatment is associated with dramatic clinical improvement and high levels of patient satisfaction.

If you’re concerned about transverse forehead wrinkles botox is an excellent treatment option. (Richard J. Bruneteau, MD, Omaha Plastic Surgeon)
