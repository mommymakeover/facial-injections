What Age To Get Botox In The Forehead?

No matter what your age, Botox is a great way to reduce muscle function that causes facial wrinkles.

If the muscles continue and increase, the wrinkles produced by those muscles become etched in the skin and permanent, thus visible all the time, not just when the muscle is active.

By blocking the muscle function, you also block production of the wrinkles.

Another thing that helps is regular use of tretinoin (Retin-A) that stimulates collagen production, making the support layer of the skin thicker where it can resist formation of permanent wrinkles. (Robert T. Buchanan, MD, Highlands Plastic Surgeon)

It is difficult to define any age for getting BOTOX injections. If you have or you feel you are developing forehead lines then small units of BOTOX can definitely be used to refine you’re your forehead and to get rid of those lines.
