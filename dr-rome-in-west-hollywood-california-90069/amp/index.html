PRP after transplant: Is it possible to damage transplanted follicles with PRP injections?
PRP injections
Jun 10, 2019
There is absolutely no way to damage you hair with PRP injections this far after hair transplant. Here, we actually treat the are worked on same day as surgery to help with healing, as well as to give the new grafts a boost. If you feel you’re loosing more hair, I recommend you go back to your performing surgeon and talk more about this.
Would getting a FUE hair transplant after 6 weeks from a minor tip rhinoplasty revision be an issue at
Rhinoplasty
Jun 10, 2019
There shouldn’t be an issue getting a hair transplant 6 weeks after Your Rhinoplasty. The position you’re in lying down doesn’t cause your nose to be touched at all. When you have a consult with a Hair Surgeon, just make it aware to him so him and his team can take the necessary precautions to make sure there is no issues.
Would FUE be able to treat the folliculitis scarring I have on the nape of my neck?
Nape of neck
Jun 10, 2019
In order to tell if a FUE surgery would be a good game plan for you, I would recommend you meet with a qualified Hair Transplant Surgeon to determine the type of scarring you have, just to see if you need a treatment plan if it is folliculitis. Once that is determined, you will be able to make the best decisions for you.

Why do some doctors recommend not eating spicy food after an FUE transplant?
Spicy Food.
May 17, 2019
The day of procedure is the most important. Spicy foods or alcohol can cause the blood to thin and the blood pressure to rise which could flush the grafts out, plus you have a greater risk of getting swelling in the hairline area, or around the eyes IF the hairline is the priority.
Would a thin cap 2 months after 3300 FUE be enough to block the UV rays?
Sun Rays
May 14, 2019
I would recommend you use a cap that is completely sealed. You can also use a sun screen as well. Two month out you really don’t have to worry about the sun affecting graft growth, but in general you don’t want to get sun burned in general.
Should I expect any further regrowth after FUE hair transplant?
5 months out
May 14, 2019
Growth usually starts between 3-4 months post surgery. You still have up to 6-7 months left to obtain full results. I highly recommend patients schedule a post op appointment with the performing surgeon every 3 months to compare pictures. That’s when you notice each stage of growth up to a year out.
