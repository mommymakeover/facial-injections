I would like to remove my bulbous tip, what type of rhinoplasty surgery should I consider?
rhinoplasty on tip
Jun 1, 2020
It is often best to address the whole nose at the same time, however once could solely focus on tip changes. A bolbous tip can impose limitations on the degree of definition and improvements one can expect from a rhinoplasty but still significant improvement of those factors can be accomplished in the right hands.
Can I get a rhinoplasty one year after a non-surgical rhinoplasty with Radiesse filler?
filler before rhinoplasty
Jun 1, 2020
It generally prefer to dissolve the filler before rhinoplasty if possible, but in cases like this that some residual filler might still be there the surgeon can still visualize the filler and remove it during the surgery (or at least for the most part).
During a septorhinoplasty is there a chance where someone can develop empty nose syndrome?
Empty nose syndrome
Jun 1, 2020
Empty nose syndrome is due to aggressive turbinate reduction. This can be done at the same time as any type of nasal surgery. There are many ways to reduce the size of the inferior turbinates and more conservative techniques can minimize the chance of such a potential problem in future.
Best filler when you have an autoimmune disease and take immune suppression medicine?
Safest filler
May 12, 2020
In general those fillers that are only composed of hyaluronic acid are considered safer as they can be completely dissolved in case of any issues.
I have some broken capillaries on my face. Can I get fillers? Are they safe for me?
Filler safety on the face
May 12, 2020
There is always some risk with intravascular injection of fillers if they are not done with proper technique but you should not be particularly at higher risk than an average person.
Is it possible to inject the area under the apples like the lateral temporal cheeks? If so what filler
Cheek filler
May 4, 2020
It is very difficult to recommend filler options without having photos. Cheek is one of the most common areas to be treated with fillers and both augmentation and also improvement of asymmetries can be achieved with proper filler technique.
Can my nose be lowered somehow so it’s not pointing so high up?
Revision for a turned up tip
May 4, 2020
Yes of course it can be revised. A turned up tip is one of the common reasons a lot of primary rhinoplasty patients are not happy and with proper cartilage grafting technique (as you can see some examples of which in our photo gallery) the rotation and projection of your tip can be adjusted in a more optimal position while addressing asymmetries of your septum and nostrils.
Can I reduce Nostril size in my profile view with alar reduction?
Alar reduction
May 4, 2020
Unfortunately alar reduction cannot or should not be evaluated like this and particularly on a profile view. That is a maneuver that often gets assessed with a full rhinoplasty assessment, Even though it can be done as an isolated maneuver, that will be usually following a rhinoplasty that has already addressed other more fundamental tip and alar issues.
If I place a filler on top of my chin implant will it develop a biofilm or an infection?
Chin filler after implant
Apr 29, 2020
 That is a very good question. Fillers are considered biological implants so even a superficial filler can technically increase the risk of infection if it is close enough to the original implant. The risk is overall low however it’s not zero.
Alar notch after graft, is there more that can be done?
Alar notching
Apr 29, 2020
This is a complicated issue. There are multiple options to address and notched ala. Another composite graft can be attempted but also an alar rim graft or alar repositioning technique might be appropriate options too depending on the individual factors and exam findings.
Can I have a rhinoplasty after I had Botox for bunny lines?
rhinoplasty after filler
Apr 29, 2020
Rhinoplasty can always be done after btox treatment of the bunny lines. As far as fillers, we prefer to dissolve the filler first and space the surgery at least 1-2 weeks after for optimal delineation of the nasal tissue and profile.
