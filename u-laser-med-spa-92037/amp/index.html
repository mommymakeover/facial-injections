Firmness in treated area after CoolSculpting? I had CoolSculpting done on my abdomen 2 weeks ago.
Firm Swelling
May 23, 2018
It is not abnormal to experience some hard swelling after a
Coolsculpting treatment. It can take a few months to see the final result after
a Coolsculpting treatment. The swelling should subside in the next couple weeks
and your results should be visible within the next couple of months. If you are
still feeling concerned about your result, I recommend reaching out to your
provider. Best of luck!
