Does Opus Plasma work on dark skin types?
Yes!
May 25, 2021
Hi, and thanks for your question. Yes, the Opus is great for all skin types. With a Fitz V it would be a good idea to pretreat for a week or so with a prep pad–we use a kojic acid brightening pad to lower the chances of PIH. However, because the Opus delivers less retained heat than a fractional CO2, it tends to be a safer choice for the higher fitzpatrick skin colors.

I hope you find this helpful. Please reach out if you have additional questions.

What does a session of Opus Plasma cost?
Depends on Number of Treatments
May 25, 2021
Hi! The average cost for an Opus Treatment will depend on the area you live in. However, because it provides such dramatic skin tightening, expect to pay about $3500 for a package of three treatments, and about $1500- $2000 for a single treatment. The number of treatments you need will depend on your skin condition and your age, as well as the type of results you are seeking.

Please reach out if you have additional questions! I’m happy to help.
