How long after a thread lift can I resume facials and RF without harming results?
Thread Lifting and other procedures
Jul 8, 2023
PDO threads for thread lifting are placed into the facial fat layer. This is well below the dermis which is where most RF has its effect. We tend to avoid "bulk heating" procedures over thread lifting for 3 or more months. If you are referring to RF microneedling- the heat from that would not disrupt the threads as it is occurring at a much more superficial level than where the threads are placed.
Are there lasers that are safe to use close to the lash line?
Lasers Close to the Lash Line
Jul 8, 2023
The laser treatment that can easily treat the skin right up to the lash line is CO2 resurfacing, such as the SmartSkin CO2 laser. If treating the full eyelid a corneal shield is often placed for extra protection, but if it is the lower lid primarily we just pull the skin down so that we are treating it without any energy directed near the orbit. Expect to do 2 or 3 sessions but the collagen made will last for years. Downtime will be for a handful of days.

Could Smart lipo on abdomen complicate future abdominal surgery?
SmartLipo and Subsequent surgery
Jul 8, 2023
Smart Lipo is a laser assisted liposuction and skin tightening procedure. It works in the fat layer underneath the skin, but above the muscular abdominal wall so it does not cause any internal scar tissue. I wouldn't expect it to cause any degree of scar tissue formation that would affect a laparoscopic procedure.
