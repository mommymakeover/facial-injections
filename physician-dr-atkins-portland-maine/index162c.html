Is it safe to do all 4 CoolSculpting sessions in one day?
Coolsculpting
Mar 25, 2021
Yes, it is completely safe to do all four Coolsculpting treatments (or more) in one day. The majority of our patients opt to treat several different areas and have multiple treatments in the same day at our office. Please note that one treatment will only tackle one area on the body but you can absolutely treat several different areas in one session/appointment. We do recommend waiting at least 8 weeks in between Coolscuplting sessions on the same treatment area.

The more areas you treat in one visit, the happier you will be.
Jul 13, 2017
The more areas you treat in one visit, the happier you will be. Just be certain that you are prepared to treat as much the second time around.
