Suzanne Kilmer, MD

Name: Suzanne Kilmer, MD
Statement: Dr. Suzanne Kilmer, MD is a board-certified dermatologist with over 25 years of experience and the founder of Laser & Skin Surgery Center of Northern California in Sacramento. She specializes in aesthetic treatments, including laser skin resurfacing, skin tightening treatments, and body contouring. She is also known for her skill in dermatologic surgery and creating artistic results. “Natural appearing results are extremely technique-dependent and best achieved by combining state-of-the-art technology, a skilled hand, attention to detail, and an aesthetic eye,” explains Dr. Kilmer.

After receiving her MD from and completing her residency at UC Davis Medical Center, Dr. Kilmer pursued a fellowship in laser surgery at Harvard Medical School’s Wellman Center for Photomedicine at Massachusetts General Hospital. There, she stayed on as a faculty member for several years before returning to Sacramento to open her medical practice. She has won a plethora of awards throughout her career, including the Vic Narurkar, MD, Innovations in Aesthetic Dermatology Lectureship in 2020. She is also a member of multiple professional organizations, such as the American Society of Dermatologic Surgery. When not working, she enjoys skiing and surfing with her husband and three children.

How many treatments of Emsculpt for abdomen? How long between treatments?
How many treatment are needed for Emsculpt to the abdomen
Jun 21, 2018
Four Emsculpt treatments in about 2 weeks and at least 2 days apart is recommended for the abdomen. Then maintenance treatments about every 2-3 months although going longer or shorter (because some people love it so much!) is ok. More studies are being done for the best timing on maintenance treatments.

You may lose some hair but the
