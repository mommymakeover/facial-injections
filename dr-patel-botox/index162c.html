How to fix mouth corner asymmetry?
Treating downturned corners of mouth with filler
Jun 2, 2020
A small amount of filler placed by an experienced injector will provide some support to the lower corner of mouth. Botox or Dysport injection into the DAO muscle may help but I usually recommend starting with the filler first.

Treating enlarged masseters with Botox/Dysport
Jun 2, 2020
It’s hard to tell based on these photos alone if your masseters are enlarged. I recommend a in person consultation with a local aesthetic physician. If the masseters are enlarged, commonly seen in patients who grind or clench their teeth, then you may benefit from Botox/Dysport treatment to relax the masseters and reduce muscle bulk. 
What do you suggest to make my face more feminine and attractive?
Enhancing face shape using fillers
Jun 2, 2020
I’m not sure why you think your face is not “feminine” but I think your face shape is beautiful. Sometimes we are our own worst critics. That being said, I think you could consider some tweaks but would enhance your aesthetic. I would look into cheek, lip and laugh line filler as well a Botox or Dysport treatment to soften frown line and give the eyebrows more of an arch. I hope this helps.
Fine lines on my forehead. How to fix?
Treating forehead lines using Botox/Dysport
Jun 1, 2020
Neuromodulators like Botox and Dysport are most commonly used to treat horizontal forehead lines. Based on your photo, I think you would be a good candidate for neuromodulator treatment.
