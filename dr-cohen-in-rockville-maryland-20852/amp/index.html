Thank you for your photos. You are still very early out from surgery. An important point to understand about nasal surgery is that the final outcome of the operation is not seen for six months to one year. The reason for this is that swelling persists for a very long time in the area of the nose. The last area in which the effects of nasal surgery can be seen is at the tip.
