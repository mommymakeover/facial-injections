If I have Botox resistance will I still see results from dermal filler?
Botox Resistance and Fillers
Aug 17, 2023
You could try another brand of botulinum toxin such as Xeomin which may have a lower risk of resistance. Xeomin is ultra-purified to remove the accessory proteins which are bound to the toxin. These proteins are not necessary for therapeutic efficacy but increase the chance of developing antibodies to the product. Resistance to Botox has no bearing on your response to fillers.
Best,
Dr. Ort

What can be done for sunken hollow dark tear troughs with thin loose wrinkly skin?
Sunken hollow dark tear troughs
May 24, 2023

This is an advanced area and it is important to see a very experienced injector. I prefer to use a blunt cannula rather than a needle under the eyes. Prominent under eye veins - what treatment will get me effective results?
Blue veins under eyes
May 24, 2023
If the vein is outside the orbital rim, it is likely treatable with long-pulsed Nd:YAG laser. This is typically non-invasive with minimal recovery. I have successfully treated blue veins under the eyes with laser. However, I do not treat if the vein is inside the orbital rim. It requires an in person evaluation to palpate the underlying bony rim to determine the exact location of the vein in relation to the structure underneath.
Sincerely,
Dr. Ort
