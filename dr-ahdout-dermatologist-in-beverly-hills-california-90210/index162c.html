Spironolactone dosing
Mar 29, 2017
The half life of this medication is 16 hours. For this reason, I prefer prescribing as twice daily. If the patient feels they won't remember taking the medication a second time during the day, then I advise them to take the full dose at once.

Melasma Treatment
Mar 29, 2017
I like to use a combination of topicals Clear and Brilliant Laser. This is a light version of the fraxel laser which is geared towards pigmentation. It is safe and effective in combination with an appropriate skin care regimen.
Is Juvederm Voluma an Alternative to Radiesse?
Voluma vs Radiesse for Cheek Enhancement
Mar 29, 2017
I certainly prefer Voluma to Radiesse for the cheeks for 3 reasons:
1. Safety
2. Reversibility
3. Increased lifting capacity
4. Increased longevity: 2 years with voluma vs. 18 months with Radiesse
