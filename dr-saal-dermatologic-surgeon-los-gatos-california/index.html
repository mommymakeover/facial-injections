Can You Exercise After Botox or Will It Decrease the Results?
Not Advisable to Exercise After Botox
Apr 11, 2017
Dear cory in chi town,
Since the Botox needs to be absorbed into the muscle after the injection, and since this takes about 3-4 hours, it's advisable not to spend a lot of time bending over with you head down, or exercising after your Botox injections, as this may cause the Botox to shift to the wrong area and create undesirable muscle weakness. Hope this helped!
Dr. S
