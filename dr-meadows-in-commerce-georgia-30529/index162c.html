How much does it cost for belly fat removal?
Waffle House or Starbucks
Apr 23, 2021
Cheaper is not always better. Although price is shopped most commonly for belly Lipo and breast augmentation it’s not worth it if you wind up with a life long complication. What is Laser Liposuction versus other techniques?
Slightly better results
Apr 21, 2021
As a surgeon who has used most of the adjunct technologies to traditional Lipo(power assisted, VASER, Smart Lipo, Cool Lipo) laser liposuction provides minimal skin tightening and not much else. Experience and good technique gets smooth results and bruising is minimal with adequate tumescence. Currently I use either VASER or power assisted Lipo simply because it lets me get more fat out and more impressive results.
How Many Laser Fat Removal Treatment Sessions Are Generally Required to See Results?
External or Internal
Apr 16, 2021
If you are talking about one of the external(non invasive) treatments plan and budget for at least two treatments before you see appreciable results. Fo laser liposuction done by an experienced surgeon you will be smaller immediately after surgery and your results will continue to improve for up to 13 weeks assuming you make any effort at maintaining a healthy diet and exercise regimen.
Laser Liposuction Side Effects
Unfortunately ,Yes

Liposuction in general is like walking around your house in the dark. As long as you know where the furniture is you can do it without a problem. How Much Fat Can Be Removed During Laser Liposuction?

Laser lipo, traditional, vaser, or power assisted a smart surgeon will limit to 5000cc’s. If that volume can’t be adhered to then split the Lipo into two sessions. Everyone likes just one recovery but it’s not worth the risks which include hospitalization or worse.

Can you do laser hair removal and laser lipo in the same area at the same time?
No problem
Feb 16, 2021
These procedures can be done around the same time or specifically hair removal can be done during your recovery from Lipo. I would not recommend doing them the same day. There are numerous cases where insults to the skin from multiple energy modalities have resulted in scarring.
