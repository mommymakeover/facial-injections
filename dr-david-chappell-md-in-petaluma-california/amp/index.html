Why are thyroid/hormone conditions listed as contraindications for this treatment?
Thyroid problems or no contraindication if they are under good control
May 29, 2020
Thyroid problems or no contraindication if they are under good control. As long as your thyroid function is under good control, whether you started out with an under active thyroid or an overactive thyroid, if your blood levels and symptoms are normal there is no contraindication for treatment. I am an endocrinologist. This is what I’ve been doing for the past 30 years.

I had my gall bladder removed in 2016. Is it safe to have SculpSure over the scars?
Sculpture over scars
Nov 21, 2019
I would avoid doing SculpSure over a large scar with thin skin especially if there’s a temple. However a small flat scar with surrounding fat should be an acceptable site. Please keep in mind that less than 50% of patients report a satisfactory response toSculpSure treatments.
If I have RA, can I have WarmSculpting?
SculpSure and RA
Nov 21, 2019
You can have sculpsure if you have RA as long as you don’t not have an active flare and or a skin lesion in the treatment site. Keep in mind that less than 50% of any patients have a satisfactory response to treatment.
Still swollen two weeks post SculpSure. Should I be concerned?
Should I be concerned
Oct 24, 2019
I don’t think you should be concerned at this point 2 wks after treatment because SculpSure treatments are not that effective under any circumstances. I certainly have not seen a major effect in two weeks apart from possibly inducing some tenderness. If you are concerned you should see your local provider. Therefore a bigger concern would be that you don’t respond at all. SculpSure does not help with skin laxity.
