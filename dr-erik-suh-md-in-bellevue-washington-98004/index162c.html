Can I get Botox/Dysport during my period/menstrual cycle?
depends on your menstrual cycle
Sep 17, 2019
Without knowing more about your cycles, it's hard to say. It's not a contraindication. But, we do tell our patients that during the cycle, your pain receptors are on high alert, and botox injections do hurt more during your cycle. Also if you have any issues with temp regulation, like you tend to sweat more or have night sweats, you might sweat out the botox which lessen the duration or how long your botox will last.
