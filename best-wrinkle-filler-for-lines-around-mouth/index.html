Best Wrinkle Filler For Lines Around Mouth

There are many options to help lessen the severity of the lip lines. 2 things that come to mind to me for skin tightening is Ultherapy or Infini.

Ultherapy is the only device FDA approved for skin tightening with no down time.

Infini is a new device using micro-needles and radio frequency heat to deliver energy deep in the tissue, essentially creating a heat injury that your body’s natural response is to build collagen and repair.

Both devices are good for stimulating collagen and can be used on the face and neck. This type of treatment generally lasts about 2 years. (Mark A. Blair, MD, Phoenix Dermatologic Surgeon)

Lip lines – best treatment

Start with Botox – only a little is needed and only in the upper lip, a total of 6 units or so. Also start skin care creams – especially tretinoin (Retin A).

Upper Lip Wrinkles & Lines

Upper lip creases

I recommend Juvederm Ultra to fill in the deep creases as well as restore volume in the depressions near the outer limit of the upper lip.

Restylane will also do the job but I prefer Juvederm Ultra for the kind of deep creases you have along with the generalized thinning of the upper lip which is visible as shadows on the lip.

It is likely that you may need two injection appointments to achieve the best results. Botox in very small doses can help the filler last a little longer, but does not restore volume in lips that have lost volume in the skin.

Along with fillers, it is important to use sun protection including a zinc oxide- containing broad-spectrum sunscreen. (Avoid the company of smokers to avoid secondhand smoke, and also the temptation to smoke in the company of smokers.). (Renuka Diwan, MD, Cleveland Dermatologic Surgeon)

Wrinkles Appearing In Different Areas Of Your Face

Smoker’s line treatment

These are difficult to treat. It always requires combination therapy. These require volume with filler, Botox to relax the muscles, and either fractional ablative laser resurfacing or Infini. Other treatments which have prolonged downtime and hypopigmentation risks are: Dermabrasion and completely ablative laser resurfacing, both of which I don’t recommend. (Steven F. Weiner, MD, Panama City Facial Plastic Surgeon)

Treatment of vertical lip lines (smoker’s lines, lipstick lines)

It is one of the best things you can do for your skin along with reducing sun exposure. There are several treatment options for vertical lip lines including fillers, Botox, microneedling, non-ablative and ablative lasers.

I usually start with a combination of a filler such as Belotero or Restylane Silk and Botox. Microneedling can be combined with the injectable treatments and does induce collagen production helping to soften the lines further.
