Can you still get silicone if you have symmetry issues?
Can you still get silicone if you have symmetry issues?
Jan 10, 2017
The short answer is absolutely. The longer answer is that regardless of what type of implant is used, perfect symmetry is almost never possible. The goal is to try and improve the asymmetry, and to try and get the breasts as symmetrical as possible. With this in mind, silicone gel filled breast implants are used all of the time to treat breast asymmetry. Hope that is helpful –

What are the benefits of either over or under the muscle?
Over the muscle risks
Oct 3, 2016
Placing a breast implant over the muscle has a much higher rate of capsular contracture, there is less soft tissue on top of the implant, increasing the chance of feeling the implant through your skin (particularly with larger implants as you are considering), and more interference with mammograms. In general, I do not think there is much benefit from placing implants on top of the muscle. Hope that is helpful –
