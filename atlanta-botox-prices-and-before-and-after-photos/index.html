What is Botox?

On the one hand, Botox in Atlanta is a very popular term, but, on the other hand, very few people know what it is. So, what is Botox and why is it called a poison for good?

Botox, or botulinum toxin preparations, is a type A neurotoxin and some other proteins that are produced by the vital activity of Clostridium botulinum bacteria.

Preparations based on botulinum toxin are a weakened botulinum toxin.

The bacterium of botulinum toxin causes a terrible disease called botulism. The toxins released by botulinum, when ingested, can block the work of the muscles, causing their paralysis.

Botulin is able to suppress the impulses coming from the nerve to the facial muscle, which contributes to the formation of wrinkles. It should be borne in mind that Botox alone does not eliminate wrinkles, it only relaxes the muscles for a while, not allowing them to shrink.

It is known that wrinkles, created by facial expressions, can give a human face a wide range of emotions. A person can not but show emotions, they are components of our life, but, nevertheless, it is emotions along with ultraviolet and age that is one of the reasons for the appearance of wrinkles on the face.

And it turns out a paradox: rejoicing or admiring, a man adds himself another portion of wrinkles on his face. How to deal with this dilemma? Botox will help to solve this problem.

After carrying out of procedure on the basis of botox, the skin becomes equal, smooth, wrinkles are smoothed out on eyes. As for untreated facial muscles, they contract in a normal rhythm, without affecting the habitual facial expression.

How does the botulinum therapy work? Using a thin needle, the drug is injected into the areas where dynamic (facial) wrinkles are localized. Most often it is the forehead area and the area around the eyes. Botulotoxin is used mainly in the upper part of the face.

Contour plastic based on hyaluronic acid is used to fill wrinkles in the area of nasolabial folds.

For this reason, pain during injection is minimized, and the injections themselves leave no traces on the skin.

In many developed countries, botox is called “lunch” injections. That is, their performance is so easy and safe that it takes place at lunchtime, after which patients return to normal life schedule.

The effect of botulinum toxin persists for six months, and the neurotoxin itself at the end of this time is completely eliminated from the body. On average, botulinum is administered three times a year. It is noteworthy that after treatment with Botox in Atlanta, the patient gets rid of the habit of actively using facial expressions, which has a number of positive actions. Muscles of the face get used to being relaxed, which helps to smooth out wrinkles and prevents the formation of new ones.

Advantages and disadvantages of Botox

There are no ideal things in our world. All procedures and preparations have their own small deficiencies.

Let’s once again clarify the advantages and disadvantages of Botox.

Botox has a number of advantages:

  • fast effect;
  • absolute safety of treatment;
  • ease in the execution of the procedure.

Botox injections could be a universal tool in treating age-related skin defects, if not for the following disadvantages:

  • botox smoothes only mimic wrinkles, with dehydration of the skin and modeling of the contour of the face, it can not cope;
  • a temporary effect, unfortunately, the procedure of botulinum therapy should be carried out regularly for a certain time.

As it was possible to notice, at injections on the basis of botox has its own shortcomings and its advantages. For this reason, the patient decides to resort to botox treatment, not forgetting the recommendations of the cosmetologist.
