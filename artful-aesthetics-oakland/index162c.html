Nasolabial fold darkness, what do you recommend to even out skin tone?
Treatment of Hyperpigmentation
Apr 6, 2018
It looks like you have some post inflammatory hyperpigmentation (skin’s reaction to injury) after your injections, which is more common in darker skin types. Sun screen is a must. Using a skin brightening product such as Lytera, or Discoloration Defense should help.
