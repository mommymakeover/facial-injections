Can I get a facelift if I take warfarin? Other options for sagging skin?
Facelift
Oct 21, 2020
Great question. One of the most common complications of a facelift is a hematoma or bleeding and with warfarin or coumadin this is substantially higher. I would advise against a facelift at this point. There are many non invasive options that are safer which include Zaffiro. I hope this helps.
I have olive tone skin. Will a facelift leave me visible scars?
Facelift
Oct 21, 2020
Great questions! Many different factors lead to scarring and everyone is different. The best indicator is usually previous scars on your body and the face does tend to heal well. When I perform a facelift I make sure all my scars are well hidden around the ear and hairline. I also start aggressive scar therapy right away which can include laser treatments.
