Can you have lipo and gastric bypass done at the same time?
Gastric Bypass and BBL
Jun 1, 2021
Hi there, I would not recommend having both surgeries at the same time, since after your bypass you will start losing a lot of fat, and this will directly affect your BBL, making you more susceptible to losing all of its benefits in a short time. So I suggest you have a consult with a certified Bariatric Surgeon and wait at least 6-12 months after your Bypass to start doing any plastic surgery procedures. I hope you can find the results you are looking for! You'll do great!- A.L

Bariatric Gastric sleeve surgery: Is it a mandate to remove 80 percent of your stomach?
Size of your stomach
May 13, 2021
Hi there, in standards gastric sleeves we usually use a calibrating bougie when cutting, this can translate to cutting about 70-80% of your stomach (depending on the patient), but of course you can discuss this with your surgeon. If you want to remove only 50% of your stomach, we can discuss it with you in your surgical plan and seek the healthiest approach. I hope you get the results you are expecting. You'll do great! A.L
