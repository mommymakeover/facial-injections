How can I correct this asymmetry?
Depends on which side you like
Jun 21, 2021
Your asymmetry is very common. The solution depends on which side your like. I can expend the right side with fillers, or the left side can be lifted to taper the shape. Will need to see more views to give you more details.
Symmetry enhancement, jawline and chin augmentation?
Yes via the Y lift technique
Jun 21, 2021
I perform the Y lift procedure very often to enhance chin and jaw line. The best material is Voluma and it can last 2-3 years. It is non surgical office procedure that takes 30 min and you can drive after.
What can I do to fix my side profile?
Chin enhancement
Jun 21, 2021
You have good frontal projection of nose and lips, your chin can be extended to match. Easy way is to try filer injection, it is low risk, non-surgical, and reversible. I like using Radiasse or Voluma and can last up to a year.
Can I do high frequency treatments after PDO threading?
Need to wait
Jun 21, 2021
I perform PDO thread lifts over the last 10 years. Even with the new and better threads it is best to wait as least 4-6 weeks before and facial message and high frequency devices. The threads should be locked in after 4-6 weeks.
How can I tighten my jaw line and neck?
At 40 may be early for surgical procedures
Apr 28, 2021
You have volume loss in your cheek area and skin looseness. First to address is volume loss that can be replaced with fillers like Juvederm in the Y lift procedure. Second is non invasive like ultherapy or radio frequency like Vivace. If you want faster results then suture thread lift can be used as well.
Flap of loose skin under my chin. What can I do about it that won't leave a scar on my neck?
You have options
Apr 28, 2021
Surgical removal would be most direct way to remove the loose skin. Non surgical option include laser/J plasma lipo to tighten the skin. Suture threads to pull and tighten the skin. All these options would improve your jaw line definition.
Would Emsculpt, Vanquish or something else be best for me?
renuvion and lipo
Apr 25, 2021
Non invasive options would give you 20-30% improvement and still cost you $3-6,000. Lipo with renuvion would get you to 50-70% with 5 days of down time costing $7-9,000, Tummy tuck would get you 70-90% costing $9-15,000 2-4 weeks of down time. with the right provider it's up to you to choose the best option for you.
Sculptra or Silhouette Instalift for my jowls/lower face?
Both are needed
Apr 25, 2021
Most people will need both volume and lift as we get older. I like to start with volume first if cost is a factor. Sculptra is a option to volumize but it takes multiple visits and may not be cost effective. HA are more predicative less down time and can last up to 2 years. 2 syringes on each cheeks.
Can RF Microneedling cause raised scarring or permanent needle scars since the needles go so deep and hea
Hard to get good results for stretch marks
Apr 25, 2021
Stretch marks are very difficult to get good results. Microneedle with RF in combination of PRP injections offer a good option. The needle can go up to 3mm deep and on high setting will create scabbing but not likely to cause scarring. expect 30-50% improvement with 3-5 treatments.
Will fillers fix my upper lips asymmetry? Will it work on scars?
Should have good improvements
Apr 23, 2021
This is a very difficult problem to correct. There are no good way to get to perfection. HA fillers like Juvederm Vollure can be used to build up the defect. Tattoo can be used to color in the border defect to improve the scar. If the scar is hard on palpation then PRP should be used first to soften the scar.
Can I widen the appearance of my mouth with lip filler?
Maybe not
Apr 23, 2021
I think you want the upper lip corners lifted and not widen your upper lip. Fillers can plump up the corners but might create a ridge or line above it. Another option is to try small amount of Botox on the upper lip to relax the muscles and open the corners which should look like it is wider.
Threads or filler: what is the best option for eliminating frown and marionette lines?
Combo would get the best results
Apr 22, 2021
Volume restoration with filler such as Voluma in the Y lift technique is the first step. Should get you about 50% improvement. Threads can be done at the same time or as second stage after 1 month of the Y lift. I like Mint and instalift threads used in combination, which should get you to 75% on optimal results. This should last 2-3 years or yearly maintenance for as long as you want.
Can nerve damage or any kind of facial paralysis occur during euro threading of the face?
Low risk
Apr 22, 2021
You should look at what kind of threads used. We use low risk threads that uses non sharp cannulas, these required higher skills to place the threads, but reduce the risk of damage. It is also important to understand the anatomy and avoid danger areas.
What nonsurgical procedures will help me get a more defined, contoured and youthful face/look?
Y lift and threads combo would work
Apr 21, 2021
You do need more volume in your cheeks, but if too much is injected your face would be too wide. Using threads in combination would lift and shape your jaw line and stay in proportion.
