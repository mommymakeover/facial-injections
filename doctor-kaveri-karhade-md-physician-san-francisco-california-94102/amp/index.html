Where Can I Legally Buy Juvederm to Inject my Own Skin?
buying fillers/botox
Dec 27, 2019
It is not legal to obtain botox or fillers in the US without a medical license. It can also be dangerous and I highly advise against this practice. While it may be tempting to succumb to lower prices, the likelihood of risks and complications is much higher. In fact, the relatively high prices of botox and filler have to do with the actual cost of the product itself from the manufacturers. Unfortunately no one can get the branded, legal products cheaper.

The price is also dependent upon the experience of the injector. Injectors are not equal- there are providers who have studied and learned cosmetic injectables over years, and others who have learned in a 2 hour crash course- higher prices are therefore typically seen with more experienced injectors.
