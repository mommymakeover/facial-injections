Accutane, then retinoid. Is this a good route?
Is Accutane a good regimen for moderately severe acne.
Aug 9, 2015
Accutane , which is a retinoid is used for more severe cystic acne. It often will put acne into remission when taken for 5 months at a dose of 0.5-1 mg/kg. Low dose Accutane will work while you are taking it , but in the long run , the acne is likely to recur. If you are female another alternative is an oral contreceptive that is approved for acne, sometimes combined with Spironolactone, which blocks female testosterone. This can also be combined with a topical retinoid.
