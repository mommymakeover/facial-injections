Once you get LASIK, are you stuck with having a flap for life? Do PRK touchups get rid of the flap?
LASIK flap / PRK
Dec 6, 2019
Hello there,
LASIK flap do not ever go away, they just heal over. Frequently, they can be re-lifted, even years later, in case you need a touch up (an enhancement). Occasionally, however, an ophthalmologist who is doing an enhancement cannot re-lift the flap. In that case a PRK enhancement can be done. So the flap stays in place and the touch up is done right over it. Hope this answers your question.
Good luck!
From NYC
