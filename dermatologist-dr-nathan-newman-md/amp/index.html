Will one syringe of Juvederm be enough for my lips?
1 Syringe for Lips
Jan 2, 2018
Number of syringes varies from patient to patient but in our office and experience, the majority of patients doing lip injections only need 1 syringe to achieve the results they are looking for. We keep their purchased syringe for them for touch ups up to two weeks after just in case they need a little more! It is best to talk to the doctor or nurse prior to any injections to know what you will need.
