Hi.  Practically everyone's face is asymmetric.  Some more so than others.  In fact about 85% of us have very asymmetric faces.  Sometimes with fillers or botox you can create the illusion of better symmetry. Ultimately its impossible to be 100% symmetric even if you choose to undergo extreme craniofacial surgery (which you should not).
