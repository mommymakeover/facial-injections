How can I avoid Botox side effects?
Choose an Expert Injector
Dec 1, 2020
In truth... choose an experienced injector. Droopy eyelid or eyebrow is due to injecting too close and Botox migrating downward. There is no patient responsibility.
How Many Botox Units for Horizontal Forehead Lines?
Horizontal Forehead Lines
Nov 18, 2020
From 5-15. I tend to not be an aggressive heavy forehead line injector. It is easy to over do it and leave somewhat flat eyebrows which is not aesthetically pleasing until the botox wears off.
What Are the Post Treatment Instructions After Botox?
Enjoy It!
Nov 11, 2020
I don't have any specific post botox instructions. I say thanks for coming in, enjoy it. have fun! In the past, people have had instructions such as don't lie down for 4 hours, Don't excericise, don't go on a flight etc. All of these have been proven to be unnecessary. For instance, botox doesn't migrate if you lie down after injection. Hope that helps.
Can Botox Be Injected Under the Eye?
Botox for Under Eye
Jun 8, 2020
Yes but carefully. Botox, by decreasing the enlarged muscles under the eye, sometimes called the "jelly roll" can open the eye aperture. It is an amazing trick that can give and open eye appearance which looks great along with decreasing the crease under the eyelash margin. Careful as it can worsen swelling under the eye if they pt has lost the muscle elasticity. A simple snap test of the lower eye can tell you if they are a good candidate or not...
Can You Exercise After Botox or Will It Decrease the Results?
Botox and Exercise
Jun 8, 2020
I don't limit patient after botox. We used to have limits (don't lay down for 4 hours, no excercise that day). I now say "no limits"... though i do encourage them NOT to go straight to bikram yoga and stand on their head. Otherwise, no limits. Hope that helps!

Forgot to rinse with water after TCA peel. Will this have a terrible effect?
TCA peels are self neutralizing
Feb 21, 2019
TCA peels once they have denatured the keratin and other proteins and you get "frosting" self neutralizes and reaction stops. Think of it like this, once the protein in egg whites has been heated and turned white, you can't continue to turn it more white. The chemical reaction has occurred. Optimal post chemical peel treatment is under the guidance of a cosmetic dermatologist. Good luck.
