RA medication’s effect on BOTOX®.
Aug 21, 2019
Because the steroids used to treat rheumatoid arthritis and the active components of BOTOX function within the body very differently, there should be no impact on the results of your BOTOX because of your RA medication. I would, however, be sure to double check with your doctor and an experienced, qualified BOTOX injector to let each of them know of your situation. There should be no drug interaction, but it's always important to be completely up front about your medical history.
