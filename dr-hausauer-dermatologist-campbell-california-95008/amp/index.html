What's the most effective treatment for my neck lines?
Necklace lines
Oct 13, 2021
These lines can be tricky as there are caused by flexing the neck down (like looking at screen) and often require a combination of treatments to help restore collagen. My first options is typically a fine line filler like Belotero which initially appears dopey then blends into the skin making a more smooth appearance. Lasers and other options on top can help with more long term restoration but my patients tend to be happiest with this option. Hope this helps as a general guide.

Is it safe to get Botox and then 4 days later the second dose of vaccine?
Vaccination and Botox
Mar 6, 2021
There is no data or reporting that suggests Botox (or any other brand of neuromodulator) and vaccination can't be done close together, within days or even on the same day. This is true for the new COVID vaccines as well. No bad reactions have been reported to the FDA reporting system or were seen in clinical trials. So it is safe to protect yourself against the pandemic and still look refreshed.
