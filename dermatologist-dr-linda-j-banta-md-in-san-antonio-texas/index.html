Is it normal for Botox in the forehead and around the eyes to wear off after two months?
Botox wore off 2 months
May 18, 2019
Botox usually lasts 3 to 4 months. There are variables that can change this time frame. Exercise can increase the metabolism . If the vial has been open too long the Botox may not be as effective . There are also incidences of Botox antibodies that can form and decrease effectiveness . Check that what you are actually getting is Botox and not a shorter acting product . Please discuss your results with your injector .
