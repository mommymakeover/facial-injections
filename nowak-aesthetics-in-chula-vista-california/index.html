Resuming Normal Routines After Botox and Juvederm?
Activity Restriction After Injectables
Nov 16, 2018
Following any injectable procedure, it is best to avoid exercise or any activity that can significantly elevate your blood pressure for at least 24 hours. Other helpful tips to reduce effects of swelling and/or bruising, would be to use cold compresses and sleep with the head of your bed elevated for a night or two after your procedure.
